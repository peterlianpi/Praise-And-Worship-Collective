Title: Topa Tawh Ton Khawm In




Verse 1
Topa tawh ton khawm in, bang bang piang ta leh
Na lampi khuamial hang, ama nung zui in
Na galte’n hong neh in, a lauhuai hang in
Zeisu tawh ton le teh, kihtaak ding om kei

Chorus
Ton khawm in, hang tak mainawt in
Na tha hong khauh sak ding hi
Nisim in amah tawh pai in
Na khualzinna lam hong pi ding hi



Verse 2
Topa tawh ton khawm in, na paina peuh ah
Khuamialna lim sung ah, lam hong hilh ding hi
Ama vang in na lampi hong taang sak ding
Keugaw gam zong lono na hong piang sak ding

Verse 3
Topa tawh ton khawm in, nang thu’n tawlnga ken
Hong thupiakna bang in, na gamta ding hi
Hong sawlna bang mang in, ama tha muang in
Zeisu nung na zuih leh, na khial ngei kei ding

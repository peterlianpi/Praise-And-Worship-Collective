Title: Shekinah Vangliatna




Chorus
Huihnun ki lamdang, muhzia hong ki khel
Kidal kihenna a vekpi’n tolhkhia
Lungdamna tuipi bang’n khang in dimlet ta
Khasiangtho omna ah suahtakna om 

Verse 1
Hih mun ah khasiangtho
Munpia in kong muak hi
Na nasepna lianpi mu ding in kong ngak hi
Shekinah vangliatna’n
Hong luah in hong dim ciang
Launa zumna ten mun ngah nawnlo
Zeisu ki lamto sangpen mun ah 



Verse 2
Ka muh ngei nailoh na lamdang te ka mu ding
Ka theih ngei nailoh
Thu lian tampi ka thei ding
Huhpa khasiangtho tawh
Ki thuah den peuh leng
Zawh lah ding na khat beek zong om kei
Sihna vangliatna ki valhtum ta 

Bridge
A hatlo mite tun thahat zo ta
A damlo mite’n damna ngah zo ta
A suakta lo te tun suakta zo ta
Aw khasiangtho in na hong sep ciang
Dawite kihem khia guallel in tai kek ta
Haksat buaina te tun tuisuak ta
Thuumna tauna te tun phatna’n kikhek
Shekinah vangliatna’n hong tuam ciang

Title: Lungmuanna




Verse 1
Simleitungah lungmuanna om ngei lo
Lungkham dahna in hong tuam zel a
I muan pente in zong I nuntakna
Hong gum zo tuan kei I lungkhamna

Verse 2
Hih leitung ah I neih sum leh paite
I lungmuang kha zel hiam sanggam aw
Nikhat ni nih I nopsakna te in
Mailam ding lungmuanhuai kei sanggam



Chorus
Lungmuanna in Zeisu sung bek hi

Gal hongsuak ding
Lungmuanna taktak Zeisu sung bek

Verse 3

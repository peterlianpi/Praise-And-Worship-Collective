Title: Nang Kong Bel Hi




Verse 1
Lungsim gal dona leh dahna sung panin
Kei sang a sang zaw, Suangpi ka beel nuam hi
Mawh lua, gim lua zo, Nang aa hong hi ta ning
A kip Suangpi aw, Nangmah kong belta hi

Chorus
Nang kong bel hi, Nang kong bel hi
Nangmah kong bel hi
A kip thupha Suangpi



Verse 2
Sunin gam dai-a, dahin lungleng laitak
Ze-etna in a, vaang tawh hong tuam ciangin
Nuntak huihpi nung, tuipi kihual laitak
Thupha Suangpi aw, hong liahin bittak in

Verse 3
Galdo lai takin, gal in hong neh ciangin
Tai in kong ko zel, hi ka belh Suangpi aw
Tuihual bang ze-et, gimna hong tun ciang in
Na sung kong bu hi, keima kha Suangpi aw

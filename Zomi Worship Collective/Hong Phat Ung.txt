Title: Hong Phat Ung




Verse 1
It baanglo, keilel hong it Pasian
Nong hehpihna in kei a ding kicing
Ka mawhna tang ding, singkhuam tung nong si
Kei nong itna sak ciim thei keng

Chorus
Topa na hoih hi, nang na lian hi
Nong itna, kip in kho tawntung hi
Khutte laam to in, nang hong liansak ung
Hong phat ung, nang na hoih hi



Verse 2
Lei leh vanpi, a mangthang zong in
Nong itna in kikhel hun nei ngeilo
A tawntung in, nang na hoih bel hi
Na min phatna la ken ka sa

Bridge

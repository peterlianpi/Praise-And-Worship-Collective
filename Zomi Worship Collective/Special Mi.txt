Title: Special Mi



Verse 1
Na hehpihna tawh, puan bang nong tuam hi
Nisim in kal thak, nong suan pih hi
Lauhuai keen thuk sung, kiat loh nang nong kem
Hong leng thaltangte, dal in nong hepkhiat sak
Chorus
Kei nong lum sak hi, lono na mun ah
Kei nong paipih hi, tuidam kiang ah
Thahatna nong guan, gualzawhna nong pia
Honpa Zeisu hang, special mi ka hi


Verse 2
Hong do galte sang, nang na lian zaw hi
Hong nawk huihpi sang, na hat zaw hi
Khulhpi lian pen sang, na muanhuai zaw hi
Kumpi lian pen sang, a belh taak zaw Zeisu
Verse 3
Kihtaak ding om kei, lauh ding zong om kei
Kei hong ompihna, ka tel nung sang
Buai luat ding om kei, manlah nang omkei
A hun hong cin ciang, a kamciam bang sem ding

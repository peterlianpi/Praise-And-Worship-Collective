Title: Zeisu Min Sung (In Jesus Name)



Verse 1
Pasian in hong do sak, ei lam ah hong pang
Amah gualzo hi hong, gual zawh sak khin hi
Ih ling nawn kei ding hi, ih khin kei ding hi
Zeisu hiah om hi
Verse 2
Ih vangik teng puakhia, zumna teng hong thuak
Amah gualzo hi hong, gual zawh sak khin hi
Ih ling nawn kei ding hi, ih khin kei ding hi
Zeisu hiah om hi


Chorus 1
Nung ta ning, si nawn kei ning
Khazih thawhkik na vangliatna
Ka sung nungta, tun suakta zo
Zeisu min sung
Chorus 2
Nung ta ning, si nawn kei ning
Hong laptohna ka tangko ding
Khazih ki lang, tun ka dam zo
Zeisu min sung
Bridge
Pasian in hong do sak, khuamialpi sawn pai in
Kumpi gam hong phut hi, tun ka ling thei nawn kei
Zeisu min suang peuh leng, galte gual lel khin hi
Eite ih awng ding hi, awng ding hi

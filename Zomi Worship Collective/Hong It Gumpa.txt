Title: Hong It Gumpa



Verse 1
Kumpite kumpi Zeisu
Van tokhom nusia’n, mibang hong suak zo
Vangnei tak kumpi inn sung pan in
A hong suak ding in kilawm pen napi’n
Ki niamkhiat in bawng ankuang hong tel zaw
Verse 2
A thuak ding thei khawl pi mah
A bawlsa leitung mi khempeuh mawhna
Singlamteh tung ah hong puak khiat sak
Pasian tawh ih omkhop tawntung theih nang
A nunna pia ding Pa kiang pan hong pai zo
Chorus
Eite ading gumpa Zeisu hong pai zo hi
Nuntakna ih ngah nang sihna hong thuak hi
Manpiak ding khempeuh hong piak sak zo hi
A lian pen pen, itna tawh hong it gumpa


Verse 3
Na khempeuh a nei Topa
Hong suah lai, a lu ngak nang zong om lo
Minphatna lukhu te suah khia in
Ling lukhu ki khusak gim hong thuak lai
A si tawh hong lei zo Pa kiang hong tun zo
Bridge
Piak khiatna khempeuh lakpan lian pen
Van leh lei khempeuh a manpha pen
A sang khempeuh a ding gupna thupha
Ei a ding gumpa hong ki pia zo

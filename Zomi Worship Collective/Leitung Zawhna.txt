Title: Leitung Zawhna



Verse 1
Bangmah lo pan in, ki pan khia a
Beidonna hang in, khitui luang zel mah leh
Hih ciang dong hong tonpih, hong makaih Topa
Muantaak leh belhtaak nang na hi
Chorus
Ka mailam ding
Bangmah lau nawn keng
Leitung a zo Zeisu nei ing
A kul leh kei ading in Topa’n
Ni leh kha te zong a khawl sak ding
Verse 2
Muhdah leh simmawh, in ka om a
Kamsiatna phungpi bang, in hong ki mu zong
Topa nang ka kiang ah nong ompih den a
Na vangliatna tawh nong tuam hi


Verse 3
Ka leitung pumpi, natheilo hi in
Haksat gentheih na te, thuak ngei lo hi leng
Topa hong itna te, ka phawk thei kei ding
Ka telsiam ta thuakna sung pan
Verse 4
Thupha hong piak hang, bek a nui hi leng
Vul in kun in ka nun-na’n mual liam ta ding
Ai zong ka kha in nang, hong nei khin Topa
Halleluiah ka lungdam hi

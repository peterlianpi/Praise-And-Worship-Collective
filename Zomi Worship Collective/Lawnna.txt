Title: Lawnna




Verse 1
A thahat lo te
Thahatna a thak hong guan kik thei
Itna lunggulh in
A hawm lungtang a hong dim kik thei

A hong ki khel sak thei
Mittaw te khua hong mukik sak thei
Kham leh ngun te sang
A manpha zaw nong lawnna hi

Pre-Chorus
Ka lungtang hong sukha pen
Kei nong itna hi Topa



Chorus
Na dikna khut tawh kei nong lawn ciang’n
Itna ang sung ah kei nong pom ciang’n
Ka lungnopna ki cing
Ka nunna hong ki lamdang sak hi
A ki tamzaan lungtang hong dam sak
Ka lungsim hong tuisuak sak den hi
Ka lungtang hong bun pen
Topa nong lawnna hi

Verse 2
A zawm khut leh khe
A thakhauh sak thei a phongto thei
A keu nuntakna
Hinna pia in a nosuak sak thei

A sak lungtang te
A nemkik sak thei a satzaan thei
Tehpih dang omlo
A vanglian a nuam nong lawnna hi

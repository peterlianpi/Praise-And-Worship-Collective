Title: Pasian Minthang Ta Hen




Verse 1
Pasian minthang ta hen, na lianpi bawl hi
Leitung a itluat ma’n, a Ta hong pia hi
Mawhna pan suahtak nang, a nunna pia hi
Mi khempeuh ading, nunna kong hong hon hi

Chorus
Phat ve un, phat ve un
Athu’n leitung zel hen
Phat ven un, phat ven un
Mi khempeuh lungdam hen
Topa Zeisu min suang in, Pa kiang zuan un
Na lianpi bawlzo hi, A minthang sak un



Verse 2
Sisan tawh hong lei zo, tatkhiatna man zo
A um mi ta ding, Pasian kamciam om zo
Mimawh pen nangawn in, Zeisu a up leh
A up phet in, a mawhmai pah ding hi veh

Verse 3
Na lianpi hong hilh a, na lianpi bawl hi
Pasian ta hangin nopna, cing i ngah hi
Hi hang lamdang Honpa, Zeisu i muh teh
Lungdamna, nuamna, lianzaw kan ding hi ven

Title: Nong Thei




Verse 1
Hoih lohna kicin lohna tawh kidim
Pau banna tawh kidim ka nunna
Hong en khial ngei lo Zeisu
Thupha tampite ka san theihna in
Kei hoihna kilawmna hang hilo
Kei nong Itna hang bek hi

Verse 2

Kei ading asia pen hong kigel ciang

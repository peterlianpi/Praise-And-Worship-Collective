Title: Aw Zeisu Amen Hong Paita In



Verse 1
Zeisu hong paikik nang, ciaptehna khempeuh leh
Pasian kamsangte genkholh te tung zo
Ih Topa Zeisu in, ama vangliatna tawh
Mi siangthote manlang in hong khawm ding
Chorus
Aw Zeisu, Amen hong pai ta in
A gimte khawlna hun hong tung ta hen
Ngak lah ing, Amen hong paita in
It leh ngaih teng ki muhkikna ding hun
Verse 2
Van ah ciaptehna te, hong tung kim khin zo ta
Ni mial, kha mial, aksi te hong kia hi
Sangpen Topa mai ah, lei leh van beimang zo
Topa hong pai ding hun hong naita hi


Verse 3
Leitung hunsia khinzo, siatna hong khang sem ta
Kidona awging tampi kiza hi
Gitlohna hong khang in, huaiham angsung genna
Topa hong pai ding hun hong nai ta hi
Verse 4
Hi gam lungdamna thu, leitung mun khempeuh ah
Gen khit mateng bei na hong pianglo ding
Ki pum piak na nei in, deihsakna tawh sem ni
Hehpihna kong khak ma in zo meng ni

Title: It Tawp Lo



Verse 1
Hoihna phatna siamna hang hilo
Pilna hatna neihsate, hang hilo
Siangtho pen mun ah ka lut theihna
Nong hehpihna hang hi Topa
Verse 2
A tawntung nang tawh ka om theih na
Na maitang mu in na khut
Kong let theih nang
Man ngen lo na nunna nong pia hi
It tawplo kei nong it man hi


Chorus

Kei nong domto hi

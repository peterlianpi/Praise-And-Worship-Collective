Title: Nang Mah Bek Hong Muang Ing




Verse 1
Ka neu lai in, laisiangtho sim in
Ka nu hong thuhilhna te ken ngilh thei keng
Topa bek, muanding hong sin sak
A muantaak bel Topa ka muang thei ta

Haksat hun ciang, Aman hong ompih
Ka mai ah meivom pi hong hepkhiat sak hi
Lungdam sing, Topa ka neih ma’n
Lung muang tak in, tun kei ka om thei ta

Chorus
A muantak bel, Suangpi na hi
Sun leh zan in, nong kem den hi
Tuu bang in, nong cing den a
Lam tawp dong in, hong ton pih aw
Nang mah bek hong muang ing



Verse 2
Ka mailam hun ding, tun kihta nawn keng

Lung muang tak in tun kei ka om thei ta

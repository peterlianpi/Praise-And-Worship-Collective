Title: A Kicing Hehpihna



Chorus
Topa nong hehpihna
It baang lo, kei a ding
Kicing hi, bei hun neilo hi
Kikhel hun, nei ngeilo
Zing sim thak suak den hi
Nong hehpihna
Tehpih ding a dang omlo
Verse 1
Haksat hun ciang, nong ompih den hi
Hong huh in, lam nong, lak den hi
Ka dah hun ciang, nong hehnem den hi
Kei a ding, ka lawm hoih pen nang na hi


Verse 2
Ka mailam hun, na khut sung om hi
Nang sung ah, lungmuang, in om ning
Hong nusia ngei lo, mawh in khial mah ta leng
A tawntung, nong ompih den hi

Title: Hanciam Lai




Verse 1
Ih hihna kiphawk lo mi, tampi lak ah
Kei zong khat in ka kihel, lam tun tel sem ing
Pauban nang bei theilo, mihing nun zia
Thei kawm sa mah in
Itna lah ding sang’n hawmthawh na tamzaw zel 

Chorus
Ken zong hanciam lai ing
Zani sang’n ka hoih zawk nang’n
Kei tha lel tawh bel
Bangmah tantun kei ning
Kei lungmuanna hong thahat sak
Khris hang in na khempeuh ah
Sep zawh nang’n thanei ing



Verse 2
Ih hihna mun khatpeuh pan, kipan khia in
Thusia sang’n thupha teel in, itna mitsuan ni
Na puk khak zenzen hang, lau nang omkei
Tho to kik zel in
Topa hehpihna’n nisim in ki cing hi

Title: Zeisu Sung Bek (In Christ Alone)




Verse 1
Zeisu sung bek, lametna om
Ka meivak leh ka thahatna
Ka suangpi leh, ka bukna hi
Tuipi guahpi in nawk zo lo

Chorus 1
Sang pen itna, sang pen nopna
Launa buaina’n, hong khaktan ciang
Hong lungmuang sak, nang bek na hi
Sangpen itna nang sung ka mu

Verse 2
Mi bang hong piang, tua Zeisu hi
Pasian Tapa takpi gim thuak
Hong piak itna, leh diktatna
Zeisu’n hong gum in simmawh thuak

Chorus 2
Singlamteh ah, Zeisu sih ciang
Pasian hehna, hong daidam in
Mawhna khempeuh, Zeisu’n puak sak
Kei sih tang ding Zeisu hong si



Verse 3
Suang hawm sungah, Zeisu kivui
Leitung khempeuh khuamial in tuam
Ni thum khit ciang, nopna hong piang 
Han sung pan Zeisu thokik hi

Chorus 3
Gualzawhna tawh, hong dintoh ciang
Ka tungah samsiatna teng bei
Amah kei a, kei ama a
Zeisu sisan manpha’n hong lei

Verse 4
Sih ding lau lo, mawhna teng mai
Kei ama a ka hih khit teh
Ka piancil pan, ka hu tawp dong
Zeisu sawlna bek ka tung ding

Chorus 4
Leitung gelna, mihing gelna’n
Zeisu kiang pan, hong khen zo kei 
Hong pai kik in, hong ciah pih dong
Zeisu vangliatna sung ka om

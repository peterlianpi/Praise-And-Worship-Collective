Title: Na Min Hong Lian Sak Ung




Verse 1
Ka kumpipa, ka Tou Zeisu, na min a lian pen e
Phat ding kilawm, biak ding kilawm na hi
Vangletnate, ukzohnate, tangtawn nang a ahi
Phat ding kilawm, phat taak Pathian na hi

Chorus
Aw Zeisu, na min hong lian sak ung
Aw Zeisu, na min hong tawi sang ung
Kumpite Kumpi na hi
Lei khempeuh phat ding kilawm
Aw Zeisu, na min lian hi



Verse 2
Nang hong bia ung, na mai kun ung
Thupina hong pia ung
Khuavan nuai ah, na min ngaih hi na e
Na itna sang, na itna lian, a hoih Pathian na hi
A tawntung in, na min ka hong phat uh

Verse 3
Ka kilawmna, ka sianthouna, na sisan pha ahi
Ka siamtanna, ka moh maina na hi
Nang hong zuan ung
Na khebul ah, zahtakna sang pen toh
Nang kia bia in, na min ngaih hong phat ung

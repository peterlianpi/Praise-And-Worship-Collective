Verse 1
 Enven ka teenna’ng gamnuam, leimite’ atel zawhloh,
 Mizawng mipoite leh, tagahte’ nopsakna gam;
 Kei hong simmawh khakei’n maw, inn le lo kaneih loh hang,
 Lei inn sang aphazaw van ah inn nuam ka nei hi.
 

Chorus
 Ngaklah veng, tua khuapi ka tun hun ding,
 To Jesuh a khutpawn ka let ni ciang;
 Lungdamna khitui te aluang ding-a,
 Kei hong honpa aw, ci-in lungdamin la kasa ding.

Verse 2
 Enven tuuno anglai-ah, melsia mipoi tampi te,
 Vanmi bang mel etlawm, Topa’n bawlpha khinzo ta;
 Lei-ah I kosiatna te, khamlukhu-in khu ta uh,
 A-lu tung teziahziah, suangmanpha tawh kizemhi.

Verse 3
 Sihna hanmual hong liamsan, apaisa it le ngaih te,
 Hih lei munsiatna gam, nusia-in hongpai un ci;
 Kei ngai-in kap kei un la, kei’omna-ah hong pai un,
 I tun ding hong ngaklah, van khuapi pan, khut hong vaan.

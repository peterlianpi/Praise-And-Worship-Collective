Verse 1
 Topa ka kidalna lum leh, ka munkip muanhuai ahi ta,
 Amah tawh tonleng kalampi nuam semsem ta;
 Ama’ khutin kei honglen-a, muanhuaitakin hongkemta,
 Topa in eite zawhna ahi.
 

Chorus
Thangsiahte’ thangzaak panin, akikhahkhia vasa bangin,
 Laamin, diangin, Topa’min kaphat theita;
 Kamawhna teng hongmaisak, kanatna teng zong hong damsak,
 Nuamhilhial ka omthei ta, hongit Topa’mai ah.

Verse 2
 Sihna liim guamthuksung nangawn, ka nawk hang ka laukei ding hi.
 A molhtum le a ciangkhut in hong lungmuang sak;
 Kagalte’ omna mai-ah, sabuai phahin an honglui ta,
 Topa in eite’ zawhna ahi.

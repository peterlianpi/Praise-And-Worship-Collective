Verse 1
 Ka khualzinna leitungah lawmhoih khat nei ing,
 Tua in hong it ka To Jesuh hi;
 Nisim hong lungdamsak, nisim la hong sa sak,
 Lawmhoih Jesuh kaneih mah, manpha bel.|
 

Chorus
 Muvanlai bangin ah, ka thahat theihnading,
 Tha khauhna le lungnopna hongpia;
 ka lawmhoih Jesuh in Thupha namkim hong pia.|

Verse 2
 Jesuh tawh kaki muh ni a ki pan inah,
 Nisimin lungdam kipaak den ing;
 Dahna thuakna te-in, hongbuaisakzo nawnlo,
 Lawmhoih Jesuh kaneih mah manpha bel.|

Verse 3
 Oh ! kong it sanggam aw, nangzong hongpai pah vaw,
 A migi ka To Jesuh kiangah;
 Sapna khempeuh kicing, nangzong hongpia nuam hi,
 Hongzuanin, kongit, kongit sanggam aw.
 
Sakkik-Coda A um mita’ dingin, manpiakloh letsong hi,
 Nuntaktawntungna nangah nading;
 Tu’n Jesuh nasaan leh, Vangam nang-a’hi ta.

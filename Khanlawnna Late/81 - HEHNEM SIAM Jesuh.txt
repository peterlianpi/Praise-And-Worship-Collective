Verse 1
 Lungzuan dahna te’n dai bang hong nawk ciangin ah,
 Hehnem siampa Jesuh ngaihsun in;
 Amah’n hong enpah-a, damna sung hongtun pah,
 A Kha sung zatui tawh, tongdamnem hong luan.
 

Chorus
 Hongliah hongdal ding, amah hong kikham,
 Topa Kha nang kiang’h hong om tawntung;
 Lungsung siktui bang nem, lungzuang kha hong khoidam,
 Hennemsiam pa Zeisu’n, a angsung hong sung.

Verse 2
 Haksatna khat peuhpeuh, na thuak khak tak ciangin,
 Lungdam ko in amah phat zaw in;
 Lungsim kiniamkhiatin, Amah zuan peuh le cin,
 Nopssak theihna lungsim, hong piaksa hi khin.

Verse 3
 It le ngaih te’n hong paisan hong nusiat ciangin,
 Dahteng thuakpih nuam Jesuh I lawm;
 Hong theisiam mahmah-a, Jesuh ko peuh le cin,
 Khamuanna le nopna, tui bang hong luangkhawm.

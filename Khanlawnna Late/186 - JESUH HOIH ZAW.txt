Title:186 - JESUH HOIH ZAW

Van aksi leh thilsiamte khempeuh,
Tuipi te leh taang te kilawm;
Ahoih na te suiseng hilou,
Tua te sang in Tou Jesu hoih zaw.

Aw kilawm na e, ka Tou Jesu,
Aw a manphat dan, suiseng hilou;
Aw a thilsiam khempeuhte sang,
Jesu hoihzaw hi aw tua te sang in.

Hauhna hoih in,siamna zong hoih hi,
Nuntakna ding i geel te zong;
Inn leh lou leh neih leh lam zong,
Tua te sang in Tou Jesu hoih zaw.

Kisak theihna Jesu ah kanei,
Manphatna te neita kei leng;
Ka bangkim hi ka Tou Jesu,
Khovel sang in Tou Jesu hoih zaw.

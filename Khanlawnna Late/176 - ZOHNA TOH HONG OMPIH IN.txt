Title:176 - ZOHNA TOH HONG OMPIH IN

Hiai khovel ah hi sualgal a hong lian a,
Van Jerusalem zuan a ka pai lai in,
Ka sung a muan na kha hong ki liin chiang in,
Toupa aw nang hong makaih na kha’n hong om pih in.

 Hong leen in sual na zaan mial ah
 Hong pi in Tuipi lian tung ah
 Ni sim in sual galdouna haksa in lauhuai zong
 Zohna toh Nang hong ompih in aw.

Ziing ni nou leh ziingsang daitui bang kia a,
Silpak leh louhingte’ nunna bawl thak zel,
Aw na mite’ nunna pahna om nawn lou,
Pahna daitui in na kha toh hong om pih in.

Aw ka zogam lei uh moh na’n dim zouta,
Khelhnate honglian a ni sim in hon tuam
Leitung nopsakna sung ah kibual ta ung,
Toupa aw nang hong ngilh ung ngaihdamna hong pia in.

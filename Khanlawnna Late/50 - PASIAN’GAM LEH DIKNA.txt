Verse 1
 Pasiangam le dikna Pasian’thu le deihna,
 Thupitak ngaihsun dih ni;
 Leitung nate sangin thu dangdang te sangin,
 Zong masazaw le cin, tua nate tawh hong piakhawm ding hi;
 

Chorus
:Sul honghei vaw, sanggam aw, Sianmang sinthu
 An bang kumkhawm in-ah, Amai zong dihni;
 Vanman tam-a, ih lungkhamna te ,Jesuh zawhna sungah
 Hong tuisuak dinghi, Vandaitui te hong luang ding hi.

Verse 2
 Athu sung’h kibualleeng, Akha sung’h kidiahleeng,
 Amathu ah lungkim leeng,
 I lunggulh na te thei, I tangsapna te nei,
 I ngaihsutna sangin, hong pianuam zaw amah muang peuhleeng.

Verse 3
 Haksatna hong tunciang, lungkhamna hong tunciang,
 Gilkial dangtaak i thuakciang;
 Pasian’mai zong zaw ni, Pasian mai en zaw ni,
 Midangte muanding sang, Jesuh kumpi muanding hoih zaw hi.

Verse 4
 I gitlohna pan-in, i lungsim niamkhiat in,
 I Topa’ kiang zuan dih ni;
 I lungsim sungkhia in, i mawhna pulaak ni,
 Tuahileh i gamsung, thupha hongpia’n hong damsak ding hi.

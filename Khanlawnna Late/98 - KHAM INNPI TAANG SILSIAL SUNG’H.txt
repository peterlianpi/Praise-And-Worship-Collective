Verse 1
 Khaminnpi taang silsial sung’h, ka tun ni ciang in,
 Kitot kisel pawl kideina omlo ding;
 Sisan phatna la sa ning, tatsa misiang honpi tawh,
 Ngaklah sing, khuttawh bawlloh innpi-a.
 

Chorus
 Lungdamna la thak tawh Topa min phat ning,
 Nuamlua sinteh Zeisu’meel ka muh ni ciang;
 Singteh niimdildial nuai-ah, kangh ngeilo gun gei-ah,
 Hong tanpa khutpawnte ka len ding hi.

Verse 2
 Leitung buaina khempeuhte’n, hong khemzo nawnlo,
 Oh! nuamluasi, Jesuh tawh nuntak khawm;
 A sisan ka mawhmaina, a liamma hang ka damna,
 Deihna teng , cinna ka to Jesuh hi.

Verse 3
 Zudawn thaikapna te le, deidanna omlo,
 Leitung sum le paii tawh lei zawh ding hi kei;
 Khawl ngeilo-in phat den ning, tuuno gualzawhna lapi,
 Teedimadiam kham tawh kibawl innpi ah.

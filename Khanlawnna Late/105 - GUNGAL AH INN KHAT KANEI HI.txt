Verse 1
 Lungkham in ka khuisat laitak,
 Gungal ah ka neih innpi;
 Ngaihsun leng nuam kasa taktak,
 Nungciang ka teen nang hi veh.
 

Chorus
 Gungal ah inn khat ka nei hi,
 Ataang innpi khat nei ing;
 Gungal ah inn khat kanei hi,
 Jesuh tawh kateeng ding hi.

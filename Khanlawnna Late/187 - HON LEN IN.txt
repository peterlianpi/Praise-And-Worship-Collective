Title:187 - HON LEN IN

Toupa aw kei hon len in, Simlei a kazin lai in;
Keikia in kapai siamlou, Ka zinna a haksa in;
Lamhaih in gammang zel ing, Na lamvak hon musak in hon zui ning maw.

Na lam vak ah ka pai theih na din, Ka lampi ah Na vak hong taan in;
Mittaw bang khua mulou a, Zanmial bang kapai lai in;
Hon len in ka puklouh na’n hon len in aw.

Buaina tui kihawt kal a, Lauhna te’n kei hong tuam a;
Lung muanna hon neisak aw, Muanhuaipa a kip tawntung;
Nang na hi Nang kia na hi, Toupa aw Na ban in hon lenden in aw.

Galkhat lam ka hong kai ma’n, Tua mual siangthou kong tun ma’n;
Lampial lou di’n hon len in, Nisim in ka nuntakna;
Hong siamthak hong hatsak in, Na angsung ka tun mateng hon pi in aw.

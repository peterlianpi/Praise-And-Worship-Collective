Verse 1
 Ka mai ah lungkham buaina,
 Bawlsiatna gensiatna ahi;
 Dawimangpa le satan khutin,
 Hongpom hang, Jehovah shalom ka hong ngak ee.
 

Chorus
 Jehovah shalom ka lungmuanna, 
 Jehovah Jire ka hatna;
 Phamawh sak ka nei lo-a,
 Topa tawh ka om den ee.

Verse 2
 Hong bawlsia dawimangpa leh,
 Oh! sihna ka theih ciangin ah;
 Oh! khuamial ka theihna hi,
 Hongtaan in Jehovah sahlom ka hong ngak ee.

Verse 3
 Aw! Topa Pasian in ah,
 Lau ke’n sihna ah zong lau ke’n;
 Lungmuang takin om phot mai-In,
 Na mai-ah ka ding zel ding a ci.

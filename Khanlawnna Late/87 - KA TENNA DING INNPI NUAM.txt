Verse 1
 Katen nading innpi nuam, Upna tawh galet ing,
 Nunna guntui luang sialsial, Huihdam zong nung hiauhiau;
 Vantung misiangtho te’n, phatnala sa ziahziah,
 Katen nading innpi nuam in, kei hong ngak gige.
 

Chorus
 Honpa’n tokhom tung pa’n, ngaklah in hon en en,
 Hua gungal pekpek ah, kham khuapi nuam sung ah;
 Phatnala sa khawm ding, tuuno pawipi sung ah.

Verse 2
 Ka kha bek hong gum hilo, kapumpi zong hong gum,
 Huh leitung gimna lianpi, hong thuaksak ngeilo ding;
 Van innnuam ka ciah ciang, hongtan Topa Zeisu’n,
 Ahongdawn ding amaw, lungdam khut kileen ding.

Verse 3
 Hih leitung pa’n vantung ah, ka leentoh ni ciangin,
 Hong honpa’ vangliatna tawh, mitphiatkalin ka leeng ding ;
 Leitung mangpha ci-in, aw nuam-zensiinteh maw,
 Halelu-Jalelujah ci’n, phatnala sasa ding.

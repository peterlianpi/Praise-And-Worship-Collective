Chorus
 Vangliatna kangah khin-a,
 Pasian’ta kahih nung sang;
 Lungdamna kohding simloh,
 Leiba dang kei’n nei nawn kei’ing.|

Verse 1
 Hong honding khat zong neilo,
 Khitui luang ka omlai tak;
 Ka khitui hong nulsak-a,
 A angsung’h kei hongpom ta.|

Verse 2
 Natna in kei hongtuam in,
 Hong vehding kaneih lohlai;
 Thoto in nadam zo hi,
 Hong cihman ka damsiang ta;
 

Verse 3
 Tu’n keipawh patau nawn ke’ng,
 Mawhmaisak-Pa nei zo ing;
 Sihna in kei hongzol hang,
 Jesuh hang’n Gupna ngah ta’ng;

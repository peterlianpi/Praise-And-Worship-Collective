Title:61 - LEITUNG MITTAWH MEL KAMUH LOH HANG



Verse 1
 Leitung mittawh mel kamuhloh hang,
 Vanglian Pa’ khutin, paii-bang hongpom den;
 Naubang hong kepna te, hambang honghuai nate,
 Anoop innuam semsem Halelujah.

Verse 2
 Leitung pilna bangmah neilo a,
 Lungmawl kei banglel, mimcii bang nongteel,
 Sian aw, naleenggahhuan, giabang zuun kacimlo,
 Lau Khasiangtho tuibang hong dimden aw.
 

Chorus
 Tung solkhapi amial zong,
 Tung sunni mual aliam zong,
 Mualliam theilo kei nongitna;
 Cinglo, taaklo, tualniam kanunna,
 A saangna lam hong kaito,
 AGAPE itna Pasian, na Min paakbang
 Kong tawina, zaila’n naubang hong awi awi ning aw.

Verse 3
 Patpuan hilo, naipuan hilo-a,
 Zeisu’sisan tawh, kizem puan silhta’ng;
 Lungin kageelgeel ciang, hoihin ahoih semsem,
 An bang hongitna lian tumdang diak sa’ng.

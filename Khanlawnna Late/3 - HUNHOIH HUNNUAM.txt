Verse 1
 Hunhoih hunnuam hongmu sak, hun kilawm hongngahsak,
 Hun manpha sung, hongtonpih ka To;
 Zumleh hiam panin hongkem; lauhuai napan hongkem,
 Cidamtakin aminpha kalo.
 

Chorus
Lungdamsing hih hunpha sungah, ka Topa′n lamhongpii,
 Nasi pha tawh hong naisakhi;
 Na omnapeuh, lungnopna kanunna′n vaangnei,
 Nongitna kei sungah, meibangin lumden hen,
 Halelujah aminthang hen.

Verse 2

 Hici bangin hongluang a thupha.

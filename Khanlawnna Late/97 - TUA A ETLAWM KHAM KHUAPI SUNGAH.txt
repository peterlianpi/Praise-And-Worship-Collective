Verse 1
 Sihna mualkuam gal ah, khuapi lian khat omhi,
 Ahoihna kuamah’n gen zolo hi
 Zankimlai-in tuuno, aksi bang in taang-a,
 Tua a etlawm kham khuapi sung ah.
 

Chorus
 Tualai-ah, ni tumlo,
 Singteh zong vul ngeilo;
 Midik te atawntung, aksi bang in taang-a,
 Tua a etlawm kham khuapi sung ah.

Verse 2
 Tualai-ah, dahna, natna, sihna om nawnlo,
 Misiang khempeuh zong teek thei nawnlo;
 Tua khua-ah ut zen sing, zan khuamial lah beita,
 Tua a etlawm kham khuapi sung ah.

Verse 3
 Leitung I nutsiatding ,ngaklah khin zotaing,
 Gimna, tawlna te cimtaak zo ta’ng;
 Lawmgual it, tampi te’n van kongkhak pan hongngak,
 Tua a etlawm kham khuapi sung ah.

Verse 4
 Pengkul hong kitum ciang, kei zong kaleengto ding,
 Meiitungah, laaktohna ka ngah ding;
 Mippoi cih om nawnlo, van misiangtho te tawh,
 Tua a telawm kham khuapi sung ah.

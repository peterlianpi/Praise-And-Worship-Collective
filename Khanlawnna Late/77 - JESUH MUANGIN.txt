Verse 1
 Zing kazinna simlei-ah, Tomvei kaal kanun sungin,
 Khat veivei mite’n hihna, beelin muangkha dekzel ing;
 Hi zong tua leitungna te sung, tawntung lungnuam om kei,
 Ka mangngilh khak Jesuh sung, deih teng kicing.
 

Chorus
 Mite belh, muanding saangin, Jesuh kumpi muan hoihzaw,
 Kumpi milian te muan saang, zong hoihzaw lai;
 Jesuh sung sapna kicing, nuntakna ciktui zong phul,
 Jesuh nei-in muang le cin, Gualzo in, na daupai ding.

Verse 2
 Leitung pilna, siamna le minthanna tuamtuam zong-in,
 Kei le kei lah kimuanglo, mite’mai enkha zel ing;
 Hi zong tua kalam-ette ’ sung, kasapna teng cingtuamlo,
 Ka mangngilh khak Jesuh sung, sapna te cing.

Verse 3
 Mite’ mai-et dingsaangin, mite belh, muanding saangin,
 Pasian zahtaakin muangin, amai enin beel denleeng;
 I tup isawmna te leh, I tangsapna te khempeuh,
 I mangngilh khak Jesuh sung, avekpi’n cing.

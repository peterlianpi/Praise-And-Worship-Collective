Verse 1
 Hih leitung in gibang khenna gam hi,
 Mitphiatkal sung nangawn, lungmuan om theilo;
 Tu nuam taleng ding, khawl nuam taleeng pai,
 Hih leitung kikhenna gam hi.
 

Chorus
 Phatni heina peuhah, Topa phatni,
 Ih khen zongin Jesuh sung kimu ding;
 Lei ah I kimu diam, van kimlai-ah,
 Hih leitung kikhenna gam hi.

Verse 2
 Ih khen zongin kimu ding khawlmun-ah,
 Hih leitung in I khualzinna gam bek hi;
 I khawl khawm zong ho-lungcim omman lo,
 Hih leitung kikhenna gam hi.

Verse 3
 Hun le tuiluang in, hongpai khengkheng veh,
 Hih leitung hun a sawt nawn ding hi lo pi;
 Leitung aw, mangpha Jesuh hong nai zo,
 Kikhen om nawnlo ding van ah.

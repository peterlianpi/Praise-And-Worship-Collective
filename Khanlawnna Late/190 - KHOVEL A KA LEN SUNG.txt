Title:190 - KHOVEL A KA LEN SUNG

Verse
Khovel a ka leen sung in, Ka hindan ding hong hilh in;
Hiai khovel ka inn a hihlouh dan, Ka mun ding na kiang hi zaw;
Ka inn ding na kiang hi zaw, Kipak a kong tun theihna di’n.

Hon theisak in aw Toupa, Khovel ka leen sungin;
Na deih leh Na lunggelna, Nisim in kei hong hilh in;
Ka nunna leh kalsuante, Toupa aw kei hong pi in aw.

Khovel a ka leen sungin, Toupa aw kei hong hilh in;
Ka deihtelnate na deih hi di’n, Na paina ka pai theih a;
Na khawlna a khawl thei din, Ka lutang nanglam hei in aw.

Khovel a ka leen sungin, Kei tunga a hong tungte;
Nang a pan Na sawl hi zeel hen aw, Nopnate ka tuah ni in;
Dahnate ka tuah ni in, Ka khitui nang hon sawl hi hen

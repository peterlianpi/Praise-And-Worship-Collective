Title:173 - NA PATHIAN SAM IN

Aw i gam gingtu hinkhua,
Siatnaten zing daibang in a nawk;
Tangthupha in a luahna gam,
Sualnate in aw a dim zouta.

 Thou inla na Pathian sam in,
 Aw i gam mangthang ding hilou maw?
 I khelhnate thupha tawi in,
 Aman hon ngaidam lai ding ahi.

Tuun leh zua a mangthang uh,
Chiin leh tuaite zong a mangthang uh;
Mulkimhuai siatnate sung ah,
Aw setan sal in a taang uh e.

Lungmuang in i omlai maw?
Don lou in hun sawt i omta ngei;
Kua a hiam itna khitui toh,
Aw a maute ding a thu ngen ding?

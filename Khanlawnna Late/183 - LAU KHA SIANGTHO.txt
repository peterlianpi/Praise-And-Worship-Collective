Title:183 - LAU KHA SIANGTHO

Aw hong ngai ung khauva bang nem, Van laukha Siangtho hon ngai ungh
Nang telllou in kalsuan dan ka theihsiam kei uh Hong phel inla, hon velcian in
Ka sung ua mawhna om khempeuh Hong halsiang in hong bawlpha in laukha Siang aw

 Aw hong ngai ung laukha siangtho
 Kote sungh ah hong sem inla
 Gualzawhna guah kote ading hong zusak aw
 Aw khat vei beek hong veh lai in
 Bah leh nguite hong phawghalh di’n
 Halhna meikuang laukha siangtho hon ngai ungh ee…

Huan tu bang in hon cing inla Lamhaih lammangte pikik in
Khelhna zanmial nuai a omte hong tanvak in Lungsik simthu lel nuamlo a
Lungtang kahha zatam omte A neem sak thei laukha Siangtho nang kia na hi

Topa na laukha vang in aw, Kipahna lui hong luanlet in
Hon dimsak in hon tuisak in laukha siang aw Si napi a lungmuang laite
Hing minpua a lungmuang laite Na hotdamna na lugnmuanna ka ngai uh e

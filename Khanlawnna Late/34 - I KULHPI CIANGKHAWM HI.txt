Verse 1
 U le nau aw lungngai dihve, ikiim ipaam khuadak dihve,
 I belhding le hongdalding omhetlo pi;
 Khuasung kongzing lamdung tengah, 
 lungmuang-a kalsuan omlo pi,
 I siamna ciat, gawmkhawmin kulhpi ciangni.
 

Chorus
 I kulhpi ciangkhawm ni, i kulhpi ciangkhawm ni,
 Tuahileh galte lutzo lo dinghi;
 Khamuangtak lungmuangtakin, i kalsuan theihnading in,
 I siamna ciat tawh semin kulh ciangkhawm ni.

Verse 2
 Huihpi nungte’n hongnawkkha leh, inn le lote mutpaai ding hi,
 Kigingkhol in lungmuanna kulhpi ciangni;
 Kisiatna lianpi hong pianma, Supnalianpi ithuakma in,
 I siamna ciat gawmkhawmin kulhpi, ciangni.

Verse 3
 Topa hongsapcil lungngai-in, Khitui luang-a naki ap hun,
 Tangmi siangte sepzia te lungngaikik in;
 Jerusalem kulhpi cimsa, aciankhopzia lungngai kawmin,
 I siamna ciat gawmkhawmin kulhpi, ciangni.

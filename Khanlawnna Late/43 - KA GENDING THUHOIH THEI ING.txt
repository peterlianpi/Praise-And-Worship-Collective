Verse 1
 Ka gending thuhoih thei-ing, ka sakding langaih nei-ing,
 Bangthu bangla hiam ci’n hongki dot leh;
 Lei le van a mangthang zong, Ka kammal mangthang lo ding,
 Ci-in honggen Pa Zeisu’ Thu hiveh.
 

Chorus
Hongpai-un sutdihni, hongpaiun zaidihni,
 Sisanmanpha zailapi, gualzawhna lapi awi ni;
 Jesuh in Topa hi, kumpite’ Kumpi hi,
 Tanbang tehpihding omlo, Vanglian I pa mah hoihbel hi.

Verse 2
 Cinate lah damsak-a, misite zong thosak hi,
 Mawhnei mite zong Thumanlam hilh hi;
 Mittawte khuamisak in, khebaite zong diangsak hi,
 Nangzong na up leh kong hongki honlai.

Verse 3
 Hankongcingte ’n cingzolo, Hansuangpi zong’n dalzo lo,
 Lamdang zingsangkhat ei adinghong tho;
 Ama’mintawh cinadam, tuaminmahtawh dawite zo,
 Thawhkik vangliatna ei hongki pia zo.

Verse 4
 Hihthu katel akipan, hihla kasa nuamsemhi,
 Kalungsung hongtang, kavangik hongzaang;
 Hihthu mah sutsutlelning, hihzaila mah awiawi ning,
 Kagualzawhna hi, kagalhiam lah hi.

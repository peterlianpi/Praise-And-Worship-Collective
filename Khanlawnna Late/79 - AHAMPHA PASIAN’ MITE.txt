Chorus
 Ahampha Pasian’mite tawh kikholhkhop liailiai mah,
 Nop acihin nop e, Hamph Pasian’mite tawh kikholh
 khop liailiai mah, Nop acihin nop e, Mi khempeuh 
 Maitai-in aom uh nopsi ee, Pa Pasian a kiphat ziah-ziah teh , 
 hampha Pasian’mite tawh, kikholhkhop liailiai mah, 
 Nop acih’n nop ee.

Verse 1
 Munkhat peuhpeuh om taleng, Nih le thum bek hi taleng,
 Pasian’ Khasiangtho in hong ompih veve;
 Tuasang holimna nuamzaw, Koi-ah I mu zen tammaw,
 Pasian’tate kikholhna mah nuam mataw.

Verse 2
 A itna’n hong lungdamsak, lungkihual in hong omsak,
 Ih lungsim sangkhan in Topa I phat ding;
 Hong Gumpa kiang longal kei, ka utzawk na mun omkei,
 Nuamlua-ing kei, Pasian’ innkuanpih pi ta.

Verse 3
 Vanah Ikimuh ciangin, Topa’n hongsap tak ciangin,
 Misiang khempeuh kongpi tawnin pai ding h;
 Tokhom kiimah itte tawh, inn ah kikhawl ding mataw,
 Tua nitakteh nuam taktak in teh mataw.

Verse 1
 Lawpgige ing zingsang khat peuh teh, Gabriel pengkul tum ke’n za ning
 Sinawnloding, ka khan lawh ciang;
 It huai Tanpa mu ding ka dawn teh, lungdam awng in lei nusia ning,
 Sinawnlo ding ka khanlawh ciang.
 

Chorus
 (Sinawnlo ding) Zingsang khat peuh ciang (ka khanlawh ciang)
 Suangmanpha kizeem,(lungdam si ning), Minthanna gam ah,
 (Vantung khuapi ah), HIh thu sutsut ning;
 A khang akhang-a, tatsa te tawh, Amah bia-in phatkhawm ta ding,
 Sinawnlo ding ka khanlawh ciang.

Verse 2
 Mitphiat kalsung in pumthak nei ning, Pasian a minthang den ta hen,
 Sinawnlo ding, ka khanlawh ciang;
 Tua khuapi ka zuat toh ciang in ah, dahna teng mangphakhak ning kei’n,
 Sinawnlo ding, ka khan lawh ciang. 

Verse 3
 Akitansa minam khempeuh te tawh, vantung hoihna ke’n velvel ning,
 Sinawnlo ding, ka khanlawh ciang;
 Tuidam luanna singkungdum gei-ah, khawlna ding mun ke’n kamuding,
 Sinawnlo ding ka khan lawh ciang.

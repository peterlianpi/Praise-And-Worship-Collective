Verse 1
 Vanmite’n ka lungdam lam hong theihpih kei,
 Sisan tawh amau mawhna sawp kullo pi;
 Vantung ah la asak uh hangin khatvei,
 Lamdang itna la kasak teh daitak ngai ding uh hi.
 

Chorus
 Tua siangtho vanmi te sak theihloh la in,
 Lamdang itna, kei’n ngaihlua sa’ng;
 Tua siangtho vanmi te sak theihlo la in,
 Hong kimuzo, ka gammanh hang.

Verse 2
 Vanmite’n la a sak "Topa a siangtho hi, "
 Pasian’ tokhom mai-ah la saden keei veh!;
 Amau’la ka sakpih ding bel lamdang kei,
 Hong it manin la ka sakpih lambel thei kei-un teh.

Verse 1
 Nisim zingni suah pelmawh bang (suahpelmawhbang)
 Topa’hoihna ke’n muden-ing (kenmuden ing)
 Sunni tumin khua amial zong (khua amial zong)
 Topa’hoihna’n hong liamsan lo;
 Katha nemciang thathak hongpia (thatak hong pia)
 Kalung neuciang itna honglak (itna hong lak)
 Zanin hongkhim zingsang hongphong, (kei hongphongden)
 Topa’hoihna’n kei hongtuamden.
 

Chorus
Sun in hongcing, zanin hongkem, hong nusialo,
 Gungei kisuan singkung bangin, hongno suah sak;
 Ka kitangsap khempeuh hongthei, hongsikh tawntung,
 Nisim ke’n Topa’ hoihna telsemsem in.
 (Lamdang itna Topa’hoihna telsemsem ing.)

Verse 2
 Zankhuamial ka ihmut sungin, (ihmut sungin)
 Topa’hoihna’n kei hongtuamden (kei hongtuamden)
 Munkhempeuh ka omna peuh ah (om na peuhah)
 Itna Pasian hong makaih den;
 Khuamial na-ah, kakhialh loh nang (kakhialh loh nang)
 Topa kamei vak hong hi. (meivak hong hi),
 Tuithuk guamsung ka kiatlohnang (ka kiat loh nang).
 Topa’n kilok lo lei hong hi.

Verse 3
 Nisim in akhawnkhong in (akhawnkhong in)
 Ka diikdingin huih hongkawm (kei hongkawm hi),
 A tawntung (a khawnkhong in),
 Ka dawnding in tui hongpiahi (tui hongpia hi),
 Duang bansammi zatam lai-ah (zatam lai-ah),
 Tangsap neilo in hongbawl hi (kei hongbawl hi),
 Hongitna theilo mi zatam (mi zatam te) 
 Gen in honkhia dingin hong teel.

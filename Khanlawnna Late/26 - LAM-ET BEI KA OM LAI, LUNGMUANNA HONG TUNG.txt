Verse 1
 Lam-et bei ka omlai, lungmuanna hong tung a,
 Ka maiah meivompi hongkaai in kalam pi;
 Mialin a lauhuai hang, khuavak ni hong taang ta,
 Kei ading hongpai Jesuh Kumpi.|
 

Chorus
 Kei tang’a ding hong sihlawh ka Topa,
 Tawntung nnna lampi hong tun zo;
 Kei tang’a ding hong suplawh ka Topa,
 Ka nuntakna hong bawlhoihsak zo.|

Verse 2
 Pheiphung suan khain bah, mitgui te kham in zeu,
 Khat veivei lei gawtaak, tui omlohna gam ah;
 Singkung bang kanunna huihkhi dam semin keu,
 Ka om laitak Zeisu’n hong delh pah.|

Verse 3
 Kei tangdingin hongding, kei tangding hongkithawi,
 Keithuak dingteng hongthuak, kei septangin hongsem;
 Kei ading hongthuumsak, kei tangin mawhlawh tawi,
 Siattutna teng a mah’n tualin lem.

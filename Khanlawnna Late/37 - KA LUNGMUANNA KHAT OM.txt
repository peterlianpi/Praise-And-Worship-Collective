Verse 1
 Ka lungmuanna khat om, Lauke’n kata aw ci,
 Hong thapia aw-neem khat kaza;
 Na lampi kong honsak, lungkhamna kuampite,
 Lametna kongpi kong suaksak hi.
 

Chorus
Lau peuhmah ke’n ka ta aw,
 Vangliatna khempeuh kongpia zo;
 Kei na hong up uh leh, Keima’ bawl sang lianzaw,
 Na bawlthei ding uh hi, Ka kammalte nungkik lo.

Verse 2
 Na guallelhna lampi, gualzawhna tawh natawnding,
 Thahat in-la, hangin hong ci;
 Lau ke’nla lungkia ke’n, namai-ah gualzawhna in,
 Hongngak cih tel-in la, lau kei-in.

Verse 3
 Khasiangtho in hongsawl. Topa’ minthan nading,
 Na khutte tung’h vangliatna kongkoih;
 Na mukte honglawngin, Ka Thu le Ka Kammalte,
 Kong koihsak khin zo hi, lau kei-in

Verse 1
 Sehneelgam sung, tui naphulsak, Ishmael tung’h, napiak bangin,
 Zogam keu-ah nunnatui luangsak aw;
 Zomite’kha dangtaaklua-in, nunna lampa’n pialkhinta ung,
 Na Khasiangtho hongsawl kik in aw Topa.
 

Chorus
Hong pikhia-in, aw ! Topa hong pikhia-in,
 Na tuu huangnuam sungah omkim dingin;
 Muvanlai tha hongguanin, tai tampi hong tonpih in,
 Na khaliim nuai’h, hong khawlsak in aw Topa.

Verse 2
 Suangpi panin, tui na phulsak, Israelte, na dawnsak bang,
 Zomi khadangtaakte hong dawnsak aw;
 Israelte na pikhia-a, Canangam sung’h, na lutpih bang,
 Khasiangthogam sung’h hong lutpih aw Topa.

Verse 3
 Van-anluum le, vamim sa tawh, Israelte navak bangin,
 Zomite zong, neek le dawn hong pia aw;
 Nangma’ sungah, nuamsa dingin, aneu alian hong huaikimin,
 Itna Pasian nangbek hongmuang ung Topa.

Verse 4
 Kei vakuan ning mangthangte kiang’h, Nong Gupna Thu taangko dingin, 
 Zogam buppi hong khanglosak kik aw;
 Laulo hangtak, thugen dingin, Khasiangtho tha hongguankimin,
 Mangthangte’ kiang, Gupnathu genning Topa.

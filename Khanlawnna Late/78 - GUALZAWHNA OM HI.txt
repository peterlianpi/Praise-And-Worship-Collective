Verse 1
 Kalei mit tawh galkhuadaking Topa,
 Buaina le khaksatna in hongtuam-a;
 Cimawh, genthei, mizawng, dongtuak kahi-a,
 Ka khamit honghonsak in Topa.
 

Chorus
 Gualzawhna omhi, Gualzawhna omhi,
 Topa’sungah tawntung gualzawhna omhi;
 Leitung gualzawhna te-in tomnosung bek hi,
 Atawntung Topa’sungah omhi.

Verse 2
 Eilam ah hong gum Topa hongpang leh,
 I lang ah kua hong langpang zo ding hiam;
 Gideon galsim bangin Topa muang peuhleeng,
 Hong guallelsak ngeilo ka Topa.

Verse 3
 Thu hongpia, gualzawhna ding thu hongpia,
 Hong ompih, atawntungin hong ompih;
 Gualzawhna lasa in Topa phatden ning,
 Gualzawhna ahongpia ka Topa’n.

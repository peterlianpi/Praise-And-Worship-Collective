Verse 1
 Mimawh kahih laitakin, galte kahih laitakin,
 A sipha tawh kei hong lei-in, hong domsaang;
 Ka vangik teng hongzaang, ka lungdamna ni-bang tang,
 Oh! alamdang itna lian kei sungah.
 

Chorus
 ( Oh! alamdang itna lian ) 2
 Calvary pan-in hongluang, Halelujah praise the lord;
 Tua lungdamna tangthu, Igualzawhna sut dihni,
 Oh! alamdang itna lian kei sungah.

Verse 2
 Ka mawhna atam hangin, hong hehpihna lianzaw lai,
 Ka lungsimin katelna sang siangzaw lai;
 Hih Thumah khumpen sa’ng, khuaizu sangin khumzawlai,
 Nisimin khum semsem in, ngaih semsem.

Verse 3
 Ka lametna kip semsem, ka lungmuanna kho semsem,
 Zeisu’hoihna tawh nisimin kei hongzeem;
 Hih Thu katel nungsang, upna tawh kasan nungsang,
 Kei sungah hongdim semsem, katel nungsang.

Verse 4
 Pasian’min thang semsem hen, Pasian’ gam zai semsem hen,
 Vantung nopna mikim sungah hongtung hen;
 Tua alamdang lungnopna, Igualzawhna la ngaihpen,
 Leitung mimal tungah hongkizeel ta hen.

Title:89 - TUA KHUAPI TE ZEIZAI

Verse


Verse 1
 Vantung kongkhak te taang pempam,
 Sabuai te kiphah dim dem,
 Kong honsa gei-ah mikhat ding veh;
 A te zialzial gun ka mu-a,
 Katen nading mun hi ta,
 Hi zah liang in inn lunggulh ngei keng.
 

Chorus
 Tua khuapi te zeizai, inn ka tun ding zong hong nai,
 Ka pa’n zong kongkhak kiangah hong ngak zen veleh;
 Hih leitung gamsimtham pa’n, suakta ding kiging zo ta’ng,
 Hi zah liang in inn lunggulh ngei keng.


Verse 2
 A innkuan kuan in kikhawm uh,
 A mai taii thei mahmah uh,
 A dahmai cihbang kimu lo pi;
 Lunggulhna tawh ka khi luang-a,
 Kha nei leng ka leengnuam ta,
 Hi zah liang in inn lunggulh ngei keng.

Title:175 - VANGLIAN PEN

Lei leh van bawl pa vanglian tung Pathian,
Na kilawm hi pahtawina tang di’n;
Na pilna sang e, Na lunggel sang e,
Na siamsate’n sut zoulou.

 Minthannate leh, vangletna khempeuh,
 Tangtawn tangtawn Nang a hi;
 Nang bang vanglian pen, Nang bang thupi pen,
 Lei leh thangvan ah omlou.

Koute hon it pa, koute honhuai pa,
Nong itna lian hi, kan in sang e;
Bangmahloute zong nong kem nong huai a,
Nong lamtou eklei pan in.

Ka dahna uh thei, ka lungkham uh thei,
Ka mangbatna uh thei pa na hi;
China damsak chia, misi hingsak chia,
Thuneihna teng Nang a hi.

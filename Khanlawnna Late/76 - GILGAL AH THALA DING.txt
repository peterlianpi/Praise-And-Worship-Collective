Verse 1
 Canan gam zuan ding in, apai diamdiam lai-un,
 Ai khua sim ding hong kithawi uh;
 Joshua lungkham lua-in, Topa’ tung thungen a,
 Na khutsung teipi zanin Topa’n ci hi.
 

Chorus
 Gilgal ah tha Ila ding a, Nungkiklo misiang te mainawt ni;
 Kha thatawh Ido ding-a, Khris gam zai semsem ding,
 Khris ading I thasaan ciat ni.

Verse 2
 Khris galkap te aw, Hongding khia mengmeng un,
 Topa’n thu hong ciam khinzo a;
 Laukei’n la lungkia ke’n, Galhiam tawh kithawi un,
 Nasiikna ciangciang nagam hongpianing ci.

Verse 3
 Tu’n Joshua teekta a, Akum zong tamlua ta,
 Galsim’ ding tamlu a, Joshua aw;
 Na galsim’ ding tamlua, Namite, tha pia in,
 Canan gam nahawm sakin Topa’n ci hi.

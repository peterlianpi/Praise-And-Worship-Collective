Verse 1
 Topa in kei hongcing Tuucing lianpa hi,
 Ka nunsung bangmah ka kisam kei ding hi;
 Lono namun tuikiangah kei hongkha hi,

Hoihna leh hehpihna in ka khansung,
A tawntungin hongzui taktak ding-a;
A nuam ataang Topa’innpha sungah,

Verse 2

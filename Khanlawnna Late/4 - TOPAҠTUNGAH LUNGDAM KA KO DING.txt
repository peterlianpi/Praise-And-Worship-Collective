Verse 1
 Topa tungah lungdam ka ko ding,
 Tuuno laibu sungӨ kamin kigelhzo;
 Amual theilo amial ngeilo ding,
 Khutmapawn tawh kigelh zo ta.
 

Chorus
 Topa tungah lungdam ka ko ding,
 Phatna biakna kong pia hi Topa;
 Lungdam koh biakna kong paipih hi,
 Saangpen Pa aw, ka lungdam hi.

Verse 2
 Tuidam kiangah Tuuno kikhah bang,
 Neek leh dawn silh leh teen, inn leh lo zong,
 A nisim in zatding sum leh pai,
 Hongsik hongpia ka Topa hi.

Verse 3
 Topa hong hehpihna kaphat ding,
 Diikding huih man ngenlo hong piak manin,
 Nitaang khataang dawnding tuisiangtho,
 A khawnkhongin hong zanghsak hi.

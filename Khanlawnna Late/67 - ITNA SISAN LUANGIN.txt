Verse 1
 Itna sisan luangin, Ka tatsa mite,
 Khuamial gimna sung’ah kahna aw kaza;
 Lainatin ka khi luang, kua sawl ding kahiam,
 Amangthang kha te, kuanpiikhia ding hiam.
 

Chorus
 Topa’n nang hong sam, lainatna aw tawhg,
 Ka nasemding te tawmlua ee;
 Mangthang Kha te’kiang, itnathu gending,
 Nang hong sawl ning ee, nahizo diam ci’ng.

Verse 2
 Na neih nalamh khempeuh, kongpiaksa hi-a,
 Khatzong siitlo-in, lakiktheipa kahi;
 A’ih zong lakik kei ning, naphal bang pia-in,
 Van-ah nong ciahciang nahawmthawh khadiam?

Verse 3
 Natuailai-in semin, tuumbang naguai ding,
 Na neihlai pia-in, beina huh hongtung ding;
 Gal le sa haksa in, buaina’n hong lap ding,
 Septheihlai semin, khua hongmial ding hi.

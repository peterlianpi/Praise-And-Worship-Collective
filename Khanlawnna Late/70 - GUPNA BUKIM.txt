Verse 1
 Gupna bukim hongpia Jehovah Pasian,
 Dikna tawh hongtuam Jehovah Tsikenu;
 Hongompih tawntung, Jehovah Shama,
 Lungnopna Jehovah Shalom.
 

Chorus
 Honghausak hi, kasapna teng hongsik Jehovah Jire,
 Hongdamsa Pasian, Jehovah Rapha;
 Ama’min ading, Thuman lampi-ah hongkha, Jehovah Ra-ah,
 Gualzawhna hong pia ka Topa, Jehovah Nissi.

Verse 2
 Topa’n "hi teh" hongcih leh hipah hi teh,
 Topa’n "nei teh" hongcih leh a nei hi teh;
 Topa’n "bawlthei" hongcih leh hi pah hi,
 Vangliatna khempeuh hong pia zo.

Verse 3
 Topa’n "damteh" hongcih leh adam hi teh,
 Topa’n "hauteh" hongcih leh ahau hi teh;
 Sihding, natding launa pan hong hon hi,
 Singlamteh tung’h hong thuaksak zo.

Verse 1
 Lungnopna zong khin teh nam tampi-in,
 Leitungin tua nopna piathei kei;
 Nasum te koi’h khol nahiam kidawmin,
 Lamhongpi ding khat kisam teitei.
 

Chorus
 Ciampeello, Jesuh sin in,
 Siatna pan itna honglak;
 Nitaangin huihnung taleh,
 Ciampeello, Jesuh sin in.

Verse 2
 Nanite gualnopna tawhkidim-in,
 Lawpin kipahna khuavakbang taang;
 Tuazawhteh dahna khuamial in tuam-a,
 Khuamial vengsakding kisamteh nang.

Verse 3
 Lungzuangin nangkia lamnapai laitak,
 Guallelhna vive tawh kidim-in,
 Lungkia-in, lungsim tamkham dek laitak,
 Tui, huih daisak pa kisam teh nang.

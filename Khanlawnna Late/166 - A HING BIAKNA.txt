Title:166 - A HING BIAKNA



Verse 1
 Toupa hoihna ngaihsun ni,
Hehpihna a thawn in pia;
A hon tel a Amah di’n hon bawlta,
Zahngaihna a hon pia zou.

 A hing biakna i pia ding uh,
 A zahngaihna ngaihsun in;
 A lungtaina’n i pumpi i piak chiang,
 Khalam biakna ahi ding.

A hauhna thupi na e,
A pilna a thuklua hi;
A lunggelna a theizou omlou a,
Tangtawn in a thupi hi.

Lei nunziate taisan ni,
A deihlam i theihna di’n;
I lungsim uh kibawlthakna om in,
Lamdanna i nei ding uh.

Sianthouna toh nungta ni,
Toupa di’n i kipia ding;
I pumpi zong Amah tenna hi a,
Bawlsia in susia kei ni.

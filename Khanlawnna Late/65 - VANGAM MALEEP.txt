Verse 1
 Leitung nuntakna-ah, sapna tawhkidim,
 Neek le dawn haksa, sum zonna zong hamsa;
 Lungkhamna atamman, innkuan kilem nawn lo,
 Innkuan nuamlo zaw, (gawtmun-neu) khat ahi.
 

Chorus
 Vangam maleep, Aw ! vangam maleep,
 Vangam maleep lei-ah zong kiciamkhol thei;
 Jesuh neikim-in, Pasian zahtak ciatleeng,
 Vangam maleep, innkuansung nuampen.

Verse 2
 Innkuansung nuam pan - in hong khangkhia mite,
 Aheina peuhah, maitaiitak omthei hi;
 Pasianin thupha pia, Pasianin hong ompih,
 Innkuansung nuamzaw, (Vangam-neu) khat hi.

Verse 3
 Innkuansung lutang in, Pate ahi-a,
 Pate siamman in, Innsung hong nuam ding hi;
 Zite’n pasal’thu mang leh, tate’n zong thumang ding,
 Tua innkuansung zaw, Topa’n makaih ding hi.

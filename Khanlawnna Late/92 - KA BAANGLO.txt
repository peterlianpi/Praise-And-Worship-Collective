Verse 1
 Leitung ah I nuntakna , lungnopna cih om ngeilo,
 Nisim lungkhamna tawh kei kadim-a;
 Lungkhamna meiipi bang ziing, ziaetna tuihual bang in,
 Lungkhamna te kei tungah baang ngeilo.
 

Chorus
 Ka baanglo, ka baanglo,
 Hih leitung lungkhamna te, kei zong van ah kaleeng ciang,
 Ka khualzinna hih leitung, dahna teng mangpha khak ning.

Verse 2
 Kei hong zui zo nawnlo ding, leitung luangkham buaina te’n,
 Vanah va bangin kei ka leen ni ciang;
 Tualai-ah dahna om lo, it le ngaih tawh kimu ding,
 Lungdamin Topa phatding nuam si’n teh.

Verse 3
 Van gamnuam ah ka ciah ciang, kham kongzing ah hong dawn ding,
 It le ngaih teng lah leengvan kai khin zo;
 Tualai mun ah kei pai ning, lawm aw nang zong nong zui diam,
 Tualai-ah lungzuan khuangaih om nawnlo.

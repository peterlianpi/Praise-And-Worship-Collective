Verse 1
 Ka khualzinna hih leitung, sawtlo ka nusia ding,
 Khuttawh bawlloh kham khuapi, zuanin ka pai ding hi;
 Lei-ah ka gentheihna le, lungzuan khuangaihna te, 
 Kapa kiang ka tun ni ciang, hong vengpah nave.
 

Chorus
 Tua a nopcii gam ah vanmisiangte in,
 Kham gawsem te tumin, phatna lasa hi;
 Halelujah ci-in van lapi sa hi.
 Tuuno sisan phatna la ke’n ka sa ding hi.

Verse 2
 Hih leitung gamsimtham pan, a taang gamnuam sungah,
 Ni le kha aksi kheng in, kei ka leengto ding-a;
 Meii tungpan misiangtho te, Zeisu’n hong piito ding,
 It le ngaih teng tawh kim ding, tua gamnuam sung ah.

Verse 3
 A siangtho vantungmite’n, Pasian’tokhom kiim ah,
 Sun le zan atawntung in, Topa’ min phat uh hi;
 Keizong vanmite bangin, sisan manphatna la,
 Kasak ni ciang to Zeisu’n hong maitaii na ve.

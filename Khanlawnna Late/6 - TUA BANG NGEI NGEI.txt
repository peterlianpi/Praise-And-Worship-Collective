Verse 1
 Tuivot ciimin dangtaak mite alung damsak bangin,
 Bawlsiatna thuak lung tamkham mite;
 Hotkhiatna lampi tawnin hong paikhiatpih tak ciangin,
 Lungdamlua in amang uh bang liang ee.

Verse 2

Verse 1
 Simlei tualmimbang kapianlim, sakmi melzong neilo-in,
 Tun, Aw thei lo Sianmang mel ma in;
 Hihhang Topa thupha tawh, Naubang kei kasenlian a,
 Tunnu’itna, Sianmang itna phawk thei ing.
 Anbang hong it tungSianmang le, mu-bang kongngaih Tun le Zua,
 Sianmang itna laambang paak lai ing;
 Naubang in hongpantah in ah, Biakinn-ah hongkhaden-a,
 Tehpih omlo Sianmang itna phawkthei ing.
 

Chorus
 Hihzah a ngaihsut thamding, Hihzah a nongkepna ding,
 Topa aw nang ading bang hilel ka hiam;
 Hihzah a nong lainatding le, Thupha hizah nongpiakna,
 Ka innkuanpihte in bang hilel ahiam.

Verse 2
 Bangci bang phatlai dingmaw, bangci bang genthei lai ding ,
 Gensiam hileng vanmi te bang in;
 Sa siam thei leng Zionla-in, Topa aw hong phatnuam ing,
 Ka paallai NaMin hong pahtawi sak aw,
 Khatvei niciang kaleinunna, mual hong liamsan ding a,
 Hankhuk sungah sambang zaal hunciang;
 Lungmuangsa le khamuang sa-in, Toopa aw nakhaliim sung,
 Laaktoh niciang Topa aw hong mangngilh ke’n.

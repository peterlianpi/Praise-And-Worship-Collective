Verse 1
 Siatna te’n I nunna hong zo nuam in,
 I kampau-ah hong siah tawntung hi;
 I khialh theih na’ngin, I puk theih na’ngin,
 Hong zol, hong khem, hong tah tawntung hi.
 

Chorus
 Hong bawlsiate samsia kik lo-in,
 Thupha tawh thuk inla, hong bawlsiate ading thungetsak in;
 Siatna le gitlohna te ki zosak peuhmah kei-in la,
 Thuhoih le Thupha tawh zo tawntungin.

Verse 2
 Na galte agilkial le an pia in,
 A dang ataak le zong tui suah in;
 Siatnate siatna tawh thukkik lo-in,
 Thuhoih thupha tawh zo tawntung in.

Verse 3
 Hongsam hi, eite thupha luah dingin,
 Thumaan sang thuhoih len tawntung in;
 Anamtuipen le adeihhuaipen in,
 Dikna puanbangin silh tawntungin.

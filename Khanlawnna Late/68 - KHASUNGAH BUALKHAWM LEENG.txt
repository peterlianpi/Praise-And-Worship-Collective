Verse 1
 Lamdanglua aw lamdanglua sa’ng, liangvai kei adingin,
 Dahna khitui hong nulsiangta, nuamna vive dimta;
 Hong itna telsemsem leeng, nuamna khitui hongluang,
 Leitung hauhna sang manpha zaw, nopna kei a hi ta.
 

Chorus
 Oh ! Jesuh a Khasiangtho hongleeng, Jesuh,
 Innkuansung’h hong om ciang;
 Nu le Pa’n zong la sakhawmta, tate zong hong laam ta,
 I innkuanin lasa khawm leeng, buaina lah omnawnlo.

Verse 2
 Kitot kiselna omnawnlo, nuamna vive dimta,
 Nupa kaal zong hong nuamsemsem, Khasungah bualkhawm leeng;
 Tu le ta te masuanin, biakinn lam paikhawmta,
 Kiteen kum lah hong tamsemsem, ngaihmel lah hoih semsem.
 

Verse 3
 Thangvanlau Khasiangtho hongleeng, Kawlgam hongphongding in,
 Vangliatna dim itna khuttawh, tuailai teng hongmanta;
 Enven ! hialai, khangnote biakinn-ah honglaamta,
 Hunkhatlai-a, amangthangsa itate ahi ve leh!

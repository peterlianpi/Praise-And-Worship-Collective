Verse 1
 Van leh lei piangsak Pasian, Ni leh kha piangsak Pasian,
 Ka nuntakna a hongbawl ka Pasian;
 Ni taang leh guahtui hongpia, tuk ciangin an hongla sak,
 Na vangliatna ka phat hi ka Pasian.
 

Chorus
 Topa aw, ka lungdamhi, nisim nong lungdamsak hi,
 Ka hinlai teng namin kaphatden ding;
 Tumging tawh Nangkongphat ding,
 laamnatawh Nangkongphatding,
 Halelujah namin thang den ta hen.

Verse 2
 Tu ni-in, kei adingin hunhoih hunnuam hong kipia,
 Topa tungӨ lungdamna ka ko ding hi;
 Lungdam koh biakna pia-in, Topa kongpahtawi ding hi,
 Ka hatna teng tawh namin ka phat ding.

Verse 3
 Khat veivei neekding neilo, khat veivei silhding neilo,
 A hihhang TopaҠhoihna kaphawk hi;
 Van-a leeng vasa teleh, gamlak Unok paak te-in,
 Ka nunna nisimin tha hong dimzeel.

Verse 1
 Lamtawntungah kei hong tonpih hi,
 Hongliah hongdal hi;
 Tawntung Pasian kei sung nungta hi,
 Avangliatna tawh kei hong tuam hi,
 Topa lianbel hi,
 Tawntung Kumpi kei sung nungta hi.
 

Chorus
Nuntta hi, Jesuh keisung nungta hi,
 Hansungpan Jesuh thokik hi;
 Kumpitokhom tung, avangkiatna tawh kidim,
 Jesuh liambel tahen.

Verse 2
 Kamawhna khempeuh hong maisakzo,
 Kei hong sawpsiang zo,
 Jesuh liamma hangin kadam zo;
 Zeisu’ zawnkhalna hangin hau zo,
 Giautau ngeinawnlo,
 Deihteng cingkhin Jesu’h hong dim zo.

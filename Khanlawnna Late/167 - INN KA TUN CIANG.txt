Title:167 - INN KA TUN CIANG



Verse 1
 Simlei ah zaw khualtaam lel ka hi a,
Ki sapna ngen hi bukimna omlou;
Lungsiknate leh lungngaihna te in,
Ka lampi a ding in lingbang a hon sun.

Inn ka tun chiang’n van inn nuam ka tun chiang’n,
Leitung lungkhamnate a beita ding;
Gimtak leh bahtak a ka puak van-gikte,
Ka pa inn ka tun chiang in a beita ding.

Simlei ah gou manphate neilou in,
Vuallel tak in simlei ah leng zong leng;
Thangvan ka tun chiang’n ka gou manpha leh,
Vualzohna in tua ah kei hon na ngak hi.

Khatvei leh lungkiatnate’n a hon tuam,
Ka hinna zong bah in a ngui sak zel;
Tuni’n ka nuam zong, zingchiang ka dahna,
Lungnop kisinna kia’n sunni ka tum zel.

Verse 1
 Nisim kanuntakna, Topa tawh kinai ta’ng,
 Lungdamlua ung, Topa kongphatden ding;
 Kakhut kakhe tawh, Topa kongphatden ding,
 Nisimin kongphatden ding ka Kumpipa.|
 

Chorus

 Lungdam ung Topa Nangtung’h lungdamung;

Verse 2
 Nisim sapna khempeuh, Nangkiangah kicingkhin,
 Zawnna ngauna lungkhamna khempeuhte;
 Nasisan-in, nong tuamsakkhin zo a,
 Na Kha le lungnopna tawh nong laihsak zo.

Verse 3
 Hongnuisak hong kapsak, hong laamsak hongdiangsak,

 Asinglamteh kamitkhain pompeuh leng.

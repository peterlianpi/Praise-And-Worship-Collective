Verse 1
 Topa tawh tonkhawmleeng nuamee, buaina kinuaisiah ta,
 Buaina sungah tawntung kigualzo hi;
 Haksatna omlo hilo-a, haksatna kizo thei,
 Topa sungah nisimin nuamkasa.
 

Chorus
 Aw! nuamsing ka Topa sungah, hong hehpihna la saning,
 Hongitna sung tawntung kalungmuang hi;
 Amah tawh tonkhawm leng nuamee, holimkhawm leng nuamzawlai,
 Topa sungah nisimin nuam kasa.

Verse 2
 Topa nangtawh holimkhawm leng, nonghona aw za ing,
 Na kammalte simin lungngaingai leng;
 Mittawh nakimuhthei loh hang, nakilawntheihlohhang,
 Kei nonglawn lam, nongsukkhaklam kathei.

Verse 3
 Laamin diangin Toopa phatin, Khasung kibualhkhawm leeng,
 Topa′n hongompihna honglangsak hi;
 Suakta lo te, suahtakna leh, damlote damna tawh,
 Topa′n hongompihna honglang sak hi.

Title:53 - KEI THO IN CIAH NIN

Verse


Verse 1
 Nathu sung nuamsa nawn keng, nakammal thangah nawn keng,
 Namin tawh ki kholh khopna te cimtaak ing;
 Nong piaksa na thupha tawh, lungkim in hong taisan ta’ng,
 Theihloh kal patpuan bang sia ding hi tangh.
 

Chorus
 Kei thoin ciah ning, Kei thoin ciah ning,
 Ka Pa in ah kei thoin ciah ning;
 Ka Pa’ kianglo ciah nading mundang nei keng,
 Ka Pa’n hong saang zolai buang in teh.
 

Verse 2
 Sep loh ding teng sem kha ing, khoih loh ding teng khoih kha 
 ing Pai loh ding mun-ah, kei pai kha ing;
 khawlh pih lawm gualngaih teng tawh,
 Topa nang hong nial kha ing,
 Hongciah ing, nong saangzo lai diam aw.


Verse 3
 Gualbawl-a, asia hi keng, keima thu a taimang hi’ng,
 Kanahlawh peek lo cih, tu’n thei ta ing;
 Tun le zua melma tang e, Zua sianmang melma tange,
 Ka pal-lai sunni bang, tum ding hi ta,

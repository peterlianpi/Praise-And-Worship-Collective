Title:198 - TOUPA I ZUAN DING

Toupa na tate lai ah kawlni suak sim in,
Huhamna leh siatna in ka gam uh a luah zou;
Ki itte ki laizomte dougal a suak zouta,
Ki itna’n mun a nei nawnta lou.

I thouding aw i zuan ding Toupa i zuan ding uh,
I gam zong Toupa’n a hon it lai;
Banghang a i Toupa mel liahtu i hih lailai,
Kisik in Toupa i zuan ding uh.

Khelh vang in aw sen momnou sanggah a tam ngei,
A it huai a naupangte nangawn in zong a thuak;
A poi in a mulkim huai tulai i nuntakna,
I Toupa a melsiatna ahi

Zuang hong thou un tun leh jua, chin leh tuaite aw,
Kisathei in om kei ni kingaihniam a hunta;
Kisik a i sul hei leh ngaihdamna i tang ding,
A liam ma’n i zuan meng ding uhi.

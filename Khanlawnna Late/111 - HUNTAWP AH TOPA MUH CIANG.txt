Verse 1
 Huntawp ah Topa muhciang, aphatna la I sak ciang,
 Tuani gualzawhna asangpen hi ding;
 Midikte tawh khatvei ciang, Galkhat munnuam I tun ciang,
 Tuani gualzawhna asangpen hi ding.
 

Chorus
Tuani gualzawh na asangpen, asangpen hi ding,
 Tuani gualzawh na asangpen hi ding;
 Kumpipa’inn I tunciang, Hosanna ci’n I sak ciang,
 Tuani gualzawhna asangpen hi ding.

Verse 2
 Misiangte tawh khatvei ciang, kham kongzing I zuihkhop ciang,
 Tuani gualzawhna asangpen hi ding;
 Vanmite tawh khatvei ciang, tawntung la I sakkhop ciang,
 Tuani gualzawhna asangpen pi ding.
 

Verse 3
 Haksatna te hong beiciang, Kimpi lukhu I ngah ciang,
 Tuani gualzawhna asangpen hi ding;
 Atawntung mun I tunciang, Kumpipa’mai I din ciang,
 Tuani gualzawhna asangpen hi ding.

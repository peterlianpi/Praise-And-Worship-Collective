Verse 1
 Ni le kha hunhoih tampi, Satan in hong zolzol hang,
 Leitung lungnopna zongin, siatna namcin in hongtuam;
 Zatamte’n mel hongma ta, pampaihmi kahih lai-in,
 Sianin hambangin honghuai, minthangkhawm veni hongci.
 

Chorus
 Leivui pan honglaamto-a, A khamkhut tawh hong zuut hi,
 Miliante tawh kikhawlsak-a, Ama mite milian in bawl;
 Topa aw nahun acindong, hong ngakzosak in aw,
 Namin than na-dingin nadeihbang hongzang aw.

Verse 2
 Khuakhal huihkhi-i sembang, neih le lam te hong beita,
 Simzawng vakvai kahih lai, zaitha patbang anemlai;
 Thoto-in kata aw ci’n, athahat ka ta hongci,
 Nangmah kong tonpih ding hi, Keima’na semin hong ci,

Verse 3
 Pilna siamna neilo-a, kamsiatna ka thuak lai-tak,
 Hopihna aw khat ka za, pilna kongpia hi hongci;
 Thagum hatna hang hilo, galkap tampi hang hilo,
 Keima Kha zaitha bek tawh, matutna kong pia ding ci.

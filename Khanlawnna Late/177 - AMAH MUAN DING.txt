Title:177 - AMAH MUAN DING

Lungkhamna te’n a hon tuam chiang’n,
Kipahnate a hong bei chiang’n;
Ka zuih siam hilou tuanglam ka zuih lai in,
Ka Toupa in a hon sinsak hi zaw.

Hon pinate ka gel chiang in,
Keimah lemgel in theihsiam a haksa ngei;
Hizong kei a di’n A vualzawlna leh,
Amah muan ding hon sinsak ahi zaw.

Kaan zohlouh a ka ngaihsutte,
Pal zohlouh a ka ngaihsutte;
A muanhuainate ka muhna ding hi zaw,
Amah a di’n a haksa omlou e.

Vuui takmai a ka om in zaw,
Aw a pialpak a lang theilou;
Tua in aw ka lam-etna a bahsak a,
Hizong Aman a pialpak hon musak.

Koimun peuh ah ka om zong in,
Hon pina khut a muanhuai hi;
Keimah hatna zaw muan ding a omlou e,
Amah hatna a muanhuai pen hi zaw.

Title: JESUH KA LUNG SUNGAH HONG LUT PHET IN (TDM - 346)
Since Jesus Came Into My Heart [2Kor. 5:17]

Verse 1
Topa Jesuh ka lung sungah hong lut phetin,
Ka lungsim hong kikhel pah hi!
Topa Jesuh ka lung sungah hong lut phetin,
Ka lung sung khua hong vak pah hi!

Verse 2
Topa Jesuh ka lung sungah hong lut phetin,
Lam ka khial nawnkei pah ding hi!
Topa Jesuh ka lung sungah hong lut phetin,
Ka mawh khempeuh hong sawpsiang hi!
 

Chorus
Topa Jesuh ka lung sungah,
Hong tum hong khawl khitphet inah;
Tuipi kilok bangh ka lung sungah khuavakna,
Thakhatin a hong tang pah hi.

Verse 3
Topa Jesuh ka lung sungah hong lut phetin,
Upna a kip ka ngah pah hi!
Topa Jesuh ka lung sungah hong lut phetin,
Up khialh nateng bei khin pah hi!

Verse 4
Topa Jesuh ka lung sungah hong lut phetin,
Sihna kuam sung khuavak pah hi!
Topa Jesuh ka lung sungah hong lut phetin,
Khuapi kong khak kimu pah hi!

Title: A MANPHATNA GEN THEI HI LENG! (TDM - 6)
O Could I Speak the Matchless Worth!

Verse 1
Hong hon Topa tungah a taang, A lian mahmah tawntung a Vaang;
Phat ding kah to thei leng, Vantung gawsemte tum kawmin;
A lamdang late sasa in, Gabriel dem nuam veng,
Gabriel dem nuam veng.

Verse 2
Mawhna pan hong tatkhiat na'ng in, Jesuh'n A si pha hong bua khin;
Nuamna la ka sa ding, A dikna la'n sa nuam sing maw;
Vantung ki zepna ki cing tawh, Ni bang ka tang den ding,
Ni bang ka tang den ding.

Verse 3
Vanglian tokhom tungah tu a, Itna melpua in a omna;
La in sasa nuam ing, A liatna le a hoih manin;
'A ngaih mahmah late sa in, Thang sak tawntung nuam ing,
Thang sak tawntung nuam ing.

Verse 4
Lungnopna ni hong tun tak ciang, Topa'n hong ciah pih ding A kiang;
Ama mai ka mu ding, Hong hehpihna gualzawhna tawh;
Hong Honpa Jesuh ka lawm tawh, Nuam sa khawm tawntung ning,
Nuamsa khawm tawntung ning.

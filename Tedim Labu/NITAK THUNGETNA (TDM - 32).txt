Title: NITAK THUNGETNA (TDM - 32)
Evening Prayer [Late 141:2]

Verse 1
Topa aw, ko ihmut ma in, Nitak thupha hong pia in;
Mawhna le kisapna ko nei, Nong hon thei, nong damsak thei.

Verse 2
Siatna in hong kimkot hangin, Thaltang tawh hong kap hangin;
Na vantung mite'n hong cing hi, Nong nai ciang ka lau kei hi.

Verse 3
Zan khua a hong mial phat hangin, Nang hong liah lo, khuamial in;
Na mite omna khempeuh ah, Nang gim ngeilo hong Cing pa.

Verse 4
Zan ciangin ko lupna tung ah, Ka si pak kha zong un ah;
A vul ngeilo pakpalh bangin, Van ah zingsang khanglo nung.

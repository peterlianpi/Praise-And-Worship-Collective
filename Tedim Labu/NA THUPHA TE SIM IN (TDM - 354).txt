Title: NA THUPHA TE SIM IN (TDM - 354)
Count your Blessings [Efesa 1:3]

Verse 1
Nuntakna tuipi tungah huih nun ciangin,
Si ding cih ngaihsun in na lung kiat ciangin;
Thupha khat nungah khat na ngaihsun inla,
Topa in tampi hong pia na thei ding hi.

Verse 2
Lungmanna vangik bangin na lel ngei hiam,
Singlamteh hong pua sakin a gik thei hiam;
Thupha khat nungah thupha na ngaihsut leh,
Nisim in a tawntung la na sa ding hi.
 

Chorus
Thupha khat nungah khat ngaihsun in;
A tampi hong pia hi theita in!
Thupha khat nungah khat ngaihsun in;
Pasian in a tampi hong pia thei ta in.

Verse 3
Midang te a sum a pai na et ciangin,
Jesuh kiangah na ngah ding sum ngaihsun in;
Vantung khua sungah na omna inn hizong,
Kuamah in lei zo kei sum a om ding hi.

Verse 4
Tua ahih ciangin gal kido laitakin,
Pasian in na khempeuh uk hi, Lau kei in;
Vantung mihing te in nang kiang ah om hi,
Lampi tawp dongin nang a hong huh ding hi.

Title: HONG ITZAW NING TOPA (TDM - 300)
More Love to Thee, O Christ [Johan 21:15]

Verse 1
Hong it zaw ning, Topa, Hong it zaw ning;
Khukdin a kong thumna, Nang hong ngai ding;
Hih bang hi kong thumna, Khris, hong it zaw ning;
Hong it zaw ning, Hong it zaw ning.

Verse 2
Nidang leitung nopna, Ka lunggulh pen;
Tuin kong deih Nangmah, A hoih pha pen;
Tuin kong deih Nangmah, Khris, hong it zaw ning;
Hong it zaw ning, Hong it zaw ning.

Verse 3
Dahna natna thuak hang, Phamawh sa lo;
Nangma sawltak te bangh, La ka sa zo;
Ka sa khawm amau bangh, Khris, hong it zaw ning;
Hong it zaw ning, Hong it zaw ning.

Verse 4
Ka sih laitak inah, Nang kong phat ding;
Ka hu tawp na inah, Ka vaikhak ding;
Ka ngen lailai hih mah, Khris, hong it zaw ning;
Hong it zaw ning, Hong it zaw ning.

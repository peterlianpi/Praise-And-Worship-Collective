Title: PASIAN IN HONG KEM DING (TDM - 23)
God Will Take Care of You [Late 121:8]

Verse 1
Pasian in hong kem ding patau kei in,
Nitang niliap sung ah nang hong kem in;
A mite hong cing hong kem gige hi,
Nangmah bek hong nusia ngeilo ding hi.

Verse 2
Pasian in hong kem ding a tawntung in,
Lampi hong hilh hi na kalsuan sim in;
Hong cing hong kem hong makaih Topa hi,
Tuidam luanna mun ah hong kha ding hi.
 

Chorus
Pasian in hong kem ding a tawp dong in,
Lamdang Pa le lawm hoih pen Honpa hi;
Na sap sim in Jesuh'n hong dawng ding hi,
Na khempeuh ah muang in hong kem ding hi.

Verse 3
Pasian in hong kem ding nuntak hun sung,
Kua ma neih loh thupha hong pia tawntung;
Hun bei ciang zongh nang hong kem lai ding hi,
Lungnuam takin A gam hong tun ding hi.

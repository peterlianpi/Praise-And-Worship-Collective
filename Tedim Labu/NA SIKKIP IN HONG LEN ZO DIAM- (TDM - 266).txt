Title: NA SIKKIP IN HONG LEN ZO DIAM? (TDM - 266)
Will Your Anchor Hold? [Heb. 6:19]

Verse 1
Na sikkip in hong lenkip zo na diam?
Huihpi hong nun ciang a, muanhuai diam?
Meiivom kai ciangin, tuihual khan ciangin,
Hong len kip zo diam? na sikkip in.

Verse 2
Huihpi nang zo ding kiptak ki khih hi,
Honpa’ khut in hong lungmuang sak hi;
Ama lungtang pan, kei lungsim sungah,
Huihpi nan zawh na’ng hatna ka ngah.
 

Chorus
Nuntakna hong len sikkip i nei,
Tuihual khan ciang zong lau na’ng om kei
A kip Suangpi tung khotak khih in,
Honpa itna sungah thukpi’n phum in.

Verse 3
Lauhuaina sungah hong lenkip ding hi,
Siat theihna hong nai i thei khol hi;
Huih sia hong nungin, tui kilok ta leh,
Tui hual in hong tum’ zo ngei ke’n teh.

Verse 4
Hong lenkip ding hi, Sihna tui hual sung,
Husan bei dek dong huihsia hong nung;
Tuihual a hat hang i ling kei ding hi,
A kimu lo lametna i nei.

Verse 5
Zan khuamial sung pan i galet ciangin,
Tembaw khawl mun tang ki gal muh khin;
Huihpi venna mun, vantung gunpiau ah,
I sikkip khia ni, kip takin ah.

Title: KHRIS LE PAWLPI ADING (TDM - 187)
For Christ and the Church We Stand [Late 61:4]

Verse 1
Khris le A pawlpi ading(ading), Lung kituak in i ding;
Kam le muk tawh phat ni, A thanem te huh ni;
Khe tawh mangthang zong ni, Khris le(Khris le) Pawl ading.

Verse 2
Khris le A pawlpi ading(ading), Thungen in sem den ding;
Lawpna, hanna thak tawh, Na khat peuh sem pelmawh;
Thuciam man zuihna tawh, Khris le (Khris le) Pawl ading.
 

Chorus
Khris le(Khris le) A Pawlpi ading, Lung kituak in(tuak in) i ding;
I nuntakna i kipia ta, Khris le (Khris le) Pawl a'ding.

Verse 3
Khris le A pawlpi ading(ading), 'Hosana' ci'n sa ding;
Hong suakta sak khin a, Zawhna hong ciam man' ah;
I thu bulphuh inah, Khris le(Khris le) Pawl ading.

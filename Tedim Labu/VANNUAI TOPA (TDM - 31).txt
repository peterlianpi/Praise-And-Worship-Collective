Title: VANNUAI TOPA (TDM - 31)
Lord of All Being [Late 19:1]

Verse 1
Vannuai Topa vantung a maang,
Ni, kha, aksi pan Na vang taang;
Na khempeuh te' bulpi Na hi,
Hong it te tawh Na ki nai hi.

Verse 2
Nuntakna Ni, khuavak Na hi,
Khuamial hun in Nang nong taang hi;
Lamet aksi'n hong lungnuam sak,
Zan vil hun sawt ngak zo tuam sak.

Verse 3
Nong maigum ciang, zan khuamial bangh,
Nong maitai ciang, zing khuavak bangh;
Sakhituihup hehpihna lim,
Mawhna sim loh Nang aa hi kim.

Verse 4
A hing tang a hong kipia teng,
Khuavak sung ah kigawm ma teng;
Thuman hong pia'n hong khah dingin,
Hong tawh kuang in, Nang a' dingin.

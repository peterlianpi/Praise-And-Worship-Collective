Title: KA HI BANGH HONG LA IN (TDM - 369)
Take Me as I Am [Late 145:18, 19]

Verse 1
Topa Jesuh ka thu ngai in, Na hong phel kei leh ka si ding;
Kiphelna thu hong pia sukin, Ka hi bangh hong la in.

Verse 2
Phelna mulo mawhna tampi, Ka neih manin Topa a si;
Nong hehpih leh Nong phel thei hi, Ka hi bangh hong la in.
 

Chorus
Ka hi bangh hong la in, Ka hi bangh hong la in;
Nangma sih keima phatna hi, Ka hi bangh hong la in.

Verse 3
Bangmah a pha ka bawl zolo, Ka thuciamna ka sia sak zo;
Na min tawh hong phelsak in aw, Ka hi bangh hong la in.

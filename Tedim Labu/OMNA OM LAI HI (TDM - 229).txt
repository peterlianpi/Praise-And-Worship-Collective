Title: OMNA OM LAI HI (TDM - 229)
Yet There Is Room [Luka 14:22]

Verse 1
 Slow with expression
Omna om lai Tuuno innpi sungah,
A vanglian pi tawh nang hong sam ta hi;
Tum in, tuin, tum in, omna om lai.

Verse 2
Nitang bei dek, ni hong liap zo ta hi,
Hong liap ta hi manlang in ah pai in;
Omna om lai, tum in, tuin tum in.

Verse 3
Mopi pawi ah zin a khop laitak in,
Hong sap mi nahih leh tum in tum in;
Omna om lai, tum in, tuin tum in.

Verse 4
Dim khin dek hi lungdamna inn sungah,
Manlang a na lut leh ngah lai ding hi;
Omna om lai, tum in, tuin tum in.

Verse 5
Omna om lai Kong kihon laitak hi,
Pai in tum le cin na laplai ding hi;
Omna om lai, tum in, tuin tum in.

Verse 6
Tum in tum in tua pawi nang ading hi,
Ki ittawntungna hai ki dawn ding hi;
Tum in, tuin tum in, omna om lai.

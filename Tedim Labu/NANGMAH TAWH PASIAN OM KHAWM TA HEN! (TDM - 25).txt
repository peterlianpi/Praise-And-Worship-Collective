Title: NANGMAH TAWH PASIAN OM KHAWM TA HEN! (TDM - 25)
God Be With You! [Rom 16:20]

Verse 1
Nangmah tawh Pasian om khawm ta hen!
A hong pi, a hong len dingin;
A tuu te hong kem bit dingin,
Nangmah tawh Pasian om khawm ta hen!

Verse 2
Nangmah tawh Pasian om khawm ta hen!
A kha nuai hong liah hong dal in;
Nisim 'Manna' hong vaak ding in,
Nangmah tawh Pasian om khawm ta hen!
 

Chorus
I ki muh(I ki muh) kik ma teng, (kik ma teng)
Jesuh kiang kimuh ma teng;(muh mateng)
I ki muh (i ki muh)kik ma teng,(ma teng)
Nangmah tawh Pasian om khawm ta hen!

Verse 3
Nangmah tawh Pasian om khawm ta hen!
Lau na'n hong lungam sak ciang in;
Ang sung hong pom bikbek dingin,
Nangmah tawh Pasian om khawm ta hen!

Verse 4
Nangmah tawh Pasian om khawm ta hen!
Itna nuai ah hong cing dingin;
Sihna tuihual sat paai dingin,
Nangmah tawh Pasian om khawm ta hen!

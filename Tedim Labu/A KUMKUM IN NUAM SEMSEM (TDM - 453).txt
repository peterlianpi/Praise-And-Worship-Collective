Title: A KUMKUM IN NUAM SEMSEM (TDM - 453)
Sweeter as the Years Go by [Late 65:11-13]

Verse 1
Mawhna sung ka om laiin, Jesuh itna’n hong zong;
A lamdang hehpihna in, Hong khakik tuubuk dong;
A thuk, a sang hehpihna, Tuipi sang’ zongh thuk zaw;
Vantung sangin zongh sang zaw, Ka deih bel hi lo maw.

Verse 2
Tanglai Judea gam sungah, Jesuh vak kawikawi a;
Hotkhiatna thu thei nuam in, Mihon hong pai uh a;
Lung tamkham te dam sak in, Mittaw te vaak sak hi;
A migitna lungsim in, Kei nangawn hong it hi.
 

Chorus
A kumkum in nuam semsem (kumkum in nuam semsem, Tu’n),
A kumkum in nuam semsem (kumkum in nuam semsem);
Jesuh itna lian zaw, Cing zaw, thuk zaw, khum zaw, A kumkum in nuam semsem.

Verse 3
A lamdang hong itna hang’, Supna, baina hong thuak;
Singlamteh tungah gimna, Phun lo, pau loin thuak;
Tatkhiatsa misiang te tawh, I aw sangkhai ve ni;
Leitung, vantung thawn sak in, Ei hong Tanpa phat ni.

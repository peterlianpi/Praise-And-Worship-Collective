Title: HONG PAI MENG IN, EMMANUEL (TDM - 80)
O Come, O Come, Emmanuel [Isai. 7:14]

Verse 1
Hong pai meng in Emmanuel, Honkhia in saltang Israel;
Pasian Tapa kilat ngak mi, Nilkhiat thuak lungzuang a thuum mi;
* Nuam un, nuam un, no Israel, Hong pai ding hi, Emmanuel.

Verse 2
Hong pai meng in Jessi suan aw, Satan khut pan honkhia meng aw;
Hellkhuk sung pan hon khia ta in, Sihna tungah gualzo sakin;
* Nuam un, nuam un, no Israel, Hong pai ding hi, Emmanuel.

Hong pai meng in Zing khua vak aw, Ka lungsim uh hong nuam sak aw;
Zan khuamial pi hawl khia ta in, Meii pailet in khua vaksak in;
* Nuam un, nuam un, no Israel, Hong pai ding hi, Emmanuel.

Verse 3
Davuid tawhtang hong pai meng in, Vantung kong pi hong honsak in;
Vantung lampi ah hong kha aw, Siatna lampi khak tanta aw;
* Nuam un, nuam un, no Israel, Hong pai ding hi, Emmanuel.

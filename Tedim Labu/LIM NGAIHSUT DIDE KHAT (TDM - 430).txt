Title: LIM NGAIHSUT DIDE KHAT (TDM - 430)
One Sweetly Solemn Thought [2 Kor. 4:16-17]

Verse 1
Lim ngaihsut dide khat, Lungsim hong phawng pahpah;
Inn lam ka naih zaw hi, tuni’n, A beisa ni te sang’n.

Verse 2
Omna tampi omna, Ka Pa inn lam naih zaw’ng;
Kumpi tokhom naihzaw’ng, tuni’n, Suang pak tuipi naih zaw’ng.
 

Chorus
Inn tawh naizaw’ng, Inn tawh naizaw’ng,
Inn tawh naizaw’ng tuni’n, tuni’n, A beisa nite sang’.

Verse 3
Vangik te kingakna, Nuntak gamgi naih zaw’ng;
Singkhuam ngak ding nai zaw’ng, tuni’n, Lukhu ngah ding naih zaw’ng.

Verse 4
Lampam ka sik khialh ciang’, Ka kiang ah hong’ nai in;
Inn tawh ka kinai zaw tuni’n, Ka ngaihsut sang’ inah.

Title: KHA PASIAN AW HONG KUM IN (TDM - 342)
God of Spirit, Descend Upon Us [Sawl. 1:5]

Verse 1
Aw a siangtho Kha Pasian aw, Ko kiangah hong kum in;
Hong sawp inla hong siangsak aw, Ko’lungsim hong luah in.

Verse 2
Ci le sa tawh nungta kote, Sia in ninkhin lua ung;
Gamtatna le ngaihsutna te, siatna tawh dim khin ung.

Verse 3
Hanciamna in lungsim luan dan, Kikhel sak zo ngei kei;
Hong piangsak Pasian’ vangliatna’n, A thakin puahkik thei.

Verse 4
Pasian tapa’n nunna pia zo, Nang le kei ading hi;
Sihna pan tho vanah kah zo, Hong kumsuk kik ding hi.

Verse 5
Aw a siangtho Kha Pasian aw, Hong kumsuk mengmengin;
Lungsim thak le ngaihsut siangtho, Ko sungah hong guan in.

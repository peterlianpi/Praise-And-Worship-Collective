Title: PASIAN' KHASIANGTHO HONG KUM SUK IN (TDM - 172)
Spirit of God, Descend Upon My Heart [Johan 1:32, 33]

Verse 1
Pasian' KhaSiangtho, hong kumsuk inla,
Ka lungsim siang semsem ding hong hei in;
Ka zawnkhalna hong hehpih suk inla,
Nang kong it ding bangin hong itsak in.

Verse 2
Kong it ding Nong deih, ka To Pasian in,
Pilna, thatang, kha, lungsim khempeuh tawh;
Nangma singlamteh hong beel theisak in,
Nang mai hong zongin hong mu zo sak aw.

Verse 3
Nong nai tawntung na kei hong phawksak in;
Lungsim sung noplohna lung muanlahna;
Nialnopna lungsim te hong zo sak in,
Hong ngak zo sakin thunget ngah loh na.

Verse 4
Van mite bangh Nang kong it theih na'ng in;
Ka pumpi'n itna siang hong zen sakin;
Na KhaSiangtho sung kei hong phum ta in,
Nong itna ka sung mei bangh lumsak in.

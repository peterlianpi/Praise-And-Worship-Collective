Title: KA NUN SUNG KA TATKHIALHNA (TDM - 256)
The Mistakes of my Life [Mang. 3:8]

Verse 1
Ka nun sung ka tatkhialhna tam zo a,
Ka lungsim mawhna tam zaw sem ta;
Ka kahna in khuamu lopi,
Kong honsa ka va zuan ding hi.

Verse 2
Ama itna lak ah kei a neu pen,
Thungetna ah zong a zawngkhal pen;
Hong sap bangh ke’n ka zuan mawk hi,
Amah in hong nial lo ding hi.
 

Chorus
Ka mawhna ka ginat lohna,
Tampi ka phawk semsem ta;
Ahih hang Jesuh’n hong sap ciang inn sung ah,
Ka tum ding kongpi hon sa.

Verse 3
Ka khialhna te hehpihna’n siin ding hi,
Ka mawhna Amah’n sil khia ding hi;
Meidawi a kip lo kei ma khe,
A tang kongpi lam zuan gige.

Verse 4
Ka nun sung ka tatkhialhna tam zo a,
Mawhna hang ka lungsim gim lua ta;
Ka kahna in khua mu lopi,
Hong Honpa’n hong tum sak ding hi.

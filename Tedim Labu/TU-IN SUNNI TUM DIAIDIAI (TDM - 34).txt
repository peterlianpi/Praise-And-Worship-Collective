Title: TU-IN SUNNI TUM DIAIDIAI (TDM - 34)
Softly Now the Light of Day

Verse 1
Tu in sunni tum diaidiai, Ka mit khua hong mui hiaihiai;
Lungsim pumpi tawlnga ning, Topa tawh omkhawm nuam ing.

Verse 2
Ka sung, ka pua Na mu khin, Hong maisak in a vekpi'n;
Ki langh a ka khialhna le, Simtham a ka mawhna te.

Verse 3
Kei ading a sawt lo in, Sun khuavak hong bei ciangin;
Mawhna dahna peng in ah, Hong la in, Nang ma kiangah.

Verse 4
Nang na neih loh hang, mawhna, Na thei ka ginat lohna;
Tua hi a, To Jesuh aw, Hong hehpih in hong en aw.

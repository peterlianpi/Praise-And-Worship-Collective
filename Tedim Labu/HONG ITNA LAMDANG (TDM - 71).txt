Title: HONG ITNA LAMDANG (TDM - 71)
Wondrous Love [Johan 3:16]

Verse 1
A mawh khin sa leitung mite, Pasian in hongit hi; Hotkhiatna a manpha pite, Man ngen lo hong pia hi.

Verse 2
Upna hangin ka Topa hi, A thokik Pasian' Ta; Sihna tawh kei hong tan zo hi, A si'n hong sawp zo ta.
 

Chorus
A lamdang mahmah itna, Pasian' hong itna hi; Kalvary ah A si ding a, Honpa hong piakna hi.

Verse 3
Itna in hong kicing sak a, Misiangte' tung gen hi; Jesuh upna hang bek inah, Eite i suak ta hi.

Verse 4
A umte aw, nuam in pai un, Vantung nopsakna hi; Hih leitungah na om lai un, Na malep ding uh hi.

Verse 5
Satan vangliatna i zo ta, Tat sa te'n la sa ni; Khris i Topa, i Kumpipa, Eite' gualzawhna hi.

Title: AMAH'N HONG KHA (TDM - 46)
He Leadeth me [Isai. 41:13]

Verse 1
A mah'n hong kha hih thu mahmah, Hehnep na dim thupha a hi;
Ka sepna,ka pai na tengah, Pasian' khut in kei hong kha hi.

Verse 2
Khatvei leh Eden bang nuamna, Khatvei leh khuamial na thukpi;
Bang ka tuak hang muanna launa, A ma khut in kei hong kha hi.
 

Chorus
A mah'n hong kha, A mah'n hong kha, A ma khut tawh A mah'n hong kha;
A'nung mantak ka zui ding hi, A ma khut in kei hong kha hi.

Verse 3
Phun lo,puam lo, lungkim tak in, Topa Na khut ka len nuam hi;
Bang bang tuak zong, bang lungkhamna, Ka Pasian in kei hong kha hi.

Verse 4
Na hehpihna tawh gal zo a, Na sep ka khit ciang a vek pi;
Sih ding lau lo,Pasian in ah, Jordan gun gal ah hong kha hi.

Title: NAUPANGTE TANGKOTE (TDM - 384)
Children May be Heralds

Verse 1
Naupang te in tatkhiatna tangko te hi thei ding a,
Amau te’n Tanpa’ singlamteh thu gen ding uh hi;
Nipi sumpi tawh LaiSiangtho a puak khia ding uh a,
Tuain tuipi gal a mite lungdam sak ding hi.

Verse 2
Naupang te in sep ding nei hi, Topa’ nasepna ah,
Lungkham, dahna te zongh nuamna suaksak ding uh hi;
Dahna in a netcip lungkham a khakham vei mite,
Khuavak bangh migitna kamkhum tawh hopih ding hi.
 

Chorus
Nau pangte in Honpa itna thupha tangko nuam a sa mahmah uh hi;
Naupang te in, mite van inn tun na’ng lungdam takin lamlak ding uh hi.

Verse 3
Naupang te kiphawk hi, Topa’ hong kamciamna sungah,
Hehpihna thupha midang te hawmsawn ding uh hi;
Topa'n amaute limtak kem in lungmuang sak dinga,
Amaute in van ah Pasian mai mu ding uh hi.

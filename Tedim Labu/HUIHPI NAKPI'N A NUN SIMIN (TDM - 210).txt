Title: HUIHPI NAKPI'N A NUN SIMIN (TDM - 210)
From Every Stormy Wind That Blows (Tune: Retreat)

Verse 1
Huihpi nak pi'n a nun' sim-in, Dahna tui bangh A khan sim-in;
Zuat ding tawldam huai, lung muanhuai,
Mun om, hehpihna tokhom nuai.

Verse 2
Ei te i lu tungah Jesuh'n, Lungdam sathau hong nilhna mun;
Mun dang teng sang' nuampen mun om,
Sisan baang hehpihna tokhom.

Verse 3
Khat le khat lungsim ki gawmna, Lawm le gual tawh ki kholh theihna;
Ki khen hang pumkhat bangh hong suah,
Hehpihna tokhom kim ki tuah.

Verse 4
Pumpi' utna le mawhna te'n, Hong nawngkai sak zo nawnlo ding;
Vantung in hong dawntuah suk ta,
Vang taang in tua tokhom tuam ta.

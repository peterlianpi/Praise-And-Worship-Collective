Title: HONG PAI UN (TDM - 221)
Come! [Matt. 11:28]

Verse 1
A zaknop huai penpen thu, thupha khat a om hi,
A lamdang thuciamna thu, kuama theih ngeiloh thu!
Dah ta lehang nuam ta leng, tua thu um ta kei leng;
'Hong pai un,' ci in hong sam, A singlamteh zuan ni.

Verse 2
Banghang in hong it lawmpa, kiang pan na taikhia hiam?
Ama kiang beel mengmeng in, A tawntung in kip in,
Huh ding nei lo in ka om, mawhna sungah ka om;
Amah ka taisan hangin, tuin ka zuankik hi.
 

Chorus
Hong pai un ka kiang, Hong pai un ka kiang,
Lungkham vangik pua te, Ka kiang hong pai un;
Ka kiang hong pai un, Ka kiang hong pai un,
Lungkham vangik pua te, Ka kiang hong pai un.

Verse 3
Ama hong sap mah bangin, A kiang nai semsem ning,
A aw neukha za peuh leng, ka ki nai semsem ding,
Tuipi galkhat mual pang khat, inn tawh ka gamlat hang;
Na khut len in hong zui ning, 'Hong pai in' na cih bangh.

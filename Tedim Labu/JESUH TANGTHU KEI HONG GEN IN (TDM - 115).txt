Title: JESUH TANGTHU KEI HONG GEN IN (TDM - 115)
Tell Me the Story of Jesus Fanny J. Crosby, 1820-
Verse 1915

Verse 1
Jesuh tangthu keihong hilh in, Athu ka lungsung gelhin;
A manpha tangthu hong gen in, Zak ngei nailoh thu khumpen.
A suah lai vantungmi te sak, La ngaih te hong sak dih in;
Sangbelna ah Pasian thang hen, Leitung' kilemna om hen.

Verse 2
Sehnelgam an ngawl in om a, Ahih teng hong gengen in;
Ei mawhna hang ziaetna thuak, Thuak hang azawh teitei na.
A thuak kum sung hong gen dih in, Dahna gimna a puak teng;
Bawlsiat, cil phih athuakna teng, Simmawh nilkhiat a thuakna.
 

Chorus
Jesuh tangthu kei hong hilh in, A thu ka lung sung gelh in;
A manpha pen Jesuh tangthu, Kei hong ciamteh thei sak in.

Verse 3
Singlamteh ah kilh a thuakna, Na sa'n lunggim a omna;
Han sung guang a, a ki cinna, Sihna pan' a hinkikna.
Hong gengen in a tangthu te, Ka muh ngeiloh a siang zaw'n;
Khitui luangin hong kapsak hi, Kei hong tan' tua itna in.

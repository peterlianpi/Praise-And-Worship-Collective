Title: LAISIANGTHO, PASIAN' THUBU (TDM - 193)
Holy Bible, Book Divine [II Tim. 3:16, 17]

Verse 1
LaiSiangtho Pasian thubu,
Ka neih manphapen ahi;
Koipan ka piang hong gen ding,
Bang kahi hiam, hong hilh ding.

Verse 2
Lam ka pial ciang', hong tai ding,
Honpa' itna hong lak ding;
Lam hong hilh ding, hong cing' ding,
Hong thuhilh ding, hong phat ding.

Verse 3
Hih leitung gam simtham ah,
Hong hehnem ding, dahna ah;
Hong hilh hi nei leng upna,
Mi in zongh zo thei, sihna.

Verse 4
Hong hilh hi nopna ngah na'ng,
Langpan' mimawh te thuak na'ng;
Ka neih manphapen ahi,
A siangtho LaiSiangtho bu.

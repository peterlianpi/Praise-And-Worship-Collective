Title: AKSI TAANG A OM DIAM? (TDM - 218)
Will There Be Any Stars? [Dan. 12:3]

Verse 1
Tuni inah tua gam nuam ke'n ka ngaihsun hi,
Nitum ciang inah ka tung ding hi;
Hehpihna tawh Honpa kiang ka din ciang inah,
Ka lu tung aksi tang a om diam?

Verse 2
Topa hatna sungah nasem in thungen ding,
Ka ngah mi bangh kei hong hisak in;
Minthan ni'n aksi taang kei ading hi kha ding,
Amah phatna tuihual bangh om ciang.

SAKIK
Ka lu tung aksi tang, aksi tang a om diam?
Nitak a ni a tum ciang inah;
Tawldamna inn sung ah ka khanlawh ciang inah,
Ka lu tung aksi tang a om diam?

Verse 3
Ama mai ka et ciang bangzah in lungdam ding,
A khephung ah suang manpha koih ding;
Kham khuapi sungah lungdamna hong khangsak ding,
Ka lu tung aksi taang a om diam?

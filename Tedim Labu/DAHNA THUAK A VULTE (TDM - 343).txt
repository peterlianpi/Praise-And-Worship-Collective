Title: DAHNA THUAK A VULTE (TDM - 343)
Come, Ye Disconsolated! [Matt. 11:28]

Verse 1
A dahte hong pai un, Na vulna pan un,
Hehpih tokhom maiah, Hong khukdin un;
Na lung liamna, gimna, Hong pai hong gen un,
* Vantung’ damsak lah, Bangmah omlo hi.

Verse 2
Lungsim hawmte’ nuamna, Lamkhial te’ khuavak,
Vul ngeilo, mual ngeilo, Lawpna hong lak;
KhaSiangtho Huhpa in, Hong nem hi nuamtak,
* Vantung damsak lah, Bangmah omlo hi.

Verse 3
En un, Hinna Anlum, En un, Hinna tui,
Pasian’ tokhom mai pan’, Hong luangsuk tui;
Hong paiun, hihlai ah, Itna an kilui,
* Vantung damsak lah, Bangmah omlo hi.

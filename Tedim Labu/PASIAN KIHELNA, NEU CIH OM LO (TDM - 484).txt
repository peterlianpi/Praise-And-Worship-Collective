Title: PASIAN KIHELNA, NEU CIH OM LO (TDM - 484)
Little Is Much, When God Is In It [John 6:9]

Verse 1
Lo sungah an te min khin zo, Mikim sep theih ding om a;
Ngai dih ve, Pasian in hong sam, tua an te a la dingin.

Verse 2
Topa in nang hong koihna mun, neu in kuama’n theih loh hang’;
Hong koihpa hangin thupi hi, Ama’n hong mangngilh ngeilo.
 

Chorus
Pasian kihelna neu cih om lo, minthanna hauhna zong ke’n;
Jesuh min suang a nasep leh Kham lukhu na ngah ding hi.

Verse 3
Na pumpi hong zawngkhal ta dia, sep zawhloh hun hong tun ciang’;
Thungetna tawh Topa ading, gal na sim’ thei lai veve.

Verse 4
Leitung haksatna te nusia’n, I nasep i zawh khit teh;
Citak a nasem pa tungah, Pha hi ta’ hong ciah ta’n ci.

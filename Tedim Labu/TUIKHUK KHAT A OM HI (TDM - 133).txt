Title: TUIKHUK KHAT A OM HI (TDM - 133)
There is a Fountain

Verse 1
Sihkhuk a dim in kihong a, Sisan tui bangh luang hi;
Tua si tawh kiphum mawhnei te, Mawhna tawh kipelh hi.
Mawhna tawh ki pelh hi, Mawhna tawh kipelh hi;
Tuasi tawh kiphum mawhnei te, Mawhna tawh kipelh hi.

Verse 2
A si mimawh pa'n tua sihkhuk, A muh ciang a nuam a;
Hih mi bangh ka gitloh hangin, Mawhna kiphel thei ta.
Mawhna kiphel thei ta, Mawhna kiphel thei ta;
Hih mi bangh kagitloh hangin, Mawhna kiphel thei ta.

Verse 3
A liamma ah hong luang si pha, Upna ka muh man in;
Hong phel nading itna la pha, Ka sih mateng sa ning.
Ka sih mateng sa ning, Ka sih mateng sa ning;
Hong phel nading itna la pha, Ka sih mateng sa ning.

Verse 4
Nung ciang hih agik i pum teng, Sihhan sung om lai in;
Topa hong phel lungdamna teng, Vantung ah la sa ning.
Vantung ah la sa ning, Van tungah la sa ning;
Topa hong phel lungdamna teng, Vantung ah la sa ning.

Verse 5
It huai Tuuno Na si manpha, Bei hun nei ngeilo ding;
Gupna ngah mi teng kikhawm ding, Mawh ngei nawnlo ding hi.
Mawh ngei nawnlo ding hi, Mawh ngei nawnlo ding hi;
Gupna ngah mi teng kikhawm ding, Mawh ngei nawnlo ding hi.

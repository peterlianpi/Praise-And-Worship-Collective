Title: AW MI SANG TAMPI'N ZAI SA DING (TDM - 152)
O for a Thousand Tongues! [Late 9:1]

Verse 1
Aw misang tampi'n zai sa ding,
Hong taang vanglian phat ni,
Hong taang vanglian phat ni;
Ka Pasian' vanglianbel na, le,
Hong hehpihna phat ni,
Hong hehpihna phat ni, Hong hehpihna phat ni.

Verse 2
Aw ka limbelh keimah hong kha'n,
Tang bangh damna hong piang,
Tang bangh damna hong piang;
Hih leitung mi khempeuh tungah,
Na thupha dim ta hen,
Na thupha dim ta hen, Na thupha dim ta hen.

Verse 3
Aw Jesuh aw Nangmah siang bel,
Dam na'ng le lungdamna,
Dam na'ng le lungdamna;
Mawhnei te'n zongh hong theih zawh na'ng,
Ka muan lianbel na hi,
Ka muan lianbel na hi, Ka muan lianbel na hi.

Verse 4
Mawhna khempeuh hong phel zo hi,
Mi vaitham zongh gum khia,
Mi vaitham zongh gumkhia;
A si phabel hong luang sak hi,
Eite hong itna hi,
Eite hong itna hi, Eite hong itna hi.

Verse 5
Bengngong te aw khuaza un la,
Pau theilo te'n phat un,
Pau theilo te'n phat un;
Mittaw te'n zongh En un, Topa,
Khelbai te diangkhia un,
Khelbai te diangkhia un, Khelbai te diangkhia un,

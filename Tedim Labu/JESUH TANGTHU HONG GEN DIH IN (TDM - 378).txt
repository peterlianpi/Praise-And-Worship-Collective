Title: JESUH TANGTHU HONG GEN DIH IN (TDM - 378)
Tell Me the Stories of Jesus [Luke 7:22]

Verse 1
Jesuh tangthu hong gen dih in, ke’n za nuam ing;
Hihlai ah hong om hi leh, bang ka gen ding hiam,
Lam gei a muh teng, tuipi tangthu,
Jesuh’ tangthu te Kei hong hilh in.

Verse 2
Naupang te tawh a kikholh na, ke’n mu nuam ing;
Tuaciang, Ama thupha te ke’n zongh ngah nuam ing,
Gitna kam bek dim hehpihna dim,
Itna khuavak taang Jesuh maitang.

Verse 3
Khuapi sungah ka zui ding hi, Naupang hon tawh;
Dial lek in, nisuh hiang tawi in Khut tawh lam in,
“Hosana”ci in Jesuh Kumpi!
Amah phatna la nakpi’n sa ning’.

Title: A TANG TAWNTUNGNA MUN (TDM - 431)
The Bright for Evermore [Mang. 21:22-23]

Verse 1
Tualai gam om ni tum loh na, Van ah ni tang tawntung;
Tualai nitak om ngeilo hi, Topa vang in tang hi.

Verse 2
Tualai mun om kilemna gam, Gimna om nawnlohna;
Tualai huihdam zongh nung hiauhiau, I tun na’ng tuamun ah.
 

Chorus
(Singlamteh) e’n (i puak leh), (Kham lukhu(te)) (i khu ding’);
(Mihoih te) (sung i ten leh), Khuavak sung i om den ding’.

Verse 3
Tualai inn om, minthang inn om, A kigen van inn nuam;
I tun nading vantung inn nuam, Ei hong’ dawn ding’ tua lai.

Verse 4
I pum luina i nusiat leh, Manlang in hong’ kheng ding’,
A tang van zin pa kiang zuan leng, I lung nuam tawntung ding’.

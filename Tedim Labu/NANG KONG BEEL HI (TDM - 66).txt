Title: NANG KONG BEEL HI (TDM - 66)
Hiding in Thee [Late 31:2]

Verse 1
Lungsim galdona le dahna sung pan in,
Kei sang' a sang zaw suangpi ka beel nuam hi;
Mawhlua, gimlua zo, Nang aa hong hi ta ning,
A kip Suangpi aw, nangmah kong bee ta hi.

Verse 2
Sun in gamdai a, dah in lunglen laitak,
Ziaetna in a vaang tawh hong tuam ciangin;
Nuntak huihpi nung, tuipi ki hual laitak,
Thupha Suangpi aw, hong liah in bit takin.
 

Chorus
Nang kong beel hi, Nang kong beel hi;
Nangmah kong beel hi, a kip thupha Suangpi.

Verse 3
Galdo laitak in gal in hong neh ciang in,
Tai in kong ko' zel hi, ka belh Suangpi aw;
Tuihual bangh ziaet gimna hong tun ciangin,
Na sung kong bu hi, keima kha Suangpi aw.

Title: BANGZAH IN KA LUNGNOP (TDM - 188)
How Pleased and Blest Was I
(Crusaders Melody)

Verse 1
Bangzah in ka lungnop, Mi honpi' aw ka za,
Tuni in Topa va zong ni;
Lungnop takin om ni, zion mual ah pai ni,
Kiciam in Topa min phat ni.

Verse 2
Zion mual ah nopna, Hehpihna tawh kidim,
A muanhuai kulhpi tawh kiuum;
Tua mun va beel ciat ni, Ama min phat ding le,
Lungdamna la ngai ding pai ni.

Verse 3
Tua ah David Topa'n, a kumpi tokhom koih,
Hehpihna tawh thumaan khen ding;
Misiang te lungdam ding, Mawhneimi te dah ding,
Ki niamkhiat in lungdam tek un.

Verse 4
Lungnopna hong tung hen, Lungdamna'n hong zui hen,
Mi khempeuh in thupha ngah in;
Topa' nopna saang hen, A pha deihna khang in,
Genzawh loh thupha hong tung hen.

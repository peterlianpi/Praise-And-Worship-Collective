Title: KALVARY LAM TAWNTUNG AH (TDM - 122)
All the Way to Calvary [Luka 23:33]

Verse 1
Kalungsim hong tuam khuamial zing mahmah a,
Hong Honpa thuak dahna lianlua a;
A thupha khuavak in kei hong taan ciangin,
Khuamialna bei in khua hong vak ta.

Verse 2
Mawhnei keimah ling in A mai ka kun hi,
Mawhmai hehpihna ka theihloh pi;
Aw khum tak in hong ci 'Dah peuhmah kei in,
Na mawhna leiba kong loh sak khin.'
 

Chorus
Keia' ding Kalvary lam tawntung ah pai, Keia' ding pai;
kei ading pai, Kei ading Kalvary lamtawn tung ah pai,
Kei'suah tak na'ng si hi.

Verse 3
Honpa' hong lah itna lamdang mahmah a,
Tokhom nusia'n Kalvary zuan ta;
Amah bek in sisan luang in hong tan hi,
Amin pahtawi in thangsak veni.

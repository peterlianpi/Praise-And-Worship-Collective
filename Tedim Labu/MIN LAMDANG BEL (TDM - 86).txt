Title: MIN LAMDANG BEL (TDM - 86)
Oh, Wondrous Name! [Isai. 9:6]

Verse 1
Aw min lamdang kamsangte'n gen, A suah ma pek inah;
Lemna Kumpi mu khol khin uh, Sawtpi lai pek inah.

Verse 2
Aw min vanglian van mite le, Misiang te'n pahtawi hi;
Min khempeuh tunga, min lian bel, Eite' bukna Kulhpi.
 

Chorus
Amin in Lamdang Thulakpa! A vanglian bel Pasian!
Tawntung Pa! Ki lemna Kumpi! tote' Topa Pasian!

Verse 3
Aw min manpha sang khan' unla, Vangliatna teng tang hen;
Hong huhna tawh mawhna zo in, Vantung hong tunpih hen.

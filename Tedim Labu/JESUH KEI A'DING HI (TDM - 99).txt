Title: JESUH KEI A'DING HI (TDM - 99)
Christ For Me [Heb. 13:6]

Verse 1
Topa Pasian longal vanah Muan ding dang omlo hi, Nuntak laiteng ka sak ding la, Jesuh kei a'ding hi.
Topa'n kei tang gimna thuak hi, Asi pha tawh hong sawp zo hi; Lungsim siangtho hong guan' zo hi, Jesuh kei a'ding hi.

Verse 2
Mi an hau te ka eng kei hi, Jesuh kei a'ding hi, Leitung hoihna ka deih kei hi, Jesuh kei a'ding hi.
Leitung hauhna kip lo ding hi, Na khempeuh kisia gawp ding hi; Kei nuamna bei thei lo ding hi, Jesuh kei a'ding hi.

Verse 3
Zawng a gimna ka thuak hangin, Jesuh kei a'ding hi, Ka thum noploh Topa'n thei hi, Jesuh kei a'ding hi.
Ka sum khempeuh ka zangh zongin, Leitung sum a sia khin zongin; Ganhing teng a si khin zongin, Jesuh kei a'ding hi.

Verse 4
Galte khut sung ka tung zongin, Jesuh kei a'ding hi, Kisiatna in hong uum hangin, Jesuh kei a'ding hi.
Leitung gal a kido hangin, Ka kha a hong do hang in-ah; Topa min tawh ka zoding hi, Jesuh kei a'ding hi.

Verse 5
Leitung nuntak a bei ciang zong, Jesuh kei a'ding hi, A ang sungah ka khawl ciang zong, Jesuh kei a'ding hi.
Gentheihna teng ka nusiat ciang, Ka hatna teng a bei khitciang; Sasa lai ning, sihna khenciang, Jesuh kei a'ding hi.

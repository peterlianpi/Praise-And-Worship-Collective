Title: GALDONA AWNG UN (TDM - 295)
Sound the Battle Cry [Mang. 2:10]

Vigorously

Verse 1
Gal dona awng un, Gal nai zo en un!
Topa dial khai un Sang takin;
Galdal silh un la, Kiptak ding un la,
LaiSiangtho’ hilhna, Na siksan un.

Verse 2
Eima lam’ mah in Zo ding theikhol in,
Gal te ngapthawh in I kuan hi;
I lum le i dial Ni ah te’ zialzial,
Thuman in thukhual Lel ngeilo hi.
 

Chorus
Khaileh! ding un, dial kiim’ hong kikhawm un,
Limtak, kiging! na kihilh sawn un;
Kuan un! mainawt, Hosana kiko un,
Khris in hong makaih hi galkap hon.

Verse 3
Mi khempeuh Pasian Hehpihna hong pia’n,
Ko a neu a lian Hong huh in;
Gal zawh ciangin ah Nangma maitang ah,
Hong suak nung’ zawhna Lukhu khu in.

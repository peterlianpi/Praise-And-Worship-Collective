Title: I TOPA HONG ITNA LAMDANG HI (TDM - 208)
Grace Greater Than Our Sin [Johan 1:16]

Verse 1
I Topa hong itna lamdang hi, Mawhna khialh na ineih hang tampi;
Lian zaw tham lai hong hehpihna si, Kalvary mual tungah hong bua hi.

Verse 2
Mawhna lungkiatna'n tuikhang bangin, Ka kha hong sut nuam hong to laitak;
Gen zawh loh, lian zaw hehpihna in, Ka belh ding Singlamteh pi hong lak.
 

Chorus
Hong(A lamdang hi) hehpihna, (Hong hehpihna) I mawhna hong maisak, siangsak hi;
Pasian (bei ngeilo hi) itna (Pasian itna), I Mawhna teng sangin lian zaw hi.

Verse 3
Ki seel zo nawn kei i mawhna nin, Bang a om diam hong sawp zo lai ding;
En un, hia a luang sisan gunpi'n, Vuk sang hong ngo zaw sak pah thei ding.

Verse 4
Lamdang kicing hehpihna lian zaw, A um khempeuh A hong pia khong hi;
Amai a mu nuam te khempeuh aw, Sang pak ve un hong hehpihna hi.

Title: ANLATE KAWH OM? (TDM - 216)
Where are the Reapers? [Matt. 13:30]

Verse 1
 Moderato
Mawhna lolai a ahoih teng buh lom,
A kaikhawm ding anla te koiah om;
Nasepna dingin thuman phiaukawi keng,
Kuamah khawl lo ding anlak khit mateng.

Verse 2
Zawi neu zongh zui in zong in phatak pi,
Lopa a sau hang buh om thei ding hi;
Lam lian zongh zui in la nusiat nei ke'n,
Vantung innpi sung hong tum khin uh hen.
 

Chorus
An la te kaw'h om, Kua hong kuan nuam?
Anlak pawi sungah kua hong lungdam khawmnuam?
Kua'n hong khoppih ding, buhlom hoih teng,
Mawhna lo sung a om buhlom teng.

Verse 3
Lo teng khempeuh ah an min khin ta hi,
Leitung khempeuh in lak ding bek ngak hi;
Ahih hang a la ding tawm lak ding tam,
La mengmeng kei leng lah a sia ding tam.

Verse 4
Tua ahih ciang hong pai un phiaukawi tawh,
An pha kaikhawm un, no mihing te aw;
An nei Topa hong pai mateng sem un,
Tuaciang anlak pawi ah lungdam khawm un.

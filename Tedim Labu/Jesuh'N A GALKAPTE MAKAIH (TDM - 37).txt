Title: Jesuh'N A GALKAPTE MAKAIH (TDM - 37)
Jesus Leads His Army [Joshua 5:15]

Verse 1
Ngai un, sapna aw in, Tu ni'n kua kuan ding; Ka thu hong nial lo in, Kua'n ka na sem ding?
Kua'n ka dialkhai tawi ding, Kua'n galdo ding hiam? Thuman Pasian ading, Kua teng kuan ding hiam?

Verse 2
Pasian' hotna muang in, Gal i zawh ma teng; Satan, zawh nading in, Galvan hoih te keng;
A siangtho tupna ah,Thu man in ding ni; Pasian ading in-ah, Khut le khe zangh ni;
 

Chorus
Jesuh'n dialkhai nuai ah, Gal (Gal) do (do)ma kaih hi; (ma kaih hi) Hih leitung gal khempeuh, Thuman tawh zo hi.

Verse 3
Galkap honpi te in, Pai kawm in la sa; Phat ni Kumpipa min, Ei te' makai pa;

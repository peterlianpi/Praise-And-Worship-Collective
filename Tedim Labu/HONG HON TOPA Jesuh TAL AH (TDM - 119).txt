Title: HONG HON TOPA Jesuh TAL AH (TDM - 119)
Majestic Sweetness Sits Enthroned [John. 19:19]

Verse 1
Hong hon Topa Jesuh tal ah, Migitna nuam pha tuang;
A lu tung vangliatna khu a, A muk pan' itna luang,
A muk pan' itna luang.

Verse 2
Leitung mi tehpih ding om lo, Amah a pha bel hi;
Vantungmi lakah zongh om lo, Amah kilawm bel hi,
Amah kilawm bel hi.

Verse 3
Gimna sung ka kiat hong mu a, Hon ding hong delh pah hi;
Keia ding singlamteh pua a, Kei dahna kisuan hi,
Kei dahna kisuan hi.

Verse 4
Ama hangin kei ka nungta, Nuamna zongh A hong pia;
Hong gualzo sak sihna tungah, Han sung pan' hong honkhia,
Han sungpan hong honkhia.

Verse 5
Ama omna vantung khuaah, Kei hong tunpih ding hi;
Pasian phatna hong lak ding a, Ka nuamna kim ding hi,
Ka nuamna kim ding hi.

Verse 6
Pasian citna le hong itna, Hibang ka theih nungsang;
Lungsim tul nei leng zongh, Topa, Kong pia khin ding hi Nang',
Kong pia khin ding hi Nang.

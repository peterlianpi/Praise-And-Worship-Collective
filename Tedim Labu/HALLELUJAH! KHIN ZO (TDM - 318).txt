Title: HALLELUJAH! KHIN ZO (TDM - 318)
Hallelujah! ‘Tis Done [Mang. 19:6]

Verse 1
Gupkhiatna ngah ding Pasian in thu hong ciam zo,
Ama Tapa Jesuh min a um khempeuhte.

Verse 2
Tua lampi gamdai in a lauhuai hang’ inah,
Topa Jesuh in kei hong paipih zo ding hi.
 

Chorus
Hallelujah! khin zo, Tapa min ka um zo;
Ama sisan in kei hong gumkhia khin zo ta!
Hallelujah! khin zo; Tapa min ka um zo;
Ama sisan in kei hong gumkhia khinzo ta.

Verse 3
Ka it ka ngaih tampi vantung gamsung tung’ zo,
Lungmuang tak tua gamah hih lapi sa uh hi.

Verse 4
Naupangno te Ama kiangah ding uh ka mu,
Gupkhiatna lasa uh Amah na nui hiuhiau.

Verse 5
Kikhopna sung kumpi te kamsang te ka mu,
Kham kongzing lampi te zui in lasa uh hi.

Verse 6
Nang le kei ading in tualai ah la om hi,
A tawntung tualai ah i phat tawntung ding hi.

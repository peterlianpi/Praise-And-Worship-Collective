Title: E’N A HUN LIAN THEI LO (TDM - 498)
We know not the Hour

Verse 1
Topa hong pai ding a hun lian kuamah’n thei lo,
Tua hun hong nai hi, ci in lim te’n hong lak zo;
Hong pai kik ding hi cih kamciamna thupha kho,
E’n ahun lian thei lo.

Verse 2
Hotkhiatna a zong mipil ta ding khuavak hi,
Topa thuhilhna LaiSiangtho sungah om hi;
Hong hilhna thu in hun bei nading hong lak hi,
E’n ahun lian thei lo.
 

Chorus
Hong pai ding kigingsa in om ciat ni,
Hong pai ding Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
Pasian vangliatna tawh Mei tung tuang in hong pai ding,
E’n ahun lian thei lo.

Verse 3
Meivak tangsak in thungen kawm in i ngak ding,
Topa hong pai dong, ngakkawm in na i sem ding;
Na lamdang pian sim hong naina thu phawk sem ding,
E’n ahun lian thei lo.

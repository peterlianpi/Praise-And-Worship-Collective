Title: KUMPIPA KONGBIANG HONG TUNG ZO (TDM - 497)
The Coming King is at the Door [Matt. 24:33]

Verse 1
Kumpipa kong kiangah om hi, Mawhnei te’ tang’ sihna thuak Mi;
Misiangtho te bekmah tu in, Kaikhawm ding hong pai hi.

Verse 2
Hong pai ding naina lak lim te, Kumsim in kicing mengmeng hi;
Vantung zingsang khuavak tawntung, Sawtlo i mu ding hi.
 

Chorus
Kongbiang ah (Kongbiang ah), kongbiang ah (kongbiang ah),
Kongbiang ah, hi, kongbiang hong tung’ zo;
Hong pai ta hi, hong pai ta hi,
Kongbiang nangawn hong tung zo.

Verse 3
Kidona, gimna veng lo ding, Leitung ah nopna zong kei in;
Honpa hong paikik ni ciangin, Mawhna, sihna bei ding.

Verse 4
Van le lei thak i tun ciangin, A tawntung in i teng’ ding hi;
Sihna om nawnlo nuntakna, A tawntung hun zangh ding.

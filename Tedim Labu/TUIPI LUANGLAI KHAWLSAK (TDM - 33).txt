Title: TUIPI LUANGLAI KHAWLSAK (TDM - 33)
He Rolls the Sea Away [Pai. 14:21]

Verse 1
Tanglai Itazila mite, Sal in a om lai tak;
Pasian in lak, A hatna tawh, Tuipi luanglai khawlsak.

Verse 2
Mawhna tuihual in hong tuum a, Khemna'n hong zol lai tak;
Topa in hong lungmuang sak a, Tuipi luang lai khawlsak.
 

Chorus
Tuipi luang lai khawlsak, Tuipi luanglai khawlsak;
Jesuh tawh om peuhleng, Bangmah kihtak nei keng,
Tuipi luanglai khawl sak.

Verse 3
Lungkiatna le launa te in, Ka lampi hong mial sak;
Kei hong kem hi, A itna in, Tuipi luanglai khawlsak.

Verse 4
Topa aw, ka hun bei kuan a, Na gam kong zuat lai tak;
Na khut in kei hong len in la, Tuipi luanglai khawlsak.

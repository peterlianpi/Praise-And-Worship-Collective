Title: KHAICI MANPHA VAWH UN (TDM - 416)
Sowing the Precious Seed [Johan 9:4]

Khaici manpha vawh un, zingsang phalvak kipat tung in,

Khaici manpha vawh un, Dawm in lungduai tak.

Khaici manpha vawh un (vawh un), Khaici manpha vawhun (vawhun),
Gamla nai ah vawh un, lungduai itna khut tawh;

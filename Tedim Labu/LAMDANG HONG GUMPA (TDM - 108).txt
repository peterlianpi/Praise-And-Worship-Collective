Title: LAMDANG HONG GUMPA (TDM - 108)
What a Wonderful Saviour! [Isai. 9:6]

Verse 1
Jesuh in mawhna hong phel zo; Hong gum Topa lamdang hi!
Hong tatna a man pia khin zo; Hong gum Topa lamdang hi!

Verse 2
Pasian tawh hong kilem sak hi; Hong gum Topa lamdang hi!
Hong silsiang si ka phat ding hi; Hong gum Topa lamdang hi!
 

Chorus
Jesuh in hong gumpa hi, A lamdang gumpa hi!
Keima Topa Jesuh, A lamdang gumpa hi.

Verse 3
Lungsim sung mawhna a phel hi; Hong gum Topa lamdang hi!
I lung sungah om a uk hi; Hong gum Topa lamdang hi!

Verse 4
Amah tawh lam ka pai khawm hi; Hong gum Topa lamdang hi!
Nisim in hong kem suak ding hi; Hong gum Topa lamdang hi!

Verse 5
Gualzawhna'ng vangliatna hong pia; Hong hon Topa lamdang hi!
Nisim, naisim zawhna hong pia; Hong hon Topa lamdang hi!

Verse 6
Lungsim khempeuh Amah pia ning; Hong hon Topa lamdang hi!
Leitung in hong' tang' kha lo ding; Hong hon Topa lamdang hi!

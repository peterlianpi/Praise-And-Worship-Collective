Title: ZION KHUA I PAI HI (TDM - 277)
We’re Marehing to Zion [Late 149:2]

Verse 1
Topa it teng pai ni, Langsak ni i nuamna;
Kituak takin la sa khawm ni, Kituak takin la sa khawmni,
Topa tokhom kiim ah, Topa tokhom kiim ah.

Verse 2
Ei’ Pasian thei lo ten, Phat nuam ta kei le uh;
Ei vantung Kumpipa ta te’n, Ei vantung Kumpipa’ ta te’n,
Thangsak un nuamna thu, Thangsak un nuamna thu.
 

Chorus
Zion khua i pai hi, A kilawm khuapha pi Zion;
I tun mateng kah toto ni (kahto ni), Ki lawm Pasian’ khuapi Zion.

Verse 3
Vantung gam sung tum ma, Khamkhua sung sik ma in;
Hong maleep hi siangtho nuamna, Hong maleep hi siangtho nuamna,
Zion mual kah lai in, Zion mual kah lai in.

Verse 4
Tua hi a, la sa un, Nul un dahna khitui;
Vantung gam ah a nopna mun, Vantung gam ah a nopna mun,
Tun nading lam i zui, Tun nading lam i zui.

Title: NIDANG LAI IN DAVID KHUA AH (TDM - 92)
Once in Royal David's City [Luke 2:11]

Verse 1
Nidang lai-in David khua ah, Bawngbuk neu khat a om hi;
Tua bawngbuk sung, bawngkuang sungah, Nu khat in ta a sial hi.
Tua nu min Mazih hi a, A ta min Jesuh Khris.

Verse 2
Jesuh pen Pasian le Topa, Van pan hong kum suk a hi;
Bawngbuk sungah hong giaksuk a, Bawngkuang sungah hong lum hi.
Mi zawngte tawh leitungah, Amah hong kikhawl suk hi.

Verse 3
A neu lai lamdang mahmah a, A nu zahtak in mang hi;
A nu in phatak in kem a, A nu a it mahmah hi.
Tapidaw nau pang te zong, Tua bang mah ding ihi hi.

Verse 4
Etteh ding hoih naupang Jesuh, Eimah bangh ni sim khang hi;
A neu lai athanem Jesuh, Nuam le dah a thei pa hi.
I dah hun ciang hong dah pih, nuam hun ciang hong nuam pih.

Verse 5
Hong itin a hong tat manin, Nung ciangin Amah mu ding;
Ithuai migi tua naupang in, Vantungah i Topa ding.
Ama omna munnuam ah, Ei hong pi ding A kiangah.

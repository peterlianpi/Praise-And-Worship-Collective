Title: KA THEI HI, KEI HONG TANPA HING HI (TDM - 146)
I Know that My Redeemer Liveth [Job 19:25]

Verse 1
Ka thei hi, kei hong Tanpa hing hi,
Leitung ah hong paikik ding hi;
Ka thei hi, nuntakna thak hong pia,
Hehpihna Vaang A khut sung om.

Verse 2
Ka thei hi, hong thuciam pel ngei kei,
A gen sa thu bei ngeilo ding;
A gilo sihna pan ka suakta,
Amah mu ding nung ciangin ah.
 

Chorus

Verse 3
Ka thei hi, ka om na'ng mun bawl khol,
A omna peuh kei zongh om ning;
A lamdang Amah'n kei hong kem hi,
Keia' dingin hong paikik ding.

Title: MANGNGILH KE’N SABBATH NI (TDM - 500)
Don’t Forget the Sabbath [Siampi laibu 23:3]

Verse 1
Sabbath mangngilh kei un, Pasian thupha piaksa,
A pha a nuam bel ni hi, Ni sagih kaal sungah;
Gimna pan’ tawldam hong pia, Lungnopna hong gen hi,
Vantung melhoihna pha tawh, A vaang hong tang suk hi.

Verse 2
Sabbath siangtho sak un, Bia un Amah tu ni’n,
A nungzui te’ kiang ah ci, “Hinna lampi ka hi;”
Hih leitung ah hong Honpa, Kiniamkhiat in zui leng’,
Nuntakna tui hong pia ding, A tawntunga luang den.
 

Chorus
Dawn ni, dawn ni, dawn denden ni, Thupha Sabbath ni;
Dawn ni, dawn ni, dawn denden ni, Thupha Sabbath ni.

Verse 3
A nuam a siangtho ni! Zangh ni a hun manpha,
Lawmpha bel Jesuh’ tungah, Sak ni’ lungdamna la;
Midik hong it Honpa aw, Nangmah Na pha bel hi,
Mi teng tungah teng’ dingin, Na thuciamna pha hi!

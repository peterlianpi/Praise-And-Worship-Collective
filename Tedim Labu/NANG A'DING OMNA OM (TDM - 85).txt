Title: NANG A'DING OMNA OM (TDM - 85)
Room for Thee [Luke 2:7]

Verse 1
Zeisu in vanah to khom nusia a,
Mi a'ding leitung hong paizo;
A hong suak dingin Betlen khua sungah,
A hong suahna ding inn omlo.

Verse 2
Jesuh in a hong kum suk laitakin,
Vantung mite'n pengkul tum hi;
Hih hang gina lo bek tawh hong suak a,
Leitung hong kiniamsak ta hi.
 

Chorus
Keima lungsim sung hong om in,
Nang ading omna a om hi;
Keima lungsim sung hong om ta in,
Nang ading omna a om hi.

Verse 3
Ngia in zong kua nei a vasa in zongh,
Sing dawn ah bu a nei uh hi;
Hih hang Galilee gam Jesuh om lai,
A lupna leitung bek a hi.

Verse 4
Jesuh in zawhna tawh hong pai suk ciang,
Pengkul aw tawh hong pai ding hi;
Amah'n a aw tawh kei mah hong sam ding,
'Ka kiang om in,' hong ci ding hi.

Title: SINGLAMTEH KA KIPAKNA HI (TDM - 135)
In the Cross of Christ I Glory [Galati 6:14]

Verse 1
Singlamteh ka kipakna hi, Hunsia khempeuh zawhna hi;
Siangtho tangthu khempeuh te' vaang, Jesuh' singlamteh kiim tang.

Verse 2
Nuntak dahna'n hong phak ciangin, Lawpna'n hong khem, lau ciangin;
Singlamteh'n hong khem ngeilo hi, Lungnop nuamna hong pia hi.

Verse 3
Lungdamna ni hong taan ciangin, Itna khuavak taan ciangin;
Singlamteh vang taang in ka ni, Hong tangpha sak sem lai hi.

Verse 4
Thusia thupha natna nopna, Singlamteh in siansuah a;
Lungnopna teh zawhloh om hi, Tua nuamna kip tawntung hi.

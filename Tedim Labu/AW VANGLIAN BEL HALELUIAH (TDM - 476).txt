Title: AW VANGLIAN BEL HALELUIAH (TDM - 476)
AW VANGLIAN BEL HALELUIAH

Verse 1
Aw vanglian bel Hallelujah, Kong’ phat uh hi Emmanuel;
Pahtawina le liatna khempeuh, Kumkhua in Nang aa hiden hi.

Verse 2
Leitung mihing namcin vontawi, Vantungmi tawh lung kituak in;
Nong tatna sisan pha hangin, Salem thak tual nuam ah leng khawm.
 

Chorus
Hallelujah, Hallelujah, Amen, (A men)
Hallelujah, Hallelujah, Amen. (A men)

Verse 3
Aw tui bangh thuk gamsing bangh sang, Na pilna le nong hehpihna;
Laukha simlei a ging kilawm, Van le lei kam te’n gen zo lo.

Verse 4
Aw hong hilh in a thuk’ na thu, Gan ankuang le hamsiat tulsing;
Van Kumpi lukhu tawh kizom, Lei Eden le Salem thak tawh.

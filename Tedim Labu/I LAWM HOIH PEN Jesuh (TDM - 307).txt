Title: I LAWM HOIH PEN Jesuh (TDM - 307)
What a Friend we have in Jesus [Pau. 18:24]

Verse 1
I mawhna le i dahna teng, A puading Jesuh i Lawm!
Pasian kiangah i sapna teng, Thum theih na’ng thupha a om.
Lungnopna tampi i taan a, Thuak loh ding pi i thuak hi;
Na khempeuh te Pasian kiangah, Thumin i koh loh hang hi.

Verse 2
Ziaet bawlsiat ahi zongin, Lungkhamna khatpeuh om maw;
Cikmah ciang lungkia ngeilo in, Topa kiangah ko in aw.
Dah teng thuakpih nuam Jesuh bangh, Lawm man dang mu zo ding maw;
Jesuh’n thei khin i sap bangbang, ama kiangah ko in aw.

Verse 3
Lungkhamna vangik bangh lel in, Zawngkhal in na thanem maw;
Manpha Honpa i belh hi in, ama kiang thungen in aw.
Mi’n hong simmawh hong huat ciangin, Jesuh kiang i pai ding hi;
A angsung hong pom hong dal in, Lungnopna hong pia ding hi.

Title: LAM HONG HILH IN, TUNG PASIAN AW (A) (B) (TDM - 24)
Guide Me, O Thou Great Jehovah [Pai. 13:21, 22]

Verse 1
Lam hong hilh in, Tung Pasian aw, Keugaw gam khualzin lai in;
Ka thanem hang, Nang na thahat, Na khut tawh hong len den in.
Van Khomun aw, Ka kham mateng hong vak aw.

Verse 2
Damna tui a hong luan khiatna, Limlang tuinak hong hon in;
Sun in meiikhuam zan in meikhuam, Lam tawntung hong kha sak in.
Hong Honpa aw, Ka lum, ka tei hong hi aw.

Verse 3
Jordan gun gei ka din ciang in, Patauh launa bei sak in;
Tuihual pi hong kantan pih in, Kanaan gam hong lut pih in.
Phatna late, Kong sak tawntung ding uh hi.

Verse 4
Lungkham, dahna le lungzinna, Launa zumna teng bei ta;
Hon pa' makaihna muang peuhleng, Lungkhamna khempeuh bei ta.
Phatna late, Kong sak tawntung ding uh hi.

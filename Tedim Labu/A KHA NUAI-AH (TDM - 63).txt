Title: A KHA NUAI-AH (TDM - 63)
Under His Wings [Late 17:8]

Verse 1
A kha nuai-ah Lungmuang tak in ka om a, Khua mial lua a, huihpi nak nun hangin;
A hong kep ding thei in, Amah ka muang a, Hong tan zo hi, Ama ta ka hi khin.

Verse 2
A kha nuai-ah dah ciang belh ding muanhuai a, Ka lungsim in tawldamna nuam ngah hi;
Leitung ah nopna hong pia thei om lo a, Tualai ah nopna thupha ka ngah hi.
 

Chorus
A kha nuai ah hong itna pan, Kua mah'n hong khen zo lo ding;
A kha nuai ah kei kateng ding a, Lungmuang tak om tawntung ning.

Verse 3
A kha nuai-ah nopna manpha mahmah a, Nuntak haksatna bei dongin beel ning;
Hong liah, hong dal, siatna'n hong su thei lo a, Jesuh sung khawl leng, lungmuang tawntung ning.

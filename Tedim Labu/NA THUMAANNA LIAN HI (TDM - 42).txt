Title: NA THUMAANNA LIAN HI (TDM - 42)
Great Is Thy Faithfulness [Late 89:1-8]

Verse 1
'Na thumaanna lian hi,' ka Pa Pasian aw,
Ki hei kikhel cih hun, Na nei kei hi;
Nong hehpihna ki khel ngeilo bei ngeilo,
Tanglai bang mah Na hi tawntung ding hi.

Verse 2
Ni, kha le aksite, ngeina bang ciat in,
Khuakhal tuk hun te le phalbi, ni pi;
Ngeina bang zui a hunhun apiang te in
Na thumaan, hehpih, itna hong lak hi.
 

Chorus
'Na thumaanna lian hi, Na thumaanna lian hi,'
Zingsang sim in hehpihna thak ka mu;
Ka sapna teng Na khut in hong guan tawntung,
'Na thumaanna lian hi,' Topa, kei tung.

Verse 3
Mawhna maisakna le a kip lungnopna,
Nong ompih lungdamna, lam Nong hilhna;
A nisim Nong kepna, mailam lungmuanna,
Apha tampi te Nangma thupha hi.

Title: KAM A GEN ZAWH HI LO (TDM - 132)
More Than Tongue Can Tell [John. 15:13]

Verse 1
Jesuh in keimah hong itna, Kei mah hong tat nading inah;
Singlamteh gilo hong thuakna, Kam a gen zawh hi lo.

Verse 2
Ka nuntak tawntung na'ng inah, Nakpi dahna A hong thuakna;
Ling ki phan lukhu hong khukna, Kam a gen zawh hi lo.
 

Chorus
Hong itna kam gen zawh hi lo, (gen zawh hi lo)
Hong itna kam gen zawh hi lo; (gen zawh hilo)
Jesuh in keimah hong itna Kam a gen zawh hi lo.

Verse 3
Pasian kiang hong thum sak Topa, Ama sung ka ngah lungnopna;
Ama si hang hong piang thupha, Kam a gen zawh hi lo.

Verse 4
Hong nai ciang kangah lungdamna, Launa ki hel lo tawl damna;
Ama hang lamet na a pha, Kam a gen zawh hi lo.

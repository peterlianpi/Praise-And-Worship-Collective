Title: TOPA HONG CIAH ING (TDM - 243)
Lord, I'm Coming Home [Matt. 11:28]

Verse 1
Pasian kiang pan gamla ka pial, Tuin hong ciah ing;
Mawhna lam sawt lua ka tawn khial, Topa hong ciah ing.

Verse 2
Hunpha tampi mawk bei sak ing, Tuin hong ciah ing;
Tu'n kap in kisik taktak ing, Topa hong ciah ing.
 

Chorus
Hong ciah ing, Hong ciah ing, Vak khia nawn lo ding;
Thupha tawh Nang aa hi ning, Topa hong ciah ing.

Verse 3
Mawhna lampial, cim ta'ng Topa, Tuin hong ciah ing;
Muang ning na itna, le thupha, Topa hong ciah ing.

Verse 4
Ka kha cina, ka lung meima, Tuin hong ciah ing;
Hong dipkik in, ka lawpna tha, Topa hong ciah ing.

Title: HALLELUJAH! HONG HONPA HI (TDM - 462)
Hallelujah! What a Saviour! [Isaiah 53:3]

Verse 1
A hong pai Pasian’ Tapa, Ama min “Dahna Thuakpa,”
Mawhneite hong Honkhiapa, Hallelujah! Hong Honpa hi.

Verse 2
Maizumna a hong Thuakpa, Kei tang’ mawhsakna Thuakpa,
A si tawh kei hong Leipa, Hallelujah! Hong Honpa hi.

Verse 3
Mawhna hang i cimawh a, Amah’n mawhna nei lo a,
Amah tawh hong Lem kik pa, Hallelujah! Hong Honpa hi.

Verse 4
Si ding a hong kikhai pa, “Ki zo zo” ci a Awngpa,
Vantung a Ki pahtawipa, Hallelujah! Hong Honpa hi.

Verse 5
Minthang Kumpi’n hong pai ding, A tatsa te hong la ding,
Hih la thak e’n i sa ding, Hallelujah! Hong Honpa hi.

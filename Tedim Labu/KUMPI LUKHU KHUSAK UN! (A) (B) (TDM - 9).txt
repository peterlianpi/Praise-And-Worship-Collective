Title: KUMPI LUKHU KHUSAK UN! (A) (B) (TDM - 9)
Crown Him! [Late 8:5]

Verse 1
Leitung mihingte aw en un, Dahna thuak Jesuh ahi;
A gal khempeuhte zo gawp zo, Mi khempeuh in bia uh hi.

Verse 2
Van tung a mi siang khempeuh in, Topa Jesuh min phat hi;
Thukhen tokhom tung tusak a, Vantung mite phatsak hi.
 

Chorus
[ Kumpi lukhu, kumpi lukhu, Topa Jesuh khusak un! ] 
Verse 2

Verse 3
Migilo te'n ling tawh ki gan, Lukhu Jesuh khusak hi;
Mi siangtho le vantung mite'n, Tu'n Jesuh a min phat hi.

Verse 4
Ama min phat in ki ko ni, Gal zawh pawi khamna ngai un;
Topa Jesuh in maan ngah hi, Lungdam huai bel hi, en un.

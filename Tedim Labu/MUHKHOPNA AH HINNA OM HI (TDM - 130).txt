Title: MUHKHOPNA AH HINNA OM HI (TDM - 130)
There is Life for a Look [Isai 45:22]

Verse 1
Singlamteh tung kikhai mi na muh ciang inah,
Thakhatthu'n hinna ngah ta un ci;
Tua ahih ciang' mawhnei te'n en un zong un,
Singlamteh a ki that pa en ni.

Verse 2
Mawhna hongphel ding hi kei leh Amah inah,
Bang ding singlamteh thuak nuam ding hiam?
Mawhna pan'n sihna tawh hong tan nuam ding hiam,
A pang pan si bang ding a luang diam?
 

Chorus
En un, hing ta un; A ki khai mi pa en in hinna ngah ta un,
Thakhat in hinna pha ngah ta un.

Verse 3
Kisik na bekin mawhna ki phel zo lo hi,
A si bek in a hong phel zo hi;
A si a hong bua Topa Jesuh kiangah,
Na khialhna khempeuh hong puak in ci.

Verse 4
Hong sapna lunghianna ding om nawnlo ding hi,
'Kicing zo hi,' ci-in hong sam hi;
Lei' a bei ciang' khat vei hong pai lai ding a,
Thu ka hong khen ding hi, a ci hi.

Verse 5
Jesuh in tawntung nuntakna hong piak nung sang,
Thakhatthu'n lungdam in sang pah in;
Hong dik sak Jesuh nungta a hih nungsang,
Na sih nawnloh ding kithei tel in.

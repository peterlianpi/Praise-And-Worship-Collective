Title: A LUNGDAM TE HONG PAI UN (TDM - 458)
Come, Ye Thankful People, Come [Isaiah 9:3]

Verse 1
A lungdam te hongpai un, An lakna la hong sa un;
Phalbi guahsia zuk ma in, An khempeuh ki kaikhawm khin;
I ki sapna khempeuh te, Pasian in hong pia ei te;
Biakinn pi ah hong pai un, An lakna la hong sa un.

Verse 2
Leitung khempeuh Pasian’ lo, Amah phatna’ng ankung po;
Tang le tahum hong pokhawm, Nuamna, dahna hong khang khawm;
Teh neih khit ciang hong gah a, A nungciangin hong tak sa;
An nei Topa in Na lo, Ko nong hah ding kong ngen zo.

Verse 3
Lo ah Topa hong kuan ding, Inn ah an te ciahpih ding;
Tuani ciang i Topa in, Ansi seep ding lo panin;
Vanmi te thupia inah, Tahum te ki khul ding a;
A tak sa teng kaikhawm ding, A tawntungin nuamsa ding.

Verse 4
Hongpai meng in Topa aw, Inn ah hong ciahpih ta aw;
Na mi te hong kaikhawm in, Mawhna dahna pan khah in;
A tawntung a siangtho ding, Nangmah tawh omkhawm den ding;
Vanmi te tawh hong pai aw, Inn ah hong ciahpih ta un.

Title: ITNA SIANGTHO, ITNA KICING (TDM - 131)
Love Divine, All Loves Excelling [I Johan 4:8,16]

Verse 1
Itna siangtho, Itna, kicing, Vantung nuamna, hong tuak suk;
Nong hehpihna tung khup nading, Ko sung hong lam in Na Buk.
Jesuh aw, hehpihna Na dim, Itna Siang kicing Na hi;
Lawp a liing ko khempeuh lungsim, Hotna tawh Nong veh ding hi.

Verse 2
Lung himawh mi' lungsim sungah, Itna Nangma Kha guan in;
Kote khempeuh Nang ma sungah, Tawldamna hong luahsak in.
Ko' mawhnopna lungsim paikhia'n, Alpha le Omega in;
Upna pankhia cing sak Pasian, Ko lungsim hong khahkhia in.

Verse 3
Hong pai in, a hon zo pen Pa, Nang nuntakna hong guan in;
Namin kong pahtawi ding uh a, Na' na ka sem ding uh hi;
Namin kong pahtawi ding uh a, Na' na ka sem ding uh hi;
Na itna sungah kipak ta, Tawlnga lo kong phat ding hi.

Verse 4
Ko nong bawlphat, zo tain la, Kawk baang lo hong siang sak in;
Kicing tak a Nong hotkhiatna, Kim takin hong ngahsak in.
Vangliatna khangto sak tawntung, Vantung kong tun mateng un;
Lungdamna, phatna, itna sung, Na mai kong tun mateng un.

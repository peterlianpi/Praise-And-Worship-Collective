Title: KULHPI MUANHUAI I PASIAN (TDM - 40)
A Mighty Fortress Is Our God [2 Sam. 22:2-6]

Verse 1
Kulhpi muanhuai Pasian hi a, Sia thei lo kidalna hi;
Tui khang bang gimna ah bit a, Sihna ding pan hong hon hi,
Tang lai a i gal pa'n I gimna ding zong saan;
Gi lo in tha hat in, Ki huat na hiam zangh in, A mah a gi lo pen hi.

Verse 2
Ei ma thatang bek i suan leh, I sepna mawkna suak ding;
Pasian teel pa'n hong huh kei leh, Cikmah in i zo kei ding,
Na thei nuam hiam tua Pa? Jesuh Khris hi, tua Pa;
Mipi' Topa Amin, Ki khel lo tawntung in, A mah'n gal A zo ding hi.

Verse 3
Siatna kidim hih leitung in, Bawl sia ding hong toto hi;
I thumanna gual zo ding in, Pasian in deih! lau kei ni,
Khuamial uk pa' hawkna, Kihta in liing ke'n la;
A hehna thuak lai un, A lel hi ta, en un, Kammal khat in zo ding hi.

Verse 4
Tua kammal pen khuamial uk te', Lungdam na ding hi lo hi;
KhaSiangtho le A thupha te, Hong panpih pa hong piak hi,
Neih sa lam sa bei thei, Pumpi zong hong that thei;
Leitung nuntak bei thei, Pasian' thu man kip in, Ama gam kho den ding hi.

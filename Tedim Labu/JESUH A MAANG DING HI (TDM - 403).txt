Title: JESUH A MAANG DING HI (TDM - 403)
Jesus Shall Reign [Late 72:8]

Verse 1
Ni a taanna mun khempeuh ah, Topa Jesuh A maang ding hi;
Leimong khat pan mong khat dongah, Ama gam in a zai ding hi.

Verse 2
Mi namkim le pau namkim in, Hong itna la in sa uh hi;
Naupang te in Amah phat in, Ama min a thangsak ding hi.

Verse 3
Ama tungah thu ki ngen in, Amah ki phat tawntung ding hi;
Zing meihal’ paknamtui bangin, Ama’ min a kizeel ding hi.

Verse 4
Tuaciang’ minam khempeuh te in, Ei Kumpipa phat ding uh hi;
Vantungmi te’n la hong sa in, Lei mi’n “Amen” ci’n dawng ding hi.

Title: TUUCINGTE'N TUUTE A CIN LAITAK (TDM - 90)
While Shepherds Watched their Flocks by Night [Luke 2:8-12]

Verse 1
Tuucing te'n zan lai gam lakah, Tuu vil a om lai tak;
Vantung mite hong pai suk a, A vanglian pi honglak.

Verse 2
Tuucing te lau in vantung mi'n, 'Lau kei un, thupha pi;
Note le leitung mi a'ding, Tuin kong paipih hi.

Verse 3
David khua Betlen khuapi sung, Hong gum dingin Khris;
David suan mihing pawl sungpan, Tuni in hong suak hi.

Verse 4
Bawngkuang sung puan tawh kitun a, Naupangin a om hi;
No tuin pai un zong un la, No na mu ding uh hi.

Verse 5
Vantung mi'n a cih laitakin, Vanmi te hong pai a;
Pasian vanglian mahmah ci-in, Sa uh hih phatna la.

Verse 6
'Vanah Pasian min lian henla, Leitung nopna om hen;
Hong hehpihna le hong itna, Mi tung om tawntung hen.

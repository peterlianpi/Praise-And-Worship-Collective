Title: Khris GALKAP TE'N AH (TDM - 179)
Onward, Christian Soldiers [Josua 6:7; Thkna. 31:6]

Verse 1
Khrisgalkap te'n ah, Galsim ding kuan un;
Eite mai a masa, Jesuh mit suan un;
Khris eite Mangpa, Makai hi lo maw;
En un A dial paina, Galphual lam manawh!

Verse 2
Jesuh min a zak ciang' ,Dawi mite tai uh;
Nawt un, tua ahihciang, Na mualsuah dong uh;
Phatna aw kizak ciang', Dawi gam kiliing hi;
Lawmte, tua ahihciang, Awng un phat na pi.
 

Chorus
Jesuh galkap te'n ah, Galkap ding kuan un;
Singlamteh pua unla, Na mailam nawt un.

Verse 3
Galkap honpi bangin, Pawlpi'n masot hi;
Tang misiang te bangin, I totna khat hi;
Eite kikhen lo in, Pumkhat bek i hi;
Lawp le zuih kikhat in, Hong ki it sak hi.

Verse 4
Kumpi za sia thei hi, Kumpi gam kip kei;
Hih hang Jesuh Pawlpi, Cikmah'n sia thei kei;
Dawi in Pasian' Pawlpi, Cikmah'n zo ngei kei;
Khris hong ciamsa hi, A ciampeel ngei kei.

Verse 5
Tua hi a hong pawm un, Mi khempeuh inah;
Ko tawh hong sa khawm un, Gal zawh nuamna la;
A minthang pahtawi un, Leitung vantung ah;
Tatsatlo in phat un, Khris Kumpi pa.

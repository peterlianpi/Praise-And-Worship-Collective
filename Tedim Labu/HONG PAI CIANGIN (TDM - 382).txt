Title: HONG PAI CIANGIN (TDM - 382)
When He Cometh [Mal. 3:17]

Verse 1
Hong pai ciangin, hong pai ciangin,
Suang manpha khawm dingin;
Ama it te, Ama aa te, A suangmanpha te.

Verse 2
Hong khawm ding hi, hong khawm ding hi,
A gamsung om ding mi;
A siangtho te, a tangpha te, A suangmanpha te.
 

Chorus
Zingsang aksi sol bangin, A tang ciat ding uh hi;
A kumpi lukhu tungah, Tee diamdiam ding hi.

Verse 3
Naupangno te, naupangno te,
Hong Tanpa a it te;
Ama aa hi, Ama it hi, A suang manpha hi.

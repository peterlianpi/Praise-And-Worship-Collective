Title: HAT IN, ZION (TDM - 413)
O Zion, Hoste [Matt. 28:20]

Verse 1
Hatin, Zion, sem meng in na nasep ding,
Miteng tungah Khuavak Pasian hilh in;
Mihing khat zongh khuamial sung a mangthang ding,
Phal hetlo hi mi hing piangsak Pa in.

Verse 2
Enven, mawhna khuamial thong inn mial sungah,
Mi tul tampi kihen in om uh hi;
Khat beek va pai lo amau ko ding inah,
A suahtak na’ng uh Topa si napi.
 

Chorus
Lungdamna thupha, Nopna thupha;
Jesuh, hong tatna Thupha, vagen meng in.

Verse 3
Mi namkim, pau namkim te kiang va gen in,
Amau hinna in Itna Pasian hi;
Kiniamsak in A bawlsa te hon dingin,
Van nunnuam piak nading hong’ si suk hi.

Verse 4
Na ta te sawl in tua thupha gen dingin,
Na sum tawh huh in manlang theih nading;
Na lungsim teng tawh nak pi’n thu ngetsak in,
Na zat khempeuh Jesuh’n hong din’ kik ding.

Title: A LAMDANG TANGTHU LA SA NING (TDM - 98)
I Will Sing the Wondrous Story [Late 89:1]

Verse 1
A lamdang tangthu la saning, Jesuh kei tang ding si hi;
Vantung minthanna nusia a, Sing lamteh sihna thuak hi.

Verse 2
A gammangte Jesuh'n zong hi, Lam a khial tuute zong hi;
Akhut tawh kei mah hong pom hi, Lampi ah hong kha kik hi.
 

Chorus
a lamdang(Alamdang) tangthu lasa (tangthu lasa) ning,
Jesuh si (Jesuh si) hi kei tang ding (hi kei tang ding);
Limlang tui (Limlang tui) khuk gei kikhop (khuk gei kikhop) ciang,
Mi siangtho (Mi siangtho) te tawh sa ning (te tawh sa ning).

Verse 3
Cinate Jesuh'n damsak a, A kipukte zong phong hi;
Mittaw in a launa tam a, Ahihhang hong gum ding hi.

Verse 4
Khuamial kei tungah hong tungta, Dahna lampi ka zui ta;
Kei hong ompih in Topa aw, Hong keem in lam hong hilh ta.

Verse 5
Sihna gun gal kai mateng in, Topa in hong cing ding hi;
I itte tawh kimu dingin, Gun galah hong kha ding hi.

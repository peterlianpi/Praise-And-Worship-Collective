Title: KUMPIPA HONG MAKAI IN (TDM - 180)
Lead On, O King Eternal [Late 5:8]

Verse 1
Kumpipa hong makai in, Kuan ding ni hong tung' zo;
Tu'a kipan gal sungah, Nang' buuk ko inn hi zo;
Kigin sung nong hehpihna'n, Kote hong hatsak a;
Hing tawntung Kumpipa tawh, Galdo la kasa ta.

Verse 2
Kumpipa hong makai in, Mawhna gal bei mateng;
Gal kilemna nuam om a, Sianthona'n zawh mateng;
Thau, tei le nam, khuang ging tawh, Pasian gam piang hi kei;
Ki-it ki hehpihna tawh, Pasian gam hong tung' thei.

Verse 3
Kumpipa'n hong makaih in, Lau lo kong zui uh hi;
Na mai kimuhna peuh ah, Lungdamna khuavak hi;
Na singlamteh ko tungah, Lampai na'ng hong taan aw;
Galzawh lukhu'n hong ngak hi, Hong pi in Pasian aw.

Title: PAWLPI IN BUL KHAT NEI HI (TDM - 191)
The Church's One Foundation [Eph. 5:23]

Verse 1
Pawlpite abul inah, Topa Jesuh hi;
A si le athu inah, Pawlpi hong bawl zo hi;
A zi siangtho piang dingin, Vantung pan hong kum hi;
Hong bua a si'n hong lei a, Hinna dingin hong si.

Verse 2
Mi lakpan hong teelkhia a, Pumkhat hong bangsak hi;
Pasian thu tawh hong suak suk, Amah khat bek om hi;
Jesuh a min siangtho in, Tua min bek phat hi;
Muanna lametna khat bek, Phat na'ng i hahkat hi.

Verse 3
Pawlpi te simmawh uh a Nakpi'n bawlsia uh hi;
Gal te in nawtgawp uh a, Pawlpi'n Suangpi beel hi;
Upna tawh midik te in, Bangtan sawt ding hiam? ci;
Zan bei a khuavak ciangin, Nopna hong tung ding hi.

Verse 4
Haksatna le bawlsiatna, Nakpi in athuak hi;
A bei ngeilo nopsakna, Pawlpi in lamen hi;
Lawpna thak tawh tua hun nuam, Lungdam in a ngak hi;
Gualzo Pawlpi in a nuam, Tawldamna ngah ding hi.

Verse 5
To thum Pasian khat bek tawh, Pawlpi kipumkhat hi;
A gualzo mi siangte tawh, Nuamsa mahmah uh hi;
Tua mi siangthote bangin, Ko nong' hehpih ding hi;
Ka ginatloh uh hangin, Nong' maan' khawm pih ding hi.

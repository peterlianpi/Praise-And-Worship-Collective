Title: MIHINGTE IN LO LAI AH (TDM - 459)
We Plow the Fields and Scatter [Pian. 8:22]

Verse 1
Eite in lo i kho a, Khaici pha i tuh hi;
Pasian in hong etsak a, Tui buak in khangsak hi;
Phalbi daingo kaisak a, Khualum zongh hong pia hi;
Huihnung in ni hong tang a, Guah in nosuaksak hi.

Verse 2
Na khempeuh a piangsak Pa, Amah bek ahi hi;
Lam gei a pak te zem a, Aksi te tangsak hi;
Huih le tui’n A thu mang a, Vasa te an vak hi;
Eite A ta’ i hi a, An hong vak zaw ding hi.
 

Chorus
Kim le pam na hoih te, Pasian hong piaksa hi;
Topa phat un, Topa phat un, Hong it lua hi.

Verse 3
Nongpiak nahoih te hangin, Pa aw, kong phat uh hi;
Lo pawi, an lak hun te in, Nuntak cidamna hi;
Nong itna thuhkik na’ng in, Bangmah dang om lo hi;
Hih hang Nang deih lian pen in, Lungsim niamkhiatna hi.

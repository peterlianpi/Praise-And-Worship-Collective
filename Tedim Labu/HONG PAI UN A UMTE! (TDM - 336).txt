Title: HONG PAI UN A UMTE! (TDM - 336)
Come Believing! [John 6:37]

Verse 1
Honpa’ kiangpan na zaksa thu, Ngai dih in, lungdamna thu;
Hong sapna dawng lo ding na hiam? Topa’ lam na kihei diam?

Verse 2
Hun tampi na beisak zo a, Anlak phat na muzo a;
Phalbi vukte tuikhin napi, Mawhna sung na bual lai hi.
 

Chorus
A umte’n zuan(un), A um te’n zuan(un)
Jesuh zuan in hingta un; (ta un,)
A umte’n zuan(un), A um te’n zuan(un)
Jesuh zuan in hingta un!

Verse 3
Na deih teel hun Jesuh’n ngak hi, Na khentat mengmeng ding hi;
KhaSiangtho in hong zol lai in, Honpa zuan in beel ta in.

Verse 4
Na lungsim Pasian kiang pia in, Khris tatna si muang in;
Vantung a om Jesuh en in! A kammal kip hi, muang in!

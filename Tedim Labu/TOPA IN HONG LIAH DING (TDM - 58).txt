Title: TOPA IN HONG LIAH DING (TDM - 58)
He will Hide Me [Isai. 49:2]

Verse 1
Leitung nuntak haksa lua a,
Launa huih bang hong nung hi;
Topa aw, Nangkong beel nuam a,
Na kiang ah kong tai ding hi.

Verse 2
imna hong thuaksak zel hangin,
Inn nuam hong tut nop man hi;
Heh man hi lo, hong it man in,
Gimna hong thuaksak thei hi.
 

Chorus
Gimna kathuak loh na'ng mun ah, Topa in kei hong kem ding hi;
Pa Pasian in A khut sung ah, Keihong kem tawntungding hi.

Verse 3
Dawi mangpa' pawl i gal te in,
Hong bawlsia nuam semsem hi;
Ahih hangin Pa Pasian in,
Lungnopna suaksak ding hi.

Verse 4
Singlamteh ka puak laitak in,
Dahna huih bang nung thei hi;
Hih hang Jesuh'n kei hong keem in,
Gal hong tuaksak lo ding hi.

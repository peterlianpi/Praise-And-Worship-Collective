Title: JESUH OM NADING MUN NA NEI HIAM (TDM - 230)
Have You Any Room for Jesus? [Mang. 3:20]

Verse 1
Na mawhna tang a thuak Jesuh, Omna ding mun aom hiam?
Kong khak kiu in lut ding hong tu, Mawh nei te'n a ngai na diam?

Verse 2
Na lung sungah na om sak leh, Nuamna hong bawl sak ding hi;
Om na'ng na om sak loh hang in, Na mawhna tang ding si hi.
 

Chorus
Topa Jesuh minthang Kumpi! A om na'ng mun tu'n bawl in!
Na lungsim kongkhak tanzau in! Hong in na lut sak ding hi!

Verse 3
Topa Jesuh in a hun ciang, A thupha tawh hong sam ding;
Tuni'n a thupha sang inla, Zing ciang ngak kei leteh pha.

Verse 4
Om na'ng mun le hun na pia in, A nuam ding ni kheng sak ke'n;
Sawt lo in hong sam pak kha leh, Na lungsim khauhsak kei in.

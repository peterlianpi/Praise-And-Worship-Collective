Title: KONG HONG KIUKIU KUA OM HIAM? (TDM - 220)
Knocking, Knocking, Who is There? [Mang. 3:20]

Verse 1
- with feeling
Hong kiu, hong kiu, kua om hiam?
Hong ngak, hong ngak, nang hong ngak!
- Beating Three Times to the Measure
Khualzin pa in midik pa hi, Amah bangh na mu ngei maw!
Ka lungsim in lamdang sa hi, Kongkhak hon lo ding na hiam?

Verse 2
- with feeling
Hong kiu, hong kiu, hiah om lai,
Hong ngak, hong ngak, na um hiam?
- Beating Three Times to the Measure
Kongkhak hon ding haksa mahmah, Singkung lopa'n tuamcip a;
Khuamial khaugui in kancip hi, Kiimkot teng ah lenkip hi.

Verse 3
- with feeling
Hong kiu, hong kiu, bang om hiam?
Hong ngak, hong ngak, nang hong ngak!
- Beating Three Times to the Measure
A kilhcip khut in hong kiu lai, A Kumpi lukhu nuai pan;
A lungnem le a migit mai, Hong gum Topa'n hong ngak lai.

Title: PAWLPI HANTHOTNA LA (TDM - 181)
The Church's Rallying Song [Siam. 25:9]

Verse 1
Tho un! Tho un! Topa'n eite hong sam zo hi,
Ding un, Ding un! um in Ama thu te;
Pai un, pai un, gen khia un Jubili kum hi,
Eite Topa Jesuh hong itna thuphate.

Verse 2
Ngai un! ngai un! Khuamial sung a si ding mite,
kiko, kiko tuipi kheng in hong ging;
Manlang! manlang! leitung khempeuh ah zelsak un,
A zawng a hau mite omna mun khempeuh ah.
 

Chorus
Sa un, sa un a la, Hong taang ta hi lamhilh zingsol i tungah;
Paipai hi i mailam ah, Lam hong hilh, hong hilh hong gum,
Topa pi! (Topa pi!) Amin, A minthang ki za tawntung hen la
'Hosana, Hosana,' kiko tawntung ni!
A galkap mite'n ngai un, Jesuh Topa bek thei ni,
'Hallelujah,' kiko ni paina tengah.

Verse 3
Pasian Pawlpi'n! Lainatna khut zankhia unla,
huh un! huh un! a cimawhte huh un!
Na khut khak un! Itna maitaina tawh hon in,
Topa' kepna tuuhuang sungah hong tunpih un.

Verse 4
Khuadak khia un! Topa kamciam ni hong nai zo,
phat un! phat un! A lianbel Kumpipa;
Lemna nopna, minthanna le vangliatna hun,
'Hallelujah,' ci'n awng in mun teng thawn sak un.

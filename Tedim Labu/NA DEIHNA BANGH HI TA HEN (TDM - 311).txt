Title: NA DEIHNA BANGH HI TA HEN (TDM - 311)
Thy Will be Done [Matt. 6:10]

Verse 1
Ka Topa aw, Nangma kiangah,Lampi haksa pai khawm laiah;
Hong cisak thei in, “Na deihna Bangh hi tahen!”

Verse 2
Dahna ka thuak, lawmte sita, Hih hang Na thu ngaihsun laiah;
Hihbang gen ding hi’ng, “Na deihna Bangh hi tahen!”
 

Chorus
Nangma deihna, (deihna) Bangh hi tahen! (tahen)
Hong ci sakthei in, “Na deihna Bangh hi tahen!”

Verse 3
Na KhaSiangtho in keima kha, Hong kipsak ding hong pai sakin;
Hihbang gen ding hi’ng, “Na deihna Bangh hi tahen!”

Verse 4
Nisimin hong puahpha inla, Na awi bangh gamta ding inah;
Gen ding hong huh in, “Na deihna Bangh hi tahen!”

Verse 5
Leitungah keima nuntakna, Bei a vantung tun ciang inah;
Hihbang gen ding hi’ng, “Na deihna Bangh hi tahen!”

Title: BUHLOMTE KHAWM IN (TDM - 212)
Bringing In the Sheaves [Late 126:6]

Verse 1
Zingsang tuh ding hi, ki itna khaici tuh in,
Sun a tuh bangh zan in zongh tuh in a ci;
A at hun ngak in la, a khop hun zong ngak in,
Lungdamna tawh buhlom te i khawm ding hi.

Verse 2
Nisa sung ah tuh in, a liap sung zongh tuh in,
Phalbi le tuuk lailung manna omlo pi;
Nasepna zawh ciangin kikhop hun hong tung ding,
Lungdamna tawh buhlom te i khawm ding hi.
 

Chorus
Buhlom te khawm in, buhlom te khawm in,
Lungdamna tawh buhlom te i khawm ding hi;
Buhlom te khawm in, buhlom te khawm in,
Lungdamna tawh buhlom te i khawm ding hi.

Verse 3
Kap kawm in pai in la, Topa ading tuh in,
Kisiatna in a hong dah sak hang a ci;
Kahna khit ciangin ah Topa'n hong sam ding a,
Lungdamna tawh buhlom te i khawm ding hi.

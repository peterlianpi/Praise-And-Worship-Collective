Title: JESUH IN HONG GUM HI (TDM - 406)
Jesus Saves! [Sawl. 16:31]

Verse 1
Lungdamna thupha ka za, “Jesuh in hong gum hi!”
Tua thupha na gen inla, “Jesuh in hong gum hi!”
Gam khempeuh ah pai in la, Mual kal, gungal kan ding a;
Va gen in Topa thupha, “Jesuh in hong gum hi.”

Verse 2
Gunpi kan in kiko in, “Jesuh in hong gum hi!”
Gamla nai mi kiang gen in, “Jesuh in hong gum hi!”
Tuikulh pan in kiko in, A galkhat na thawnsak in;
Leitungah lungdamna in, “Jesuh in hong gum hi.”

Verse 3
Gal kapna sung kiko in, “Jesuh in hong gum hi!”
Singlamteh tung sihna in, “Jesuh in hong gum hi!”
Hehpihna i deih ciangin, Tua thu te na ngaihsun in;
Sihna zawh ma'n la sa in, “Jesuh in hong gum hi.”

Verse 4
Leitung ah na kiko in, “Jesuh in hong gum hi!”
Mi khempeuh lungdam sak in, “Jesuh in hong gum hi!”
Mualtung le kuamthuk sungah, Hong phelna thugen inla;
Hih zawhna thu lasakna, “Jesuh in hong gum hi.”

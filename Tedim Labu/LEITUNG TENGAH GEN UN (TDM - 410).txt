Title: LEITUNG TENGAH GEN UN (TDM - 410)
Tell the Whole Wide World [I Tim. 1:15]

Verse 1
Leitung teng Jesuh thu gen un, Gungal khat pan gal khat ah;
Jesuh thu mawhnei te gen un, A khuavak pha tangsak un.

Verse 2
Lungdam Thu’n gamlapi zeel hen, Khuamialna mun gam khempeuh;
Lungdamna khuavak theisak un, Thangsak un khuamial gam ah.
 

Chorus
Thangsak un(Thangsak un), leitung tengah,
Lungdam Thu (gun gal khat pan gal) gungal khatah;
Leitung teng Jesuh thu gen un, A tawntung Amin phat un.

Verse 3
Tuipi tung le mualtung tengah, Lungdamna Thu tangsak un;
Mun khempeuh Jesuh Thugen un, Hong sapna Thu thangsak un.

Verse 4
Mi khempeuh a’ding thungen in, Lungdamna tawh huh ciat un;
I gam a’ding zong thungen un, Jesuh ei gam a’ding in.

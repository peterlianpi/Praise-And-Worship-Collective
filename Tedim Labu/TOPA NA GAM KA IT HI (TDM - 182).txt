Title: TOPA NA GAM KA IT HI (TDM - 182)
I Love Thy Kingdom, Lord
(Tune: St. Thomas)

Verse 1
Topa na gam it ing, Gamlapi aa na inn;
Thupha a piaksa A Pawlpi, Ama si tawh leizo.

Verse 2
Na Pawlpi ka it hi, Na ding aa kulhpi mai;
Hawmthawhna le itna mit tawh, En in, khut tawh hong len.

Verse 3
Pawlpi ka it manin, Luangsak luankhi tui bangh;
Keem in, cing in, A na sem ning, Leitung bei ni mateng.

Verse 4
Ka lungdam theih zahin, Vantung ka tun ciangin;
Amah tawh ne khawm nuamsa in, Hong itna kaphat ding.

Verse 5
Na thumanna bangin, Zion mual ah na sang ding;
Leitung minthanna te bei in, Nuamzaw vantung thupha.

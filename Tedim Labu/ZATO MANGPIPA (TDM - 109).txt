Title: ZATO MANGPIPA (TDM - 109)
The Great Physician [Matt. 28:20]

Verse 1
A siam bel hong damsak thei Pa, Hong it Topa Jesuh hi;
Dahna hong nopsak na'ng inah, A hong sapna aw ngai in.

Verse 2
A hing kik Tuuno vanglian Pa, Jesuh ke'n ka hong um hi;
Hong gum Topa Na min a pha, A lian bel kaci ding hi.
 

Chorus
Vantung mi a lasak na, Leitungmi pha bel cihna;
Sa ni a ngaih mahmah la, Jesuh pha bel min hi.

Verse 3
Jesuh maitang mu ding inah, Vantung ka paitoh ciangin;
Ahong itna tokhom kiangah, Phatna lasa tawntung ning.

Verse 4
Khialhna, launa teng a bei sak, Topa Jesuh min bek hi;
Kazak ciang' ka lung hong nuamsak, A manpha Jesuh min hi.

Verse 5
Na mawhna a hong phel Topa, A hong sapna aw ngai in;
Vantung lam nuamtak zui inla, Jesuh tawh zawhna ngah in.

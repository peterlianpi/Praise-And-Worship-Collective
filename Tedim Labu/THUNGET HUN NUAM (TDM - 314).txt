Title: THUNGET HUN NUAM (TDM - 314)
Sweet Hour of Prayer [Late 55:17]
Slow

Verse 1
Thunget hun nuam, thunget hun nuam:
Lungkhamna pan hongsam khia a,
Ka deih ka sapna a tuamtuam,
Ka gen theihhun ka Pa maiah,
Dahna hong tun ciang’ thungen leng,
Ka lungsim hong nuamtuam saka;
Mawhna’n hong zol ciang’ thungen leng,
Dawimangpa’ thang pan ka suakta.

Verse 2
Thunget hun nuam, thunget hun nuam:
Va lenpih in keima thumna,
Keima kha thupha a pia nuam,
Thuman adik Pasian kiangah,
Keima maiah um in muang in,
Hong pai in, hong cih khit nungsang;
Thunget hun nuam hong tun simin,
Lungkhamna teng Amah suan ta’ng.

Verse 3
Thunget hun nuam, thunget hun nuam:
Kei lungsim hong hehnem lai in,
Pasian hong ciam’ Kanan gamnuam,
Luah ding ka lentoh mateng in,
Hih ka pumpi puan bangh suah a,
Sang dingin tul ngeilo noptuam;
Ka lentoh ciang kha ning mangpha,
Thunget hun nuam thunget hunnuam.

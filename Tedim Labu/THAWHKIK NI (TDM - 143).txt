Title: THAWHKIK NI (TDM - 143)
The Day of Resurrection [Marka 16:6]

Verse 1
I Topa hong thawhkik ni, Lungdam paisan pawipi;
Pasian paisan pawipi in, Sihna pan hinna hi.
Leitung aw, gen khia dih in, Lei pan' vantung dong' ah;
Gualzawh lungdamna la tawh, Jesuh'n hong tunpih ta.

Verse 2
Aman in i muh theih na'ng, I lungsim siangsak ni;
Jesuh'n thawhkikna khuavak, Hong tangsak khin ta hi.
Ama thugen ngai le hang,Tel takin i thei ding;
A kiphatna aw ging te,Tel takin i za ding.

Verse 3
Vantung te lungdam hen la, Leitung in la sa hen;
Leitung le a sunga te'n, Gualzawhna gen uh hen.
Muh theih muh mawh khempeuh in, Aw ki khat in gen hi;
Jesuh thokik khin ta a, Ki lungdam tawntung hi.

Title: JORDAN GUN GEI AH (TDM - 433)
On Jordan’s Stormy Banks [Mang. 15:3]

Verse 1
Jordan gun gei ah ka ding a, A gal lam ka en hi;
Kanaan gam nuam hong kilang a, Ka luah nading gam hi.

Verse 2
Tua gam phabel sungah inn ah, Khuavak tawntung om hi;
Pasian le Jesuh vang in ah, A tang tawntung ding hi.
 

Chorus
Tua gam nuam sungah ei i khawl ding hi (khawl ding hi),
A nop tawntungna gungal khat ah (gungal khat ah);
Nung ciangin Moses le Tuuno lasa ding hi, Jesuh tawh mang tawntung ding hi.

Verse 3
Cikciang’ tua gam ka tung ding hiam, Khuamial omlohna ah;
I Pa maitang lungdamna dim, Cikciang’ ka mu ding hiam.

Verse 4
Ka sin sungah lungdamna ding, Leitung sawtpi om lo;
Jordan gunpi kilok hangin, Lungdam in ka tum zo.

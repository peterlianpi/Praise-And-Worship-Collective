Title: KO TENG THUM NI SUAHNA PANIN (TDM - 93)
We Three Kings of Orient Are [Matt. 2:1-11]

Verse 1
Koteng thum ni suahna pan in, Mual le guam gam lapi kaan in;
Letsong puak in kong pai uh hi, Hih aksi pi zui in.

Verse 2
Kumpi khat suak zo ngel hi a, A Kumpi lukhu ding inah;
Ei tung hong mang denta hen, ci'n, Kongpuak uh, kham manpha.
 

Chorus
Aw lamdang mahmah zan aksi, Kumpi' vang a tang Aksi,
Hong masuan in lam hong hilh in, Khuavak thak phual hong tun in.

Verse 3
Ke'n zong pak namtui kong keng hi, Pasian tawh ki nai na lim hi;
Thungetna le phatna biakna, Van a Pasian puak ni. 

Verse 4
Kei aa pawh paknamtui kha hi, Lungkhamna le thuakna lim hi;
Dah in kap in, sisan luang in, Han vot sung lum ding hi.

Verse 5
Minthanna tawh akidim Pa, Kumpi, Pasian hong kipia Pa;
Hallelujah, Hallelujah, Lei le van in phat ta.

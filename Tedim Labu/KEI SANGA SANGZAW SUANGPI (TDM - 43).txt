Title: KEI SANGA SANGZAW SUANGPI (TDM - 43)
The Rock That Is Higher Than I [Late 61:2]

Verse 1
Khat veivei ciang i nuntakna, Hak sat mahmah hun hong tun ciang;
Lungkham sinkham, dah le kap in, Huihpi' seem bang in hong seem thei.

Verse 2
Khat veivei ni tum thei lo bang, Khat veivei i gim diak bang hi;
Leitung nuntak gimna sung pan, Thupha Suangpi beel leng nuam ding.
 

Chorus
A lian Suangpi ka beel nuam hi,
(beel nuam hi) Kei mah sang-a asang zaw Suangpi;
(khang suangpi) A kip Suangpi ka beel nuam hi,
(beel nuam hi) Kei mah sang-a a sang zaw Suangpi.

Verse 3
Tua Suangpi ke'n ka beel nuam hi, Dahna nopna ka thuak zong in;
Mual sang guam thuk sung ka pai zong, A suk a to ah ka pai zong.

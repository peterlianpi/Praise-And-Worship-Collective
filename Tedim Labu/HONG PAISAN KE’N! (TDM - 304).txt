Title: HONG PAISAN KE’N! (TDM - 304)
Pass Me Not! [Sawl. 2:21]

Verse 1
Hong paisan ke’n migi Topa, Kong thum hong ngai in;
Midang te’ kiang Na baan bangmah, Kei hong kheng kei in.

Verse 2
Hehpihna Na tokhom maiah, Kisik kong khukdin,
Nang hong huh in, ka uplahna, Noptuam hong pia in.
 

Chorus
* Topa, Topa, Kong thum hong ngai in;
* Midangte’ kiang Na baan bangmah, Kei hong kheng kei in.

Verse 3
Nangmah phatna bek kasuang hi, Na mai muh nading;
Na thupha tawh nong phel ding hi, Lungzan dam nading.

Verse 4
Ka nuamna teng bulpi Nangmah, Hinna sang phazaw;
Leitung, vantung omlo kuamah, Nang sang a phazaw.

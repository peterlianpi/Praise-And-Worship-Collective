Title: KEIMAH HONG BUSAK (TDM - 62)
He Hideth my Soul [Isa. 32:2]

Verse 1
Keima Topa Jesuh lamdang Honpa hi, Kei ading lamdang Honpa hi;
Suangpi ki tam sungah ka kha hong liah sak, Nuamna gun pi bang hong mu sak.

Verse 2
Keima Topa Jesuh lamdang Honpa hi, Amah'n pua khia keima van pi;
Amah'n hong len a kei ka liing kei ding hi, A nini in tha hong pia hi.
 

Chorus
Suangpi kitam sung keima kha hong liah sak, Tua suangpi'n gam gawtak liap sak;
A itna sung ah ka nuntakna phum ta, Ama khut in kei hong hum ta, Ama khut in kei hong hum ta.

Verse 3
Ki sim zo lo thu pha hong pia kimat in, Ama hoihna tawh kidim in;
Lungdam in la ka sa Pasian min thang hen, Hih bang Tanpa ka nei hi ke'n.

Verse 4
Ama dikna puanpaak silh a ka thawh ciang, Amah dawn in ka leeng toh ciang;
Ama hong itna le hong hotna kicing, Van ah awn tampi tawh sa ning.

Title: PASIAN TAPA'N GAL DO HI (TDM - 140)
The Son of God Goes Forth to War

Verse 1
Kumpipa lukhu ngah nading, Pasian Tapa'n do ding;
A dialkhaina kilak ding hi, A nung kua in zui hiam?
Dahna a thuak hang' thum loin, Gimna a zo thei mi;
Leitung singlamteh pua te in, A nung a zui uh hi.

Verse 2
Bawlsiat thuak pen nungzui khat in, Han galkhat mu thei hi;
Topa vantung khua ah mu a, 'Hong gum in,' ci'n ngen hi.
Topa sih laitak a galte, A mawhna a phel bangh;
Athat te' ading thungen ta, Ama nung kua'n zui hiam?

Verse 3
A teel'sa sawm le nih tungah, KhaSiangtho hong tung hi;
A mau upna na liat manin, Pasian in a gum hi.
Bawlsiatna tampi thuak ta leh, Sahang kih ta lo hi;
Sihna thuak ding lu asu hi, Amau nung kua'n zui hiam?

Verse 4
Misiangtho te tampi tangval, Nungak le nu le pa;
Puanpaak silh in Jesuh maiah, Lungdamna la hong sa.
Launa, nasepna, gimna in, Vantung lam kah to hi;
Topa, amau nung zuih nading, Komau Nong huh ding hi.

Title: KHASIANGTHO, KHUAVAK NA HI (TDM - 173)
Holy Ghost, with Light Divine [Late 25:5]

Verse 1
KhaSiangtho meivakpi aw, Keima lungsim hong taan aw;
Khuamialna na hawlkhia in, Khuamialna khuavak sak in.

Verse 2
KhaSiangtho vangliatna aw, Kei lungsim hong sawpsiang aw;
Khialhna in hun sawtveipi, Ka lungsung hong ukzo hi.

Verse 3
KhaSiangtho lungdamna aw, Ka lungsim sung nuamsak aw;
Gimna khempeuh hawlkhia in, Dahna lungsim damsak in.

Verse 4
KhaSiangtho a dik mi aw, Ka lungsim sung hong om aw;
Milim khempeuh susia in, Nangmah guakbek hong maang in.

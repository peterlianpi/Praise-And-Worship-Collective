Title: HONG HEHPIHNA LAMDANG (TDM - 55)
Amazing Grace! [I Kor. 15:10]

Verse 1
Hong hehpihna lamdang zen si! Keibang lel hong hotna;
A gammang sa hong mu kik hi, Mittaw tu'n khua mu ta.

Verse 2
Hehpihna'n hong ki dawm sak a, Launa teng bei sak hi;
Ka up cil a kipan inah, Manpha ka sa pah hi.

Verse 3
Launa,gimna, dahna sung pan, Hong pai suak pih zo hi;
Tuciang dong hong zawh pih tawntung, Inn hong tun pih ding hi.

Verse 4
Van ah kum tul kum then om in, Ni bang i taang ding hi;
Pasian phatna acil sangin, Nuam zaw semsem ding hi.

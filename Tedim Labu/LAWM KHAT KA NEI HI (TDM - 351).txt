Title: LAWM KHAT KA NEI HI (TDM - 351)
I’ve found a Friend [Pau. 18:24]

Verse 1
Kei in Lawm hoih khat ka nei a! Ka theihma in hong it hi;
Ki itna khau tawh hong kai a, Amah tawh hong henkhawm hi.
Keima lungsim hong khih khin a, Kuamah in phel zolo hi;
Kei Ama aa, Amah keia’, A tawntung ding hita hi.

Verse 2
Kei in Lawm hoih khat ka nei a! Hong hon dingin hong si hi;
Nuntakna bek hong pia lo a, Amah mah hong kipia hi.
Kei neihsa bangmah omlo a, Ama neihsa hi khin hi;
Lungsim thatang le nuntakna, Ama aa hi tawntung hi.

Verse 3
Kei in Lawm hoih khat ka nei a! Vangliatna khempeuh nei hi;
Ka lampi ah hong cing’ dinga, Innpi ah hong kha ding hi.
Minthanna taang ka gal muh ta, Hong thahat sak kik ding hi;
Tu’n vil ding, sem ding, do ding a, Nungciang ki tawlnga ding hi.

Verse 4
Kei in Lawm hoih khat ka nei a! Migi thuman, lungnem hi;
Vaihawm siampa lamhilh siampa, Thahat galdo siampa hi.
Hong it Topa ka nei khinta, Hong khen zo ding omlo hi;
Sihna’n hong khen zolo ding a, Topa aa ka hita hi.

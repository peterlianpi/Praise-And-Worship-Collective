Title: JESUH KUMPI SUAK HI (TDM - 272)
Enthroned is Jesus now [Mang. 7:9]

Verse 1
Jesuh Kumpi suak a, Van tokhom tung tu hi;
Kumpi lukhu ki khusak a, Misiang te’n pahtawi hi.

Verse 2
Sim zawh loh mi honpi’n, Puanpaak silh tee’ zihziah;
Pahtawina nisuh hiang tawi in, Phatna la sa ziahziah.
 

Chorus
Tat (khiat) sa misiang te tawh, Lung (sim) muang in Honpa tawh,
I lung a kim ding hi, Nung (nung) ciangin!
Nung ciangin! (Tatkhiat sa misiang te tawh)
nung ciangin! (lungsim muang in Honpa tawh)
I lung a kim ding hi, Nung ciangin!

Verse 3
A mau ading Si pa, Tuuno a phat uh hi;
Tuuno’ sisan hang kilemna, Lukhu khu ciat uh hi.

Verse 4
Kha Siangtho hehpih na, Ko ading nong pia hi;
Gualzo misiang te tawh van ah, Nong kipawl khawm sak hi.

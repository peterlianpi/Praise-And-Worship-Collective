Title: THUKZAW, THUKZAW (TDM - 480)
Deeper, Deeper [I Kor. 2:10]

Verse 1
Thukzaw, thukzaw, Jesuh’ itna sungah, Nisim hong pi in;
Sangzaw sangzaw, vantung pilna sangah, Hong kalsuan pih in.

Verse 2
Thukzaw, thukzaw Thupha KhaSiangtho aw, Hong thuk paipih in;
Ka nuntakna Jesuh’ deihna sungah, Phum khit mateng in.
 

Chorus
A thuk (zaw, thukzaw in) zaw, thukzaw in, thukzaw, thukzaw in,
A sang (zaw, sangzaw in) sangzaw nisim in;
Hong khang (sak in Topa) sak in Topa, khangsak in Topa,
Manpha Na kammal sungah.

Verse 3
Thukzaw, thukzaw Haksat thuakkul ta leh, Hong thuk paipih in;
Jesuh itna sung hong zungkha sak in, Hong gahpha sak in.

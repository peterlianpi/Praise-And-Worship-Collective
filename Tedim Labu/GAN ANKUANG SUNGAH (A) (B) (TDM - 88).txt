Title: GAN ANKUANG SUNGAH (A) (B) (TDM - 88)
Away in a Manger [Luke 2:16 ] (1st Tune)

Verse 1
Bawngbuk sungah nauluai cih lah omlo a, I Topa gan kuang sungah hong ki sial ta;
Van pan aksite'n zong hong ensuk kiukiau, I To Jesuh, lo keu tung ihmu ciuciau.

Verse 2
Bawngte hamna aw in naungek phawng zel hi, Hih hang Naungek Jesuh lah kap se lo hi;
Naungek Jesuh aw Nang kong ngaih mah bangin, Van pan hong en in khuavak dong hong kem in.

Verse 3
Ko kiang ah nai tak in hong om pih inla, Ka Topa Jesuh aw, kei hong ngai inla;
Naupangte khempeuh tu'n hong kem, hong cingin, Na kiangah om thei ding in hong bawlpha in.

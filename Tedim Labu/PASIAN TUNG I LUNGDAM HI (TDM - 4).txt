Title: PASIAN TUNG I LUNGDAM HI (TDM - 4)
Now Thank We All Our God [Late 66:7]

Verse 1
Lungsim khut le kam tawh, Pasian tung i lungdam hi,
A bawl lamdang te tawh, Leitung khempeuh lungdam hi.
Nu te' ang pan ki pan, Tuni ciang dong tak in,
Ki gen thei lo itna'n, Hong keem thupha bel hi.

Verse 2
Hong cing, migi Pasian, I khan tawntung hong nai hen,
Lungdam lungnopna siang, Hong ompih sak tawntung hen.
Lungmang laitak inah, Lamman hong tawnsak ding,
Lei pan vantung dong ah, A sia hong peel sak ding.

Verse 3
Pa Pasian le Tapa, A hong ompih KhaSiangtho,
Tun phatna lungdamna, Van sangbel ah i puak to.
Lei le van in tawntung, Biakna a pia uh hi.
Tuma, tulai, tunung, Amah bek Pasian hi.

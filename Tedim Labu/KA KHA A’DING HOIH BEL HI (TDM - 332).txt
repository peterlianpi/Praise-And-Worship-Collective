Title: KA KHA A’DING HOIH BEL HI (TDM - 332)
It is Well with My Soul [Late 55:18]

Verse 1
Gunpi taang lo bangh nuam na’ng nong hilhna in,
Dahna tui bangin hong leet hi;
Topa hong hilhna pha hi ka kha ading,
Hoih bel hi, kha ading, hoih bel hi!

Verse 2
Dawi in hong khem a bawlsiatna hong tun hang’,
Ka up ding a thuciam om hi;
Topa Jesuh in ka thaneem hong en hi,
Hoih bel hi, kha ading, hoih bel hi!
 

Chorus
Hoih bel hi, Hoih bel hi, kha ading! kha ading,
Hoih bel hi, hoih bel hi, kha ading.

Verse 3
Hong it a, a nuam i ngaihsut ciang inah,
Ka mawh ka khialhna sung pan in;
Ka suahtak nading singlamteh tung si hi,
Ka lungsim in ka phat hi Topa.

Verse 4
Topa Jesuh tunung ciang’ hong pai ding hi,
Ka lung khamna teng bei ding hi;
Topa hih lei tungah hong pai ding inah,
Ka KhaSiangtho ading a pha hi.

Verse 5
Topa aw nong pai ding hun kong ngak uh hi,
Han hilo, van hi, ko tupna;
Topa’ aw le van mite pengkul aw hi,
Ka lametna le ka tawldamna.

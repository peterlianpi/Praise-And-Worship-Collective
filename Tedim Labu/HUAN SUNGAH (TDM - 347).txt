Title: HUAN SUNGAH (TDM - 347)
In the Garden [Matt. 26:36-46]

Verse 1
Pak tung zingdai a nom lai-in,
Keimah guak huan sungah ka vak hi;
Hong ki hopihna ka zak awging in,
Pasian Tapa’ aw khum hi.

Verse 2
Hong hopihna khum mahmah a,
Vasa te’n zongh ngai khipkhep uh hi;
Kei a hong sak tua la ngaih mahmah a,
Ka bilkha’n ka zaza hi.
 

Chorus
Amah’n hong tonpih in hong hopih a,
Amaaa ka hih hong gen hi;
Amah tawh om khawma nuam ka sakna,
Kua mah’n ciam kha ngei lo hi.

Verse 3
Zan khuamial sung ahi zongin,
Amah tawh ka om khawm den nuam hi;
Launa tam taleh hong zui in “Pai ni”,
Hong cihna aw ka za hi.

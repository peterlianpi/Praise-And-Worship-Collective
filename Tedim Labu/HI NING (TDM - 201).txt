Title: HI NING (TDM - 201)
'I Will' [Isai. 12:2]

Verse 1
Kei hong Honpa Thu in hong kipia a, Suahtakna kicing hi;
Tun ka lungsim khentat loh phamawh ta, Kong sang ta diam, ka ci.

Verse 2
Tu in Topa, Nang kong sang nuam ta hi, Na itna'n hong zo ta;
Khris Topa aw, Nang kongbeel nuamta hi, Nangmah bek kong muangta.
 

Chorus
Hi ning! (Hi ning!) Hi ning!(Hi ning!) Pasian aw nong huh hi,Nangaa bek hong hi ning;
Nong tatkhiat na'ng, Na si manpha luang hi,
Nangaa bek hong hi ning.

Verse 3
Ka thanemna, Lampial ding ka lau na, Topa aw nong thei hi;
Na nasep na'ng hong guan in, thahatna, Nangbek kong mitsuan hi.

Verse 4
Na hehpihna tu'n hong pia in, Topa, La in ka sasa ding;
Lungdam takin tu'n ka gen khia ding a, 'Khris aa bek hi'ng' ci ning.

Verse 5
Leitung Na om lai a hong zuan te in, 'Topa nong sem diam' ci;
'Kong sem nuam hi' ci a Na dawn na in, Kote' lungmuanna hi.

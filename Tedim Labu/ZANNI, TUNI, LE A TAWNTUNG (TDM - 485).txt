Title: ZANNI, TUNI, LE A TAWNTUNG (TDM - 485)
Yesterday, Today, Forever [Heb. 13:8]

Verse 1
Upna bek tawh i ngah theih thupha lamdang lua hi;
Zanni, tuni, a tawntung Jesuh kikhel lo hi;
Mawhnei te gum nuam, cina te zongh damsak nuam den;
A dah mi te lungnuam sak hi, A minthang tahen!

Verse 2
Mawhnei te lawmbawl in gum nuam a, a hong’ zong Pa;
Tu’n zuan in, kisikna tawh kuun in A khephung ah;
“Thu hong’ khen kei ning’, na mawh nawn ke’n a ci Pa in;
Na mawhna hong’ maisak nuam hi tanglai mahbangin.
 

Chorus
Zanni tuni, le a tawntung Jesuh kikhel lo,
Na dang’ khempeuh a kikhel hang’ Jesuh kikhel lo!
A minthang tahen! A minthang tahen!
Na dang’ khempeuh a kikhel hang’, Jesuh kikhel lo!

Verse 3
Leitung aom lai, Jesuh’n cina te damsak hi;
I natna te A thupiakna tawh dam thei lai hi;
Numei khat in A puan mong’ a lawn ciang’ dam pah hi;
Tuabang upna mahtawh nang zongh hong damsak thei hi.

Title: TOPA PHAT NI! (TDM - 35)
Bless the Lord, my Soul! [Late 34:1]

Verse 1
Minthang Kumpi phat un, Amah bek Pasian,
Na lamdang lian hong lah khiat man in phat ni;
Zan a meikhuam tawh lam hong makaih Pasian,
Sun a meiikhuam tawh hong pi Pasian phat ni.

Verse 2
Khawn khong tatna hong pia, phat ni, i Pasian,
Sapna teng hong luanna Tuinak hi, phat ni;
Phat ni, hong it, hong kem, a migi Pasian,
I thungetna te hong dawng Pasian phat ni.
 

Chorus
Vantung mite'n phatun, (Vantungmite'n phat un, Kham gawsem tum in)
Kham gawsem tum in; (Vantungmite'n phat un, Kham gawsem tum in)
A mi te'n zong kun in, (A mite'n zong kun in, Amai ah bia un)
A mai ah bia un; (A mite'n zong kun in, Amai ah bia un)
A gam lian pi sung ah, (A gam lianpi sungah, Kum tawn, khang tawn in)
Kum tawn, khangtawn in, (A gam lianpi sungah, Kum tawn, khang tawn in)
[ A bawl sate'n phat un ]3, Ke'n zongh ka phat ding.

Verse 3
Ei te hong it man a, hong ziaet Pasian,
Amah tawh hong kinai zaw sak Pa phat ni;
Upna, lawpna hang hong gualzo sak Pasian,
It le ngaih teng tawh hong gawm Pasian phat ni.

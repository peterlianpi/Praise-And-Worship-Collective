Title: DAI DIDE, ZAN SIANGTHO (TDM - 89)
Silent Night, Holy Night [Luka 2:8-12]

Verse 1
Dai dide! zan siangtho! Mial khikhe, tawm simloh,
Gal a Naungek lupna kiim, Nu siangtho le ama kiim;
Naungek limtak ihmu, Naungek limtak ihmu.

Verse 2
Dai dide! zan siangtho! Thakhat in, theih kholhloh,
Tuucingte' kiim tang pempam, Vantungpan phatna puakkham;
'Khris hong suak zo hi, Khris hong suak zo hi.'

Verse 3
Dai dide! zan siangtho! Mipilte'n, van en to,
Lam hilh Aksi tang zui in, Lut uh hi biakna lui in;
I Kumpi hong suak hi, I Kumpi hong suak hi.

Verse 4
Dai dide! zan siangtho! Aksi pi'n, hong taan' hen,
Vantung mite bang inah, Sa un Kumpi phatna la;
Hong Honpa hong suak hi, Hong Honpa hong suak hi.

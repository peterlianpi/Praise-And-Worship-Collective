Title: TO Jesuh THAHATNA (TDM - 472)
Thlk. 33:
Verse 27

Verse 1
To Jesuh’ thahatna suang inla,
Ham bangh hong’ huai’ in, hong huh ding hi;
Hong itna thu na phawk den nakleh, Phatna la sak nuam ding.

Verse 2
To Jesuh thahatna suang inla,
Lam hong tang sak ding, tang sak ding hi;
Hong paipihna peuh lungdam zui in, A aw nem no ngai in.
 

Chorus
A hatna le (A hatna te le hongit) hong itna muangin,
A hatna le, hong itna kicing,
A hatna le (A hatna te le lamet), Van innpi en in,
Hong Tanpa na mu dinghi.

Verse 3
To Jesuh thahatna suang inla,
Na buaina khempeuh Amah pia in;
Na vangik lel in na kun zongin, Jesuh kiang thungen in.

Verse 4
To Jesuh thahatna suang inla,
Na neih teng pia in bangkim pia in;
Itna le hehpihna tawh kidim, Hong ensan lo ding hi.

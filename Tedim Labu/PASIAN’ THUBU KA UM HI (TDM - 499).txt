Title: PASIAN’ THUBU KA UM HI (TDM - 499)
PASIAN’ THUBU KA UM HI

Verse 1
Pasian’ Thubu ka um hi, Jesuh’ thusim kisutna;
Van zuatna tuang lam’ LaiSiangtho, Vangam ka tun theih na’ng.

Verse 2
Pasian’ lungsim a thuk dan, Mihing le mawhna omzia;
I damna zongh hong telgen in, Thubul pen ahi hi.
 

Chorus
LaiSiangtho! LaiSiangtho! (Aw LaiSiangtho! Aw LaiSiangtho!)
Ka up ka khiat lah pen hi;
LaiSiangtho! LaiSiangtho! (Aw LaiSiangtho! Aw LaiSiangtho!)
Pasian’ Thubu ahi.

Verse 3
Jesuh’ thu kicinna hi, Pasian’ hong piak ahi hi;
Sihna thawhkikna hong hilh hi, Nuntakna bu ahi.

Verse 4
Zan a meivak tang bangin, Na thu in lungsim hong taan’;
Nadang te sangin manpha zaw hi, Lungsim sung ka kem ding.

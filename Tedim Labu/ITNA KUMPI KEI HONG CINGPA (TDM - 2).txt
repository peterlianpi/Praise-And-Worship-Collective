Title: ITNA KUMPI KEI HONG CINGPA (TDM - 2)
The King of Love My Shepherd Is [Late 23]

Verse 1
Itna Kumpi kei hong Cing pa, Amah'n hong huh lah ngei lo;
A mi suak leng deihna cing ta, Amah kei aa hi den zo.

Verse 2
Nuntakna tui luanna mun ah, Tatsa kei hong pai pih hi;
Kei hong pi hi, lo nona ah, Vantung an in hong vaak hi.

Verse 3
Topa'n hong ompih tawntung a, Sihna ka kih ta kei hi;
A ciang khut in lung muang sak a, Singlamteh in hong pi hi.

Verse 4
Ka khantawn in ka nuntak sung, Kei nong cing khial ngei kei hi;
Tuu cing pha aw, Na innpi sung, Phatna la kong sa ding hi.

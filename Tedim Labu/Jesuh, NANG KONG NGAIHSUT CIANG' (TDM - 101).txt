Title: Jesuh, NANG KONG NGAIHSUT CIANG' (TDM - 101)
Jesus, the very Thought of Thee [1 Peter 2:7]

Verse 1
Jesuh thu ngaihsut laitak in, Lungdam huai mahmah hi;
Hih sang' a mai ka muh ciangin, Lungdam zaw lai ding hi.

Verse 2
Jesuh a kici min sangin, Min ngaih zaw om lo pi,
Vantung leitung a hi zongin, Ki mu zo ngei lo hi.

Verse 3
Dahna thuak te' ki hehnepna, Lung niamkhiat te' nuamna;
Thungen a zong mite ading, Itna lian bel hi ta.

Verse 4
Kam tawh a ki gen khia thei lo, Thupha a ngah uh hi;
A kisim khin zo lo itna, Topa Jesuh thu hi.

Verse 5
Topa Jesuh nangmah sim loh, Nuamna dang' om nawnlo;
Tu le nungciang a tawntung in, Komau phat ding om in.

Title: MAWHNA’ ZOL ZUI KEI IN (TDM - 289)
Yield Not to Temptation [Mang. 2:7]

Verse 1
Mawhna’ zol zui kei in, Na zuih leh khial ding;
Khatvei zo phot le cin, Azom zawh nop ding;
Pasal! nakpi’n do in, Lunggulhna mialpi;
Jesuh en gige in, Hong zawhpih ding hi.

Verse 2
Kholhpih gilo peel in, Kam hoih lo zangh ke’n;
Pasian’ min zahtak in, A mawkna’n zangh ke’n;
Thukhual in, citak in, Thuman in migi;
Jesuh en gige in, Hong zawhpih ding hi.
 

Chorus
Hong huh nading thungen in, Tha hong pia hong kem ding hi;
Hong huh nuam hi Topa in, A hong zawhpih ding hi.

Verse 3
A zo te Pasian in, Kham lukhu pia ding;
I puk khak zel hangin, Upna tawh zo ding;
Eite hong Honpa in, Tha hong gua’n ding hi;
Jesuh en gige in, Hong zawhpih ding hi.

Title: ZUI LAI NING! (TDM - 283)
Follow On! [John 12:26]

Verse 1
Paak te a palhna mun, Tuidam a luanna mun,
Mual kuamah hong gum Topa tawh ka pai ding hi;
Ama hong paipihna teng ah ka zuizui ding hi,
Zawhna lukhu ngah dong ka zuizui ding hi.

Verse 2
Huihpi a nung a tuipi nakpi a luanna,
Mualkuam ah hong gum Topa tawh ka pai ding a;
Amah in ka khut hong let ciangin ka lau kei ding,
Topa in hong nai laitak ka lau thei kei.
 

Chorus
Ka zui ding hi! Jesuh ka zui ding hi,
Gamlapi gam khempeuh ke’n ka zui ding hi!
Ka zui ding hi! Jesuh ka zui ding hi,
Hong makaihna mun teng ah ka zui ding hi.

Verse 3
Guam thukpi a hi a, keensang dawn ahi zong,
Hong hon Topa tawh ka omkhawm ding atawp dong;
Ama tot sa lampi tawn-in kei hong ciahpih ding,
Pasian’ mualtung kikhopna hong tunpih ding.

Title: MI TAM KISAM TAWNTUNG (TDM - 270)
Thy Work, O God, Needy Man’s Hands [Luka 21:2]

Verse 1
Topa Pasian, Na nasem ding Mi tam kisam tawntung
Tuate in sem kim zo lo ding; Huhna puak kei le ung.

Verse 2
Topa Nangmah kong it man un, Nasep nading in ah
Kong piak uh zangh in Nangma thu’n; Jesuh min ding inah.

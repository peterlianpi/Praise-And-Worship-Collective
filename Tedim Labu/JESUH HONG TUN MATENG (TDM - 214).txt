Title: JESUH HONG TUN MATENG (TDM - 214)
We'll Work Till Jesus Comes [Jer. 31:16]

Verse 1
Gimna sung pan lamet gam nuam, Cik ciang ka tung ding hiam?
Cik ciang galvan ka koih ding hiam, Lailung ka muang ding hiam?

Verse 2
Sim leitung lai lungdam omlo, Lum bang sung omlo tawh;
Sim leitung sau nuam ahi lo, Gam bang daihna a hi.
 

Chorus
Jesuh (Jesuh) hong tun ma teng, Jesuh (Jesuh) hong tun mateng;
I na (I na) sep zawh ciangin, Inn nuam sung om khawm ding.

Verse 3
Limbel siangtho ngah ding inah, Lam bang na pial ke'n ci;
Lengvan siang nuam ka tun mateng, Nuamna hong pia ding hi.

Verse 4
Limbel nen ah khawl zo ta in, Lam dang ka pial kei ding;
Amah bang a ka pham nung ciang, Lengvan siang nuam tung ning.

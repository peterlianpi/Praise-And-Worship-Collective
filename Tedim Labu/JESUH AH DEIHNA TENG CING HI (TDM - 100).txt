Title: JESUH AH DEIHNA TENG CING HI (TDM - 100)
Jesus is All the World to Me [Gal. 6:14]

Verse 1
Jesuh ah deihna teng cing hi, Hinna, nuamna, van nuai; Nisim in ama tha lo-in, Ka puk ding a lauhuai;
Ka dah ciang Ama kiang ka pai, Mun dang banglo ka mai a taii; Ka dah ciang hong nuam sak, Amah Keima Lawm.

Verse 2
Jesuh ah deihna teng cing hi, Lungkham laitak ka Lawm; Thupha deih in Amah zuan leng, A man'n hong pia nawnnawn;
Nitaang le guahtui hong pia hi, Tuk ciangin an hong la sak hi; Ni taang, guahtui le an hongpia, Kei ma Lawm.

Verse 3
Jesuh ah deih na teng cing hi, A tung'h ka thumaan ding; Kei tung ah hong thuman ka Lawm, Bangci'n ka nial zo ding;
Amah zui leng ka khial ngei kei, Sun le zan hong cing khial ngei kei; Sun le zan in zui ning Amah, Keima Lawm.

Verse 4
Jesuh ah deihna teng cing hi, Lawmpha zaw deih tuan keng; Amah tu-in ke'n ka muang a, Muang lai ding sih ma teng;
Hih bang Lawm tawh nuntak a nuam, Bei ngeilo ding hinna a nuam; Bei ngeilo nuntakna, nuamna, Keima Lawm.

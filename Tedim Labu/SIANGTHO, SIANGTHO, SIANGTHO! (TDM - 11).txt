Title: SIANGTHO, SIANGTHO, SIANGTHO! (TDM - 11)
Holy, Holy, Holy! [Mang. 4:8]

Verse 1
Siangtho, siangtho, siangtho! A lian pen To Pasian,
Zingsang tungin phatna la, Kong sak to ding uh hi;
Siangtho, siangtho, siangtho! Hehpih thei a vang lian,
Thum ki gawmna, Siangtho thupha Pasian!

Verse 2
Siangtho, siangtho, siangtho! Na misiang khempeuh in,
Kham lukhute suahkhia in, Nangmah hong bia uh hi;
Vantung misiangte in, Nangma mai ah kun in,
Tawntung Pasian, Nang mah hong bia uh hi.

Verse 3
Siangtho, siangtho, siangtho! Khuamial hong liah hang in,
Mawhna mit in Na vanglian, Hong muh theih loh hangin;
Nangmah bek Na siangtho, Kuamah'n hong ban zo lo,
Na vangliatna, Itna le sianthona.

Verse 4
Siangtho, siangtho, siangtho! A lian pen To Pasian!
Leitung, tuitung, vantung ah, Na min ki phat ding hi;
Siangtho, siangtho, siangtho! Hehpih thei a vanglian!
Thum ki gawmna, Siangtho thupha Pasian!

Title: TUNG SUNNI TUM TA-A (TDM - 28)
Day Is Dying In the West [Late 55:17; Isai. 6:3]

Verse 1
Tung sunni hong tum ta a, Tawlngakna hun hong tungta;
Zan aksi hong suah lai in,
Thangvan paak bang palh lai in, Pasian bia ni.

Verse 2
Nuntakna teng a Uk pa, Van nuai teng Nangma omna;
Nangma mai hong zong kote,
Na ang sungah hong khawm ve, Kinai tak in.
 

Chorus
Siangtho, siangtho, siangtho Topa Pasian;
Leitung vantung ah Na vaang,
Nangma phatna hong ki langh, Sangbel Pasian.

Verse 3
Khua hong mial semsem hangin, A tang aksi te kheng in;
Ko lungsim hong kah to hi,
Nang mai kong mu nuam uh hi, Van nuai Itpa.

Verse 4
Aksi te, le zan le sun, Mu ngei nawnlo ding a hun;
Hong tun ciang vantung pan in,
Tawntung zingkhua vak sak ta'n; Ko te' mai ah.

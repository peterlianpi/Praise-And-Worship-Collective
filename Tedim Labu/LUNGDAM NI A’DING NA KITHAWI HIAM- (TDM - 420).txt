Title: LUNGDAM NI A’DING NA KITHAWI HIAM? (TDM - 420)
The Happy Crowning Day [1 Thes. 4:17]

Verse 1
Vantung panin vangliatna tawh Topa hong pai kik ding hun,
Lei le van mi a thei kuamah om lo hi;
Mi siangthote’n Amah dawn in a mite hong sam ding a,
Tua ni ciang pengkul aw ging i za ding hi.

Verse 2
Mit phiatkal tomno khat sungin i kikhel khin ta ding a,
Si thei nawnlo dingin i kikhel ding hi;
Amah it te’n Amah suun ding van pan hong kum suk ding a,
Sunkim, zankim, zingsang zong hi thei ding hi.
 

Chorus
Na kithawi hiam? Na kithawi hiam? thawi hiam? Kumpi dawn dingin,
Sem kawm ngak kawm na kiging zo hiam? (kigingzo hiam?)
Na kithawi hiam? thawi hiam? Kumpi dawn dingin,
Lungdam ni ading na kithawi hiam?

Verse 3
I mawhna si bangh a san hang’ Kalvarypan hong’ tan ta,
Singlamteh liim nuai ah damna va zuan ni;
Amah’n eite hong kem den a, vuk bangin hong’ ngosak ta,
Meiipi tung tuangin hong pai suk kik ding hi.

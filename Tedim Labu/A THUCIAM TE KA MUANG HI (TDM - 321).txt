Title: A THUCIAM TE KA MUANG HI (TDM - 321)
Standing on the promises [2 Kor. 1:20]

Verse 1
Topa thuciamna pha muang takin om ning,
A tawntung in hong gum Topa vaang um ning;
A minthang hici inah ka kiko hi,
A thuciamna pha ka muang ding hi.

Verse 2
A sia thei lo Topa thuciamna ka muang,
Upkhialh man a launa huih bangh hong nun hang’;
Nuntak na’ng aa a thupha bel tawh zawh na’ng,
A thuciamna pha ka muang ding hi.
 

Chorus
Muang in (A thuciamna pha muang ning),
om ning (A thuciamna pha muang ning),
Hong Gum Topa Pasian thuciamna pha muang ning;Muang in (A thuciamna pha muang ning),
om ning (A thuciamna pha muang ning),
Hong Gum Topa Pasian thuciam muang ning.

Verse 3
Tuin a thuciamna muangin ka om hi,
A sipha tawh hong sawp leh ka siang ding hi;
Mawhna sungpan suahtak ding thu ka ngah hi,
A thuciamna pha ka muang ding hi.

Verse 4
Topa Jesuh na thuciamna ka muang hi,
A thupha tawh khau bangin hong hen ding hi;
KhaSiangtho tawh dawimangpa ka zo ding hi,
A thuciamna pha kamuang ding hi.

Verse 5
A thuciam te muang peuh leng ka puk ngeikei,
KhaSiangtho hong hopihna aw ka za thei;
Hong Honpa sungah ka kinga hithiat thei,
A thuciamna pha ka muang ding hi.

Title: PAI IN SEM IN (TDM - 387)
Go and Work! [Luke 14:23]

Verse 1
Tapidaw ihmu nawn ke’n, Mi ngah na’ng sep theih lai sem;
Khutzep in ding mawk ding maw? Topa in thu hong pia leh.

Verse 2
Topa min na it ding hiam? Hong ciamna na um zo diam?
Sapna teng A kiang ngen in, Hong sapna na nial ding maw?

Verse 3

Verse 4

Title: LA HONG SASA NUAMSAK DEN (TDM - 158)
He Keeps Me Singing [Matt. 1:21]

Verse 1
Ka lung sungah la ngaih hong ki sa, Jesuh hong hopihna hi;
Lau ke'n nang kong ompih tawntungna, Lungmuang takin om in, ci.

Verse 2
Ka nuntakna mawhna'n sugawp zo, Lungsim sia, natna dim sak;
Jesuh'n a sia teng hong lep sak zo, A ngaih la khanglo kik sak.
 

Chorus
Jesuh Jesuh Jesuh, Min teng pan ngaih pen;
Lunggulh teng ki cinna, La hong sasa nuam sak den.

Verse 3
Hong hehpihna pha tawh kivak in, A kha nuai ka beel zo ta;
A maitai ka muh tawntung manin, Awng in la ka sa thei ta.

Verse 4
A sawtlo in hong pai kik ding hi, Van ah hong lato dingin;
Theih loh gam ah ka leengto ding hi, Amah tawh mang khawm dingin.

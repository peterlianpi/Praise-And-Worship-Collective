Title: SINGLAMTEH KIANGAH (TDM - 120)
Near the Cross [Efesa 2:23]

Verse 1
Singlamteh kiangah om na, Kalvary mualtung ah;
Nuamna hong pia ciktui in, Mi teng ading luang ta.

Verse 2
Singlamteh kiang ka ding a, Hehpihna'n hong mu a;
A taang zingsol Aksi in, Ka kiimkot ah taang ta.
 

Chorus
Singlamteh, singlamteh, Ka kisaktheihna hi;
Vantung khua ka tun ciangin, A nuam ka ngah na'ng hi.

Verse 3
Jesuh singlamteh kiangah, Ngak a kong om ciangin;
Singlamteh a liim nuaiah, Hong gum hong om sak in.

Verse 4
Singlamteh kiangah ka om, Athu um tawntung ning';
Gungal khat a kham khuapi, Ka tun mateng ngak ning.

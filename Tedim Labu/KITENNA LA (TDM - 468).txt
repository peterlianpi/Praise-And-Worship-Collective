Title: KITENNA LA (TDM - 468)
Wedding Song [Late 45:13-14]

Verse 1
Pasian in kitenna pawi bawl khin zo hi,
A siangtho innkuan a suak dingin;
A kicing innkuan khat a suahna dingin,
A siangtho nuntakna a nei ding.

Verse 2
Jesuh in Tapidaw te a it manin,
A nuntakna a hong piak bangin;
Hih zi nei pa in zongh tua bangin hih hen,
A siangtho thuhilhna zui ta hen.

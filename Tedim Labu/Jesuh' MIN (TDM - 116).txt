Title: Jesuh' MIN (TDM - 116)
The Name of Jesus [Matt. 1:21]

Verse 1
Jesuh min bangzah in ngaih hiam, Ama' la te sasa nuam ing;
Kalung hong kim sak mahmah hi, Jesuh amin manpha hi.

Verse 2
Ka lungsim le ka dahna teng, A thei khin pa' min ngaih kasa;
Launa lungmanna te beisak, Jesuh a min ka it hi.
 

Chorus
'Jesuh' bangzah a ngaih min 'Jesuh' Nisim in tua mah;
'Jesuh,' kici min manpha Phat tek ni, (Phat tek ni a) a tawntung in.

Verse 3
Tua min ngaih ka zaza nuam hi, Ka lungsim hong nopsak khial kei;
Ama la te'n khitui nul sak, Jesuh a min thangsak un.

Verse 4
Kua mah'n gen thei ngei hetlo hi, Tua kait min, bangzah'n ngaih hiam;
Aw, tua min phatna bek ging hi, Jesuh a min phat ve un.

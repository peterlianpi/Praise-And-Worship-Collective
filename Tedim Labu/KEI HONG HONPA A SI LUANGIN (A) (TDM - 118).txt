Title: KEI HONG HONPA A SI LUANGIN (A) (TDM - 118)
(A)
Alas! and Did My Saviour Bleed [Luka 22:19]

Verse 1
Kei hong Honpa A si luang in, Ka Kumpi hong si maw?
Tangtel bangh bek keimah ading, A lutang hong zangh maw?

Verse 2
Keima gitlohna te hangin, Singlamteh tung' tau maw?
Lamdang hehpihna le itna, Bang tawh teh thei ding maw!

Verse 3
Kei zongh singlamteh ka muh ciang', Zum in ka kiseel diam;
Lungdamna tawh lung nehno in, Ka khi ka luang sak diam.

Verse 4
Ahihhang khitui takna in, Itna loh khin zo kei;
Topa ka pumpi kong pia hi, Hih kan' ka hih thei kei.

Title: MAWHNEI TE AW HONG PAI UN (TDM - 107)
Come, Ye Sinners [Isai. 55:1]

Verse 1
Jesuh in vantung om napi, Leitung ah hong paisuk hi;
Mihing te tawh hong omkhawm a, Cina te cidam sak hi;
LaiSiangtho sungah kithei ta, Ahoih alian phabel hi.

Verse 2
Khua khempeuh ah pai kawikawi a, Mi khempeuh a hehpih hi;
Mihing zawngkhal khempeuh te le, Mittaw, khelbai, misi te;
Athu in' hong dam kik sak a, Lamdang kisa mahmah hi.

Verse 3
Jesuh, leitung a om lai-in, Thupha vive a gen hi;
Keimah zongh Jesuh ka um a, Ka lungdamna piang ding hi;
Ka uplohna Pa Pasian in, Phatakin hong bawlpha in.

Verse 4
Pasian in thupha hong piakna, Jesuh'n mikiang ah gen hi;
Mihing kimkhat in a um a, Kimkhat in a um kei hi;
A umlo te in lungkhamna, Jesuh cihbang piang ding hi.

Verse 5
Kei sih tangding Topa Jesuh, Singlamteh tungah si hi;
Ama lawmte in a vui a, Ni thum ciang' a thokik hi;
Tu'n vantung khua ah a om a, Mi khempeuh a hehpih hi.

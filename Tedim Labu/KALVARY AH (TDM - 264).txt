Title: KALVARY AH (TDM - 264)
At Calvary [Eph. 2:8]

Verse 1
Kiphatsakna’n kum sawt ka bei sak,
Kei ading kithat Topa don lo;
Kei ading hong sih lam ka phawk kei, Kalvary ah.

Verse 2
Pasian’ thu in ka mawh lam phawksak,
Bang ka hi ka phawk ciang ka liing a;
A mawh ka Kha thum in ka ki hei, Kalvary lam.
 

Chorus
Man piak loh hehpihna a lian,
le Mawh maisakna kei tungah khang a;
Ka Kha vangik tu in suakta hi, Kalvary ah.

Verse 3
Tu’n Jesuh a vekpi’n ka pia khin,
Tu’n Amah ka Kumpipa hi ta;
Tu’n a si sa Kha in la a sa, Kalvary mah.

Verse 4
Aw, gupkhiatna thu a geel itna,
Aw! mi tung dong hong puaksuk thupha;
Aw! a khauh kizopna lei hong dawh, Kalvary ah.

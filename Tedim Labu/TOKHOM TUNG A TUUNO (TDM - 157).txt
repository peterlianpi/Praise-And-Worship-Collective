Title: TOKHOM TUNG A TUUNO (TDM - 157)
Crown Him with Many Crowns [Isaiah 9:6; Mang. 19:6, 12]

Verse 1
Tokhom tunga Tuuno, Lukhu namkim khu zo;
Ngai un, a ngaih vantung la pi, La dang' teng meng khin hi;
Ka kha aw tho inla, Nang' ading hong si Pa;
A lianbel Kumpipa ci in, A phatna la sa in.

Verse 2
Ki itna Kumpipa, En ve un! A liamma;
A khut, A pang ki mu lai hi, Minphatna in tuam hi;
Hih thupha lamdang lua, Phawk zawh lohin thuk lua;
Vantungmi'n zongh en ngam loin, Kuun hi, nuailam en in.

Verse 3
Hong kilem sak Kumpi, Galvai vengsak Pa hi;
Phatna aw, siatna suankhia in, Vannuai kilem na'ng in;
Vantung khuapi nuam sung, Amah in uk tawntung;
A liam, Ama khe kimkot ah, Dawnna paak kipia pah.

Verse 4
A tawntung a Kumpi, Hun le ni uk Mangpi;
Leitung pumpi na piangsak zo, Gen zawh loh Na Siangtho;
Hong tan ka Topa aw, Kei tang' nang' nong si maw;
Na min phat tawp ngeilo ding hi'ng, A tawntung sa ding hi'ng.

Title: AW GAM NUAMPI (TDM - 451)
Beulah Land [Thkna. 33:28]

Verse 1
An le leng tui gam ka tung zo, A phatna teng kei aa hi zo;
Lungdam huai ni tang tawntung hi, Keima khuamial lengmang zo hi.

Hong Honpa kei tawh hong vak khawm, Nuam takin kei tawh hong om khawm;

Kei ading bawl kholhsa innpi, Tuipi galah ka galmuh ta;
Ka tenna ding vantung khuapi, A tangpha hi tuipi galah.

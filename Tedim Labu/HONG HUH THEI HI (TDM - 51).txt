Title: HONG HUH THEI HI (TDM - 51)
Able to Deliver Hebru 7:
Verse 25

Verse 1
Lungmang mi aw, na kihta kei in, Nangma Topa Pasian na muang ta in;
Thungen in, na singlamteh gik hang in, Amah'n hong huh ding hi.

Verse 2
Lungmang mi aw, gal tam hangin ah, Gal nanna le thu upna lian sak ta'n;
Topa vangliatna tawh na do ding a, Galzo na hiding hi.
 

Chorus
Amah in nang a hong gumthei lai hi. Ama khut in gal liahna hi ding hi,
Amah in nang hong gumthei lai ding hi. Tuahi a lau kei in.

Verse 3
Lungmang mi aw, lam haksat ciangin, Topa'n kihtakna a paihkhiatsak ding;
Huhna na deih leh A mah'n nei lai ding, Amin phasak ta in.

Title: PHAT UN! PHAT UN! (TDM - 161)
Praise Him! Praise Him! [Late 146:2]

Verse 1
Phat un! phat un! eite hong hon thupha Jesuh,
Sa un! sa un! hong itna thang sak un;
Dawn un! dawn un! vantungmi a lianbel te aw,
Amin siangtho pahtawi in liansak un!
Tuu cin bangin Jesuh'n naupang te cing' in,
Ama angah tawi hi sun niloh in;
* Phat un, phat un, A liatna pha dem zo om lo,
* Phat un, phat un, nuamna la tawh phat un!

Verse 2
Phat un! phat un! eite hong hon thupha Jesuh,
Gimna, sihna, ei mawhna hang' thuak hi;
Dawnun! dawnun! Singlamteh thuak Topa Jesuh,
Suahtak nading lametna Suangpi hi!
Eite' dahna hong puak sak Jesuh hi a,
Bei theilo hi lamdang hat hong itna;
* Phat un, phat un, A liatna pha dem zo om lo,
* Phat un, phat un, nuamna la tawh phat un!

Verse 3
Phat un! phat un! eite hong hon thupha Jesuh,
Amah phat na'n vantung innpi thawn hi;
Mang tawntung ta, eite hong hon Topa Jesuh,
Kamsang, Siampi, Kumpi za ngah ta hi!
Khris hong kum ciang' leitung zo gawp dinga,
Ukna vangliatna khempeuh Ama a';
* Phat un, phat un, A liatna pha dem zo om lo,
* Phat un, phat un, nuamna la tawh phat un!

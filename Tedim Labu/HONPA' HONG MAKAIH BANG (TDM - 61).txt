Title: HONPA' HONG MAKAIH BANG (TDM - 61)
Where the Saviour Leads [Matt. 8:19]

Verse 1
A siangtho tui luanna mualkuam zui dingin,Jesuh in lam hong pi leh lungdam zui ning;
A khut zan in, tuipi hual tung hong sawl zong,A mang te gup nading A thu va puak ning.

Verse 2
Bangmah om lo, mualsang tung ahi zongin,A lawm te tawh kikhen, a mangthang tuuno;
Huih le guahpi a zu zongin va zong ning,Gumpa hong lah bang mailam nawt va pai ning.
 

Chorus
Lam hong hilh bang zui ning, Lungdam tak in zui ning;
Gumpa hong hilh bang in zui ning, Lungdam tak zui ning.

Verse 3
Ka zui ding hi, Gumpa hong lah mahbangin,Dah taleng, nuam taleng, lungdam in zui ning;
Gum pa'n thei hi, a muanhuai thuman lampi,A tang kham khuapi sungah hong tun pih ding.

Title: MAWH PHELNA, NOPNA, VANGLIATNA (TDM - 96)
Pardon, Peace, and Power [Jer. 33:8; Late 29:11; Sawl. 1:8]

Verse 1
Topa tung lungdam ngah nuam te'n, Kisaktheihna paii in;
Um in Ama thu apha te, Hong piak ding ngaihsun in.

Verse 2
A thu le hong itna pha a, A si tawh hong sawp hi;
I mawhna khempeuh hong sawp a, Nopna pha ki ngah hi.
 

Chorus
 (Beating four time to the measure)
Mawhphelna nuam vanglianpi, Lungdam sak ning a min phatna;
Sianthona pha vantung om hi, Kei tang si hi, Jesuh Khris Topa.

Verse 3
Mawhna vanglian sukgawp nading, Siamna pha hong pia hi;
Lungsim sunga siantho nading, Kha pha hong bawlsak hi.

Verse 4
Adang lungsim zawh nading in, Khasiangtho hong pia hi;
Aphabel Khris Topa in, I lung sungah om hi.

Verse 5
Naupang bangmah'n i up ciangin, Thupha i ngah ding hi;
Thupha bel Khris Topa in, Hongpiak ding ciamsa hi.

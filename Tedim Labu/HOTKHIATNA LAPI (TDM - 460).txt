Title: HOTKHIATNA LAPI (TDM - 460)
A Song of Salvation [Johan 3:16]

Verse 1
Honpa’ itna thu ka za hi,
Itna lamdang himah hi ding hi;
Hih hang, itna hehpihna tawh,
Van pan kei ading hong kumsuk maw? (suk maw?)
Van pan kei ading hong kum suk maw?

Verse 2
Sisan luang in hong thuak ka thei,
Sing tungah gimna thuak in hong si;
Mun khempeuh ah hih thu ki thei,
Hong khasiat man kei ading thuak hi (thuak hi),
Hong khasiat man kei ading thuak hi.
 

Chorus
Hi, hi, hi, kei ading hi, Keima ading hi,
Topa hong kum suk, A sihna, tul ngeilo hong itna, Nang le kei ading hi.

Verse 3
Vantung thu hong ki gen ka za,
Jesuh a um te’n mu ding uh hi;
Kei ading vantung ah omna,
Bawl kholhsa in a om hiam? innpi (inn pi),
Bawl kholhsa in a om hiam? inn pi.

Verse 4
Topa kong dotna hong dawng in,
Nang kiang lo kua kiang pai ding ka hiam?
KhaSiangtho kei hong hilh sak in,
Honpa le vantung om, ci’n nong ciam (nongciam),
Honpa le vantung om ci’n nong ciam.

Title: KE’N KA MUANG HI (TDM - 323)
Fully Trusting [Late 119:42]

Verse 1
Topa thu kiciamna thei a, Up khialhna ka paai ding hi;
Lungkiatna thuakloh na’ng inah, A thupha ka um ding hi.

Verse 2
Ki mawhsakna Jesuh inah, A si pha tawh hong sawp hi;
Keimah hong siangtho sak zota, Innpi ah hong kha ding hi.
 

Chorus
A kicing hi, a thuphapi, Keimah inah ka um hi;
A kicing hi, a thuphapi, Keimah inah ka um hi;

Verse 3
Topa Jesuh hong hehpih man’, Kihtak launa omlo hi;
Ka lampi ah khua a mial hang, Khuavak hong taangsak ding hi.

Verse 4
Ka nuamna teng Jesuh ka pia, Amah bek ke’n ka deih hi;
Amah in leitung teng uk a, Deihna teng A kiang om hi.

Verse 5
Ka vekpi in Jesuh ka pia, Ka lungsim ka pum khempeuh;
Ka lametna a om khempeuh, Tawntung Ama kiang om hi.

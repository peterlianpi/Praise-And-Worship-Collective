Title: DO NILOH IN (TDM - 291)
Hold the Fort [Isai. 12:2]

Verse 1
Aw lawm te aw, dialpei en un, Vantung a a’ khai’;
Hong huh ding Mangpa hong kuan ding, I zawhna a nai’!

Verse 2
En un gal tampi hong pai a, Satan hong masa;
Thahat te kimkhat si zo a, Hanna bei dek ta.
 

Chorus
Galdo in tu’n ka hong pai ding! Jesuh’n dial hong lak;
A thupha tawh om tawntung ning, Dial na lak kik sak.

Verse 3
A vanglian dialpei ki mu hi, Peengkul aw ki za;
Hong uk Mangpa a minpha tawh, Gal te ka zo ta.

Verse 4
Gal kapna sawtvei haksa a, Huhna hong nai hi;
Hong uk Mangpa hong pai laitak, Lungdam khin un, ci.

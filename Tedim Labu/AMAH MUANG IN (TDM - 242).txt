Title: AMAH MUANG IN (TDM - 242)
Only Trust Him [Matt. 11:29]

Verse 1
Mawhna a nei te hong pai un, Topa in hong hehpih;
Ama thupha bel up ciangin, Nuamna i ngah ding hi.

Verse 2
Nuamna a hong pia ding Jesuh, A si pha bel luangsak;
Vuk sang' in hong ngo zaw sakthei, A si sung hong phum ta'n.
 

Chorus
Amah muang in, Amah mung in, Tuin ah muangin;
Hong gum ding hi, hong gum ding hi, Tuin hong gum ding.

Verse 3
Nopna sung tun' nading Jesuh, Thuman lampi a hi;
Ziakai kei in, Amah um in, Thupha na ngah ding hi.

Verse 4
Mi siangtho kikhopna mun le, Minthanna mun pai ni;
Lungdam om tawntungna munah, Nuamna i ngah ding hi.

Title: DING UN, DING UN, Jesuh’ MI (TDM - 293)
Stand Up, Stand Up for Jesus [Late 46:16]

Verse 1
Ding un, ding un, Jesuh’ mi, Singlamteh galkap te;
A kumpi dial saang khan un, Lelhna thuak sak lo e!
Gal khat khit khat aban in, Amah’n hong zawhpih ding;
Gal khempeuh zawh khit ciangin Khris, Topa mang ding.

Verse 2
Ding un, ding un, Jesuh’ mi, Mangun, pengkul sawlna;
Galpi phual ah va kuan un, Tuni, Topa ni pha.
Pasal ki sa teng kuan un, Gal te a tam hang’ in;
Tha le tha kisi sak un, Hang seem un, lau ciangin.

Verse 3
Ding un, ding un, Jesuh’ mi, Ama tha bek suang un;
Mihing tha’n hong lel sak ding, Nomau tha muang kei un.
Pasian thu galvan bangin, Silh in vil in, thu ngen;
Gal le sep ding om ciangin, A baihsa’n om khial ke’n.

Verse 4
Ding un, ding un, Jesuh’ mi, Gal bei hak nawn lo ding,
Tu’n gal kap nading zing ciang, Gal zawhna la ging ding.
A zo te khempeuh in ah, Nuntak lukhu ngah ding;
Vanglian, Kumpi pa kiang ah, A tawntung mang khawm ding.

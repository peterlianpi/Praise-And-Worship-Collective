Title: TANGLAI-A A LIAN BEL TOPA (TDM - 47)
O God, Our Help in Ages Past [Late 90:1, 2]

Verse 1
Tanglai a a lian bel Topa, Nung ciang a lawpna hi;
Tui kilok ciang na omna sung, Hehpih in hong liah hi.

Verse 2
Topa Pasian tokhom kiim ah, Misiangtho te'n nuamsa;
Na khut tawh Na liahna, kepna, Bit bel ding hi Topa.

Verse 3
Mual le guam te a ki khun ma, Lei pumpi pian ma in;
Tawntung Pasian Na hi khin a, Kip den ding kumkhua in.

Verse 4
Kum tul kum then Topa mai ah, Nitak om ngei lo hi;
Khuamialna in a leeng mang a, Zingsang khuavak bang hi.

Verse 5
Tuikhu bang hinna bei thei hi, Manlang in tawp thei hi;
Zan, nitak, zingsang ciang mang hi, I mang tawh ki bang hi.

Verse 6
Tanglai a a lian bel Topa, Nung ciang a lawpna hi;
Gimna thuak ciang hong keem pha in, Na inn ah hong kha in.

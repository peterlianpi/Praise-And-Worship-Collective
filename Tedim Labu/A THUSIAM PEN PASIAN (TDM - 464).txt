Title: A THUSIAM PEN PASIAN (TDM - 464)
O God, Most Gracious [Matt. 19:6]

Verse 1
A thusiam pen Pasian aw, Deih teng cinna Na hi;
Na nuntakna laigil tawh, Amau te Na gawm hi.
Sih dongin amau te gel, A thuciam uh kip hen;
Na vangliatna tawh ki geel, Na thupha sang uh hen.

Verse 2
Leitung ah Na om lai in, Kana Na pai lai tak;
Kiteenna pawi va sim in, Tui, lengtui Na suaksak.
Tualai mopina pawi a, Na thupha hong tung hen;
Nangma deihna bangin ah, Nang sung kip den uh hen.

Verse 3
Nangmah hong muan theih nang un, Topa aw, Na huh in;
Haksatna a thuak ciang’ un, Na om pih tawntung in.
Leitung a nusiat ni dong’, Nang’ sungah kip uh hen;
Na innpi ah hong’ tun dong’, Nangmah hong’ muang uh hen.

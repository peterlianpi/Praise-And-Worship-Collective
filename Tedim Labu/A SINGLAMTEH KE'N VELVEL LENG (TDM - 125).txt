Title: A SINGLAMTEH KE'N VELVEL LENG (TDM - 125)
When I Survey the Wondrous Cross [Gal. 6:14]

Verse 1
Vanglian Kumpi pa hong sihna,
A singlamteh ke'n velvel leng;
Kei' hauhna kei kiphatsakna,
Ke'n tu'n bangmah in sim nawn keng.

Verse 2
Khris ka Pasian' sihna lo,
Kisialhpih ding bangmah om kei;
Ka deih bel teng khat zongh siit lo,
Ama' si khekna in kanei.

Verse 3
En ven, A lu, A khut, A khe! Dahna,
itna hong luangsuk khawm;
Na mu ngei maw linglukhu le,
Itna, dahna gawm kilawm khawm.

Verse 4
Leitung khempeuh pia thei hi leng,
Tam lua ka ci ngeikei ding hi;
A hong itna ke'n ngaihsun leng,
Kapum ka tak ka pia nuam hi.

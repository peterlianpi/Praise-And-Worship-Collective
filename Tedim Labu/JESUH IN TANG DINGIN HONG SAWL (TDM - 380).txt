Title: JESUH IN TANG DINGIN HONG SAWL (TDM - 380)
Jesus Bids us Shine [Matt. 5:16]

Verse 1
Jesuh in eite khuaimei vak bangin,
Zan khuamialna sungah tang in hong sawl hi;
Hih leitung khuamial sungah i tang ding,
Nang na omna kiim ah, Kei, kei’ kiim ah.

Verse 2
Jesuh’n hong tang sak Ama adingin,
I meivak a mial ciang’ Amah’n hong mu hi;
I meivak tang van pan Amah’n en suk,
Nang na omna kiim ah, Kei, kei’ kiim ah.

Verse 3
Jesuh’n hong tang sak mi teng adingin,
Khuamialna tampi tawh kidim hih leitung;
Mawhna, dahna, natna sung i tang ding,
Nang na omna kiim ah, Kei, kei’ kiim ah.

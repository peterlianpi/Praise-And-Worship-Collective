Title: PASIAN IN HONG KEM HI (TDM - 52)

Verse 1
Gimna thuak hang lungmang kei in, Pasian in hong kem hi;
Ama itnan nuaiah om in,

Verse 2
Nasep nakpi nasep lai tak, Pasian in hong kem hi;
Na paina lam lau na om leh,

Chorus
Pasian in hong kem hi, Nisim inah, tawntung inah;

Verse 3
Amah'n na deih teng hong pia ding, Pasian in hong kem hi;

Verse 4
Ziaet, bawlsiana thuak hangin, Pasian in hong kem hi;

Title: JESUH AW, KA SIH DONGIN (TDM - 374)
O Jesus, I Have Promised [Joshua 24:21]

Verse 1
Jesuh aw, ka sih dongin, Nangma nasem dingin;
Kiciamna kip ka koih zo, Nangmah ka Lawm ka To;
Hong panpih tawntung le cin, Nang lam hong hilh lecin;
Galdona kihta kei ning’, Lampam sikkhial kei ning’.

Verse 2
Nong nai hong phawk khialsak ke’n, Thu dang’ te’n hong neh den;
Muhnop huai te in hong leng, Zaknop huai te’n hong meng;
Ka gal te hong nai tawntung, Ka kiim le ka lung sung;
Tua te sang hong nai zaw in, Mawhna pan hong dal in.

Verse 3
Nangma pau hong za sak in, A siang a teltak in;
Lung sung huihpi a nun ciang’, Mang lo, ka phun nop ciang’;
Ka lungmuan theih na’ng inah, Nong sawlna, nong khaamna;
Hong theisak hong ngai sak aw, Ka lungsim hong cing’ aw.

Verse 4
Jesuh aw, nong ciam zo hi, Nangmah a hong zui mi;
Nangma nasem te inah, Nang tawh mang ding, van ah;
Jesuh aw, ka kiciam zo, Sih dong’ Nangmah ka To;
Kong zuih zawh na’ng hong huh in, Ka Lawm, ka Topa in.

Verse 5
Na khekhap, hong mu sak in, Keimah hong tawn sak in;
Kong zuih theih na’ng mantakpi, Nangma tha kong muang hi;
Hong pi, hong sam, hong kai ta’n, A tawp dong hong len ta’n;
Vantung dong’ hong tonpih aw, Hong hon ka Lawmpa aw.

Title: SIHNA GUNGAL KHAT AH (TDM - 436)
Beyond the Swelling Flood [Mang. 21:4]

Verse 1
Jesuh si in hong sawp siang hi, Kikhawm ding sihna gungal ah;
Natna hong tun’n kik ding nai lo, Nuamna thu kikum in om ni;
Sihna gungal khat ah.

Verse 2
Dahna hong tun ding ka lau lo, Upna’n ka mu vantung ka en;
Ka za hi vantung mi’n la sa, Khitui khempeuh Topa’n nul khin;
Sihna gungal khat ah.
 

Chorus
[ Sihna (gungal khat ah) gungal khat ah,]
Verse 3
I tung ding hong nai ta!
[ I tun (ding hong nai ta) ding hong nai ta,]
Verse 3
Sihna gungal khatah.

Verse 3
A it te tawh kikhop nuamna, Bang aw ka za ding hiam tua ah;
Ka lungsim hong vut hong tho sak, Na dang deihna tua mun mang sak;
Sihna gungal khat ah.

Verse 4
Topa hong hilh bangh ka zuihna, Lungdam ka ngah a kicinna;
Khawl ngeilo in ka phat ding hi, Khitui khempeuh hong nul sak hi;
Sihna gungal khat ah.

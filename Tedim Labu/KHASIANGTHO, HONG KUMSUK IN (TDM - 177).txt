Title: KHASIANGTHO, HONG KUMSUK IN (TDM - 177)
Come, Holy Spirit, Heavenly Dove [Job. 4:23]

Verse 1
KhaSiangtho vakhu bangh inah, Hong pai in vanglian tawh;
Na itna mei bangh hong tawh in, A dai ko lungsung ah.

Verse 2
Kote lasak ngei bang inah, Thoto in sa zo lo;
Hosana ci'n ko aw ging lo, Ko te biakna si ta.

Verse 3
Topa tuabang ko si ding maw, Hehpihhuai mahmah ung;
Ka hong itna uh a neu hang, Nang nong itna lian zaw.

Verse 4
KhaSiangtho vakhu bangh inah, Hong pai in vanglian tawh;
Gumpa itna hong buak inla, Kote hong tawhkuang in.

Title: A ZO ZO HI (TDM - 128)
It is Finished [Luka 9:18]

Verse 1
Aneu a lian kua ci lo, Mawh, mawh lo ci lo;
Jesuh'n vekpi ading khin, Sawtpi pek in ah.

Verse 2
Saang pen A tokhom tung pan, Kum in hong sih ciang';
Na khempeuh a vek in zo, A aw ngai dih un.
 

Chorus
A zo zo hi! Takpihi, Vekpi'n zo khin hi;
Hih hi, mawhnei te i sap, Mawhna pulak ding.

Verse 3
Lunggim van pua na seem te'n, Koi ah seem na hiam?
Khawl dih in Amah'n zo zo, Sawtpi pek inah.

Verse 4
Na vangik pi' teng khia in, Jesuh khe kiang khia'n;
Amah bekbek kiang ding in, Na khempeuh cin khin.

Verse 5
Jesuh nasepsa muang in, Na up theih ma teng;
Hanciamna in kimang lo, Sihna hong tun zaw.

Title: GAMLAPI AH MUAL KHAT OM HI (TDM - 123)
There is a Green Hill Far Away [Johan 19:16]

Verse 1
Keima a'ding sih-na a thuak, Khuapi pua lam om a;
Jesuh singlamteh na om hi, Gamla mualpi tungah.

Verse 2
Amah in gimna a thuakna, I thei zo kei ding hi;
Ahih hang komau tang dingin, Amah in a thuak hi.
 

Chorus
Amah'n pha takpi hong it hi, Amah i it kik hi;
A hong gum thei a si um in, Ama na i sem ding.

Verse 3
Ei-te kiphel na ngah dingin, Amah in a si hi;
Asi pha tawh sawpsiang manin, Vantung i tung ding hi.

Verse 4
Khialhna man piak nading inah, Mi dang siangtho lo hi;
Amah bek in vantung khua-ah, A hong lut sak thei hi.

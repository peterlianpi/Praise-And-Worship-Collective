Title: NA HOIHNA THUPHA KIGEN (TDM - 184)
Glorious Things of Thee are Spoken [Late 146:2]

Verse 1
Zion khua kongpi suak dingin, LaiSiangtho ah gen zo hi;
Ciampel ngeilo i Topa in, A om nading hong bawl hi.
Akip tawntung suangpi tung ta, A kiling zo kei ding hi;
Gupna ding thu tawh om khawm a, Galet in kilawm thei hi.

Verse 2
A hong it tawntung na panin, A hong luang hinna luitui;
Tanu tapa te in tua tui, Lungdamta ka dawn uh hi.
Tuabang luitui om ngei hileh, Kuamah thakiam lo ding hi;
Thupha hong pia Pasian bangin, Khang khat pan' khang khat kip hi.

Verse 3
Mihing omna kimkot tengah, Meii le meikhuam hong langh sak;
Hong ompihna lah na'ng inah, A vangliatna hong mu sak.
Amau' an ding manna pia a, Amau' thumna Aza hi;
Hosana, ci-a kiphatna, Vantung dong in kiza hi.

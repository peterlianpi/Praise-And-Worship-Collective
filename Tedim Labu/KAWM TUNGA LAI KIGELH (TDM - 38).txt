Title: KAWM TUNGA LAI KIGELH (TDM - 38)
The Handwriting on the Wall [Dan. 5:5]

Verse 1
Belshazzar in a gam sung, milian tul khat kai khawm hi, A siangtho khamhai te la in, zu dawn na in zangh uh hi;
Kumpipa' innpi sungah, a gualnop laitak un ah, 'Oi leh bangh cihna ahi tam?' en vaw, khut khat, kawm tungah!

Verse 2
Daniel zong hangsan tak in, mipi mai ah hong ding hi, Kiphasak kumpipa taai in, a khialhna te pulak hi;
'Na vekpi un thuak nu teh, 'ci'n ki gelh hi, lai sung ah, 'Na kumpi gam a bei hi ta, 'ci'n ki gelh hi, kawm tungah!
 

Chorus
Pasian khut in gelh, kawm tungah! (lai gelh kawm tung ah!) Pasian khut in gelh kawm tungah! (lai gelh kawm tung ah)
'Tawi cing kei teh' hong ci kha diam? 'cing mah teh' hong ci zaw diam? Hia khut in gelh lai hi, kawm tung ah! (lai gelh kawm tung-ah!)

Verse 3
Daniel in upna tak tawh, thuman gen ngam, sem ngam hi, KhaSiangtho piak hi, tua thusim, A mah hat sak, thei sak hi;
Judea gam a inn sung leh, a sal taanna inn sung ah, Amah in a khiatna thei khin, Pasian lai gelh kawm tungah!

Verse 4
Ei tatna te zongh, ciam teh in ki gelhgelh hi, Jesuh hong sawl bangbang mang in, A ma kiang va zuan meng ni;
Thukhen ni hong nai zo hi, hong tung ding mi teng tung ah, Mawhneite' thuak ding gimna lian, hong ki gelh ding, kawm tungah!

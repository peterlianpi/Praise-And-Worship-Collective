Title: ZANKIM GAMDAIH KIM LAITAKIN (TDM - 83)
It Came Upon the Midnight Clear [Luka 8:14]

Verse 1
Zankim gam daih kim laitak in, Hong ging vanglian la pi;
Vantung mite hong kumsuk in, Kham gawsem tawh sa hi;
'Vantung Kumpipa kiang pan in, Nopna leitung dim hen;'
Ci'n hong sak laitak leitung mi'n, Dai khipkhep in ngai hi.

Verse 2
Van kong pan hong pai lailai mah, Kilemna kha zaak in;
Gimna dimna hih leitung ah, Van la ngaih kiza lai;
Dahna lungkhamna kuam tung ah, Hong leeng diaidiai uh a;
Pau nam tampi gamlum lak ah, Vantung la pha hong sa.

Verse 3
Leitung nuntak vangik nuai ah, Vangik lel a gim te,
Nuntak haksa lam to a kah, Pheiphung suan kha mi aw;
En un, lungdam nuam nading hun, Manlang va bang hong leeng;
Gimna lam gei ah na khawl un, Vantung la ngai dih un.

Verse 4
Thei un, hong tung pet mah ding ta, Tang kamsang te gen sa;
A kum a kha hong veei ding a, Hong tung ding kham hun pha;
Leitung ah nopna lemna pha, a vaang hong taang ding ta;
Vantung mi te hong sak la pha, Leitung mi'n dawng ding ta.

Title: KA UPPA KA THEI HI (TDM - 185)
I Know Whom I have Believed [II Tim. 1:12]

Verse 1
Pasian hong hehpihna thupha, Kei kiang hong tun theihna;
Ka kilawm lohpi hong deihna, Banghang hiam thei zo ke'ng.

Verse 2
Hong honkhia zo thei hih upna, Kei tung' A hong' piakna;
Athu um leng lungnop theihna, Bangci banghiam thei ke'ng.
 

Chorus
Ahihhang ka upPa ka thei hi, Amah muanhuai hi,
ke'n ka thei zo hi; Ama tung ka piaksa te zongh,
Keem zo ding hi, tuani dong'.

Verse 3
KhaSiangtho in mi lungsim hei, Mawhna hong phawksak thei;
Jesuh hong musak, umsak thei, Bangci banghiam thei ke'ng.

Verse 4
Topa mai ke'n ka muh ma in, Apha asia tuah ding;
Gentheih nading, nopsak nading, Bang ka sunsiah thei ke'ng.

Verse 5
Topa cikciang hong pai ding hiam? Ka sih khit hizaw diam?
Vankim laiah ka dawn zaw diam? Bangmah kei in thei ke'ng.

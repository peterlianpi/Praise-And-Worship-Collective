Title: HONG MASUAN IN KHUAVAK AW (TDM - 44)
Lead, Kindly Light [Late 5:8]

Verse 1
Kiim kot khuamialna a tang Jesuh aw, Hong ma suan in; Ka inn pan in gam la pi ah ka om, Lam hong hilh in;
Kei ma khe hong kepsak in, gamlapi, Muh ding ngen lo, kal khat bek pha zo ta.

Verse 2
Tua bangin thungetna, ni dang lai in, om ngei lo hi; Ka teel sa lam ka zuih hang in tu ni, Nong kha ding hi;
Kisaktheihna ding in ka deih kei mah'n, Ka kiphasak, Ka mawhna ngaihsun ke'n.

Verse 3
Sawtpi Nangmah'n thupha nong piak manin, Hong pia lai in; Gam kuam, mualkal, tuipi kheng Topa in, Lam hong hilh ta;
Khuavak ciangin sauveipi ka it mi, Lungdam in ah, kei mah'n ka um ding hi.

Title: JESUH NANGMA DEIH BANG (TDM - 373)
My Jesus, As Thou Wilt [Matt. 26:39]

Verse 1
Jesuh, Nangma deih bangh, Keimah’n zongh deih nuam ing;
Nangma itna khut sung, Hong ki ap khin nuam ing.
Dahna nuamna sungah, Nangmah’n hong tonpih in;
Ci zo nuam ing, Topa, “Nang deih bangh hi sak in.”

Verse 2
Jesuh, Nangma deih bangh, Luankhi’n liah zel zongin;
Ka upna aksi tang, Mual sak, mang sak kei in.
Nang zongh hih leitung ah, Na dahna kah manin;
Nang tawh kap ding inah, “Nang deih bangh hi sak in.”

Verse 3
Jesuh, Nangma deih bangh, Kei ading pha khin hi;
Mailam a piang bangbang, Nangmah kong ap khin hi.
Lung patau lo keimah, Tangtak ka zuan van inn;
Hin lai, sih ciang’, ka la, “Nang deih bangh hi sak in.”

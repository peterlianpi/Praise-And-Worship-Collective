Title: HONG SIANGSAK TUIKHUK (TDM - 138)
The Cleansing Fountain [Zech. 13:1]

Verse 1
En in, thuk in a zai tuikhuk, A luang tawntung tui khuk;
Vang nih pan in hong luangkhia a,
Vuk bangh hong ngo sak ding, Vuk bangh hong ngo sak ding.

Verse 2
Kalvary khuam tungah Lungkham, Natna thuak in hong si;
Lamdang sisan guntui hong luang,
Vuk bangh hong ngo sak ding, Vuk bangh hong ngo sak ding.
 

Chorus
Hong pai un tuikhuk hong luang hi, tuni'n,
Hong pai khempeuh man ngen lo in, Na mawh hong sawp sak ding.

Verse 3
Mi khempeuh dam nading hong luang, Tua thupha tuikhuk pan!
Upna tawh A si sung bual leng,
Vuk bangh hong ngo sak ding, Vuk bangh hong ngo sak ding.

Verse 4
Lungdamna thupha na zak ciang, Hong paiin, ci'n hong sam;
Upna tawh Asi sung bual leng,
Vuk bangh hong ngo sak ding, Vuk bangh hong ngo sak ding.

Title: HING HI (TDM - 142)
He Lives [Gal. 6:14]

Verse 1
Kabiak a hing kik Honpa tu'n leitung ah om a,
Mite'n bangbang gen taleh A hin lam ka thei ta;
Hong hehpihna kathei aa A aw ka zaza hi,
Amah ka kisap laitak hong nai den hi.

Verse 2
Ka kiim ka paam leitung teng A hong itna dim a,
Lungmang' launa ka tuah ciang' A ang sung ka beel a;
Huihpi nun ciang' A khut in kei hong len gige hi,
Amah hong ki lat ding ni hong tung ding hi.
 

Chorus
 [spirited]
Hing hi, (hing hi) hing hi, (hing hi) tu ni'n Honpa hing hi,
Hong tonpih a, hong hopih hi, ka paina khempeuh ah;
Hing hi, (hing hi) hing hi, (hing hi) mihing Honkhia pa hi,
Bang ci theih na hiam nong cih-leh, kalung sungah hing hi.

Verse 3
Nuam un, nuam un! Jesuh' mi, sa un lungdamna la,
Hallelujah ci unla phat un Khris Kumpi pa;
Amah a zong te' Muan pa a gim te Huhpa hi,
Tua bang itna lian a nei mi omlo hi.

Title: TU KA KHANGNO LAI UN (TDM - 389)
Now in the Days of Youth [Thu. 11:9]

Verse 1
Tu ka khangno lai in, Hun hoih zat theih lai in,
Topa ko lungsung hong om in, Vekpi’n hong kipia ung;
Kong ki piakna bangun, Ki lawm bangh hong sang in,
Nuntak sung teng sepna peuh ah, Pa deihna bangh hi hen.

Verse 2
Hong hilh in Na deih bangh, Ka gamtat theih na’ng uh,
Nang deih bangbang gamtat theih na’ng, Ka lungdam ding uh hi;
A lianpen teel tuanlo, A neupen paai tuanlo,
Ko sepna te Nang sep bangin, Hong thupi khin sak in.

Verse 3
Thu hoih thupha deihna, Kote hong hilhhilh in,
Hun tomcikno khat sung bek zongh, Ka siat khak loh na’ng uh;
Ko tha tawh din zawh na’ng, Nang hong panpih ta in,
A siangthona te bek deih ung, A sia te deih kei ung.

Verse 4
Ka suah tung’ bangmah un, KhaSiangtho hong pi in,
Itna gamtat hoih neih theih na’ng, Lungnop hithiatna tawh;
Kum tampi sung man ding, Ka kigin kholh na’ng uh,
Tawntung nuntakna hun nuampi, Ka zong den ding uh hi.

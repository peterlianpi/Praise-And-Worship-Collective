Title: LUNGDAM THU TAANG SAK IN (TDM - 408)
Send the Gospel Light [Late 43:3]

Verse 1
Tangsak in, tangsak mengmeng in, Tuipi kilok kantan in;
Hotkhiatna thu zeel sak mengin, Migi Gumkhia Pa’ min in.

Verse 2
Tangsak in, kha te’ sihna ah, Tua zan khuamialna munah;
Hat meng in hun te beibei veh! A nai, a nai in, bei ta.
 

Chorus
Tang sak in, tang sak mengmeng in, Tuipi gal, tuikulh te dong;
Tangthu lamdang va zasak in, itna, hehpihna khawnkhong.

Verse 3
Tangsak in, Topa’n hong sawl hi, A thu siangtho lim ngaih dih!
“Paikhia in, ka thu va gen in, Hong ompih den ning,” a cih.

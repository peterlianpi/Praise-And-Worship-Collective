Title: NGAI UN! VANTUNG MI'N LASA (TDM - 84)
Hark! the Herald Angels sing [Luka 2:14]

Verse 1
Ngai un! vantung mi'n lasa, A suak Topa minthanna;
Leitung nuamna hong khangsak, Mawhna khempeuh phel ding ci.
Leitungmi khempeuh te in, Lungdamna tawh tho un la;
Jesuh Betlen ah suak hi, Vanpi dong ah minthang hi;

Verse 2
Jesuh Topa Pasian Ta, A om tawntung Kumpipa;
Nungak sung pan hong suak hi, Mi bangh inah hong piang hi.
Pasian in mi bangh piang a, Pasian itna mi sungah;
Ka hong khawlkhawm sak nuam ci, Emmanuel Jesuh hi;
 

Chorus
Vantungmite'n lasa hi, 'A suak Topa min thang,' ci.

Verse 3
Dawn un kilemna Kumpi, athuman Ni hong tang hi;
Nuntakna le khuavakna, Hong puak suk khawm cidamna.
A vangliatna nusiat in, Eite sih loh nadingin;
Nihvei suak sak ding hong tuak, Hong phongto dingin hong suak;

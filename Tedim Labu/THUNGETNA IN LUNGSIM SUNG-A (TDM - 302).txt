Title: THUNGETNA IN LUNGSIM SUNG-A (TDM - 302)
Prayer Is the Soul’s Sincere Desire [Efesa 6:18]

Verse 1
Thungetna in lungsim sunga, Lunggulh takna ahi;
Meibang hong lumsak awm sunga, Deihna lianpen ahi.

Verse 2
Khuisatna, khitui luanna zongh, Thungetna mah ahi;
Pasian’ lamet tohna bek zong, Thungetna mah ahi.

Verse 3
Mawhnei mi khat lungsim tak tawh, Kisik in thum peuh leh;
Vantung mite’n nuam in kiko, “En vaw, thungen ta veh.”

Verse 4
Thuzui ta ding thungetna in, Hinna hu bangh ahi;
Thungetna tawh sihkong pan in, Vantung khua ah lut hi.

Verse 5
Nangmah, hinna thuman lampi, Pa kiang tun nading in;
Nangma tot sa thunget lampi, Kote zongh hong hilh in.

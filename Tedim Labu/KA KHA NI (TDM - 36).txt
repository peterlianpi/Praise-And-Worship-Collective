Title: KA KHA NI (TDM - 36)
Sun of my Soul [Luke 24:29]

Verse 1
Hong gum Topa ni taang bangh hi, Ama kiang khuamial ngei lo hi;
Topa aw, Na mai kong muh na'ng, Meiilum kai te na theng sak in.

Verse 2
Topa, ka lung kham ciang in ah, Mit gui in a kham luat lai ah;
Topa aw,Nang ma ang sung ah, Ka tawl ka hong dam sak nuam ta.

Verse 3
Topa aw, Nang nong om kei leh, Sih ding ka kih ta keimah in;
Keimah tawh a omkhawm ding in, Ni sim in Topa hong pai hi.

Verse 4
Tu lai a Na thu hong sel a, Thupha bel hong nialte in ah;
Nung ciangin a khialh loh na'ng in, Lam a khial te na hehpih in.

Verse 5
Leitung sihna ka tuah ma un, Thupha hong pia'n ka phawk lai un;
Tuipi bang thuk Na itna sung, Hong tun pih in, Na van inn sung.

Verse 6
Nitak a a thum a taute, Ci dam lo a, a zawngkhal te;
Na thupha kong na hon'in la, A lung na dam sak in, Topa.

Title: HONG SUPLAWH ZEN MAW! (TDM - 72)
Love Held Him to the Cross [Ef. 5:2]

Verse 1
Singlamteh tung sihna pan, Ciktui phulsak zo Topa'n, Ka mawhna khempeuh hong sawpsiang ta (sawp siang ta)
Tel zawhloh itna thuk tawh, Van tokhom nusia ta maw! Suut zawh ngei ding hi kei hong itna (hong itna)

Verse 2
Lungkhamna'n hong tuum laitak, Jesuh'n ka van hong puaksak, Kua dang kei tangin si ngam ding maw (ngam ding maw)
Ka nuntak sung phat den ning, Lungdam in la sak den ning, Lamdang hehpihna'n hong honkhia maw! (honkhia maw)
 

Chorus
A tul gemgam sing tungah, Khut le khe khet a thuakna, Keimah bek hong it ma'n hi zen maw! (hi zen maw!)
Kei tang sihna hong thuak ma'n Suahtakna ka ngah thei pan, Jesuh bekin hong suplawh zen maw! (sum zen maw!)

Verse 3
Mawhna teng sawp siang zo a, A Kha ka sung hong teng ta, Nisim ka kha tawh teci pang hi (hong pang hi)
Khualmi bang ka vakthap hang, Ka inn nuam nai semsem tang, Minthanna dim in ka mu ding hi (mu ding hi)

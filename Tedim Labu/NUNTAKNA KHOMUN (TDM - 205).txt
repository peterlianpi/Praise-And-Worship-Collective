Title: NUNTAKNA KHOMUN (TDM - 205)
Break Thou the Bread of Life [Johan 6:35]

Verse 1
Nuntakna khomun kei, Hong pia'n, Topa;
Galile tuili gei, Na hih bangh mah;
Ka lungsimvah zo kei, Laisimnatawh;
Nangmah tak ka lungdeih, Hinna Thu aw.

Verse 2
Thuman thutak hong pia'n, Ka Topa aw;
Khomun Na piak bangzia'n, Na thupha tawh;
Tua hi leh kol teng kia'n, Ka suakta ding;
Thuman in hong khahkhia'n, Ka lungnuam ding.

Verse 3
Kei ading Topa Nang, Nuntak khomun;
Keimah ka suahtak na'ng, Nangma Thu gun;
Nang tawh vantung luah na'ng, Nong vaak ding hi;
Hong it sak in thuman, Itna Na hi.

Verse 4
Topa aw, KhaSiangtho, Kei hong puak in;
Keima mit khua mu lo, Vaksak dingin;
Na thu thuk LaiSiangtho, Hong telsak ding;
Na Thu sung pan Nang, ko, Hong musak ding.

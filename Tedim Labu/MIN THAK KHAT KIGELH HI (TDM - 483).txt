Title: MIN THAK KHAT KIGELH HI (TDM - 483)
A New Name In Glory [Heb. 12:23]

Verse 1
Khatvei laiin mawhnei kahi a,
Mawhmai na’ng Topa ka zuat leh:
Hong kipia khong hilhial nahi a,
A kammal kip den hiveh (den hiveh).

Verse 2
Pasian’ hehna thuak ding launa tawh,
Singlamteh phung ka khukdin a,
Vantung te kihong in ka mit tawh
Ka min ki gelh ka mu ta! (ka mu ta)
 

Chorus
Vangam sungah min thak khat kigelh hi,
Kei’ min hi (Kei min hi) keima min hi! (kei’ min hi!)
Puanpak silh vantungmi tein la sa hi,
Mimawh khat hong’ tung’ ci; (hong’ tung’ ci)
Vantung gam sungah min thak khat kigelh hi,
Kei min hi, (kei min hi) Keima min hi! (kei min hi!)
Ka mawhna mai zo a, vantung lam ka zuan ta! Nuam hithiat zen si!

Verse 3
Hehpihna hang’ gupna ki ngah ta,
Thubu sung ka mu nuam mah sing!
Tel zo ing, ka mawhna mai zo ta!
Sisan hang’ damsiang zo ing (siang zo ing).

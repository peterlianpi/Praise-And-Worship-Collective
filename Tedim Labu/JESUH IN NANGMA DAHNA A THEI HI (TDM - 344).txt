Title: JESUH IN NANGMA DAHNA A THEI HI (TDM - 344)
Jesus knows thy' Sorrow [Isaiah 53:4]

Verse 1
Dahna a om khempeuh, Topa Jesuh’n thei;
Lungsim kisikna le, Thungetna zongh thei;
Lau keei loin um in, Na lung khauhsak in;
Na van Topa suan in, hong nuam sak ding hi.

Verse 2
Ama lungsim sungah, Nang pha hongsa hi;
Na galte khut sung pan, Nang hong gum ding hi;
Ama deihna bangin, Lam hong hilh ding hi;
A vaang taanna lamah na tung thei ding hi.

Verse 3
Gal nado Jesuh’n thei, Na thumna za hi;
Na lunggim lam zongh thei, Na kiko za hi;
Na kha thakhauh na’ng in, Na tai loh na’ng in;
Lungsim nuam hong pia ding na khitui nul ding.

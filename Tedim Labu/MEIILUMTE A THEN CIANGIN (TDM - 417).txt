Title: MEIILUMTE A THEN CIANGIN (TDM - 417)
When the Mists have Rolled Away [Solomon la 2:17]

Verse 1
A siangtho mahmah mual tungah, Meiilum a then ciang inah;
Ni sa a vang taanna vakpha, Cik le luineu te tung tu’n;
Sakhituihup avangin ah, Itna pha lai kisim ta;
Eite kimu dingin a hun, Meiilum a then ciang inah.

Verse 2
Mihing te khuamial sungah khial, Leitungmi ahih mangngilh;
Phat nading a hahkatna a, Kiitna thupha pai khia;
Nopsak na’ng Topa’n hong phel ding, Eite khempeuh hong liah ding;
I ngak dingin zingsang hun hi, Meiilum te a theng ding hi.
 

Chorus
Eite i (Eite i) kimu ding hi (kimu ding hi),
Leitung ah (leitung ah) kimuh bangin (kimuh bangin);
Tua thupha le lungdamna ni, Zingsang a phalvak ciang ah;
Eite kimu dingin a hun, Meiilum a then ciang inah.

Verse 3
Meiilum te ei khempeuh tungah, A leentoh laitak inah,
I it khempeuh i lawmte tawh, Eite i kimu ding maw;
Khua mialcipna gungal khat ah, Khuavak nitang hong tung ta;
A lamdang khuavak i muhna, Meiilum a then ciang inah.

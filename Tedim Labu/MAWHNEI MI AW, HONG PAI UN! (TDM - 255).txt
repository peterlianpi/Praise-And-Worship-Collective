Title: MAWHNEI MI AW, HONG PAI UN! (TDM - 255)
Come, Sinner, Come! [Mat. 11:28]

Verse 1
Jesuh’n hong sam, hong pai in! Mawhnei mi aw;
Thu kong ngetsak, hong pai in! Mawhnei mi aw.
Jesuh na neih hun zo ta, Mawhnei mi aw;
Amah na theih hun zo ta, Hong pai meng in.

Verse 2
Na vangik tawh hong pai in! Mawhnei mi aw;
Jesuh zuan ding hong pai in! Mawhnei mi aw.
Jesuh’n nang hong khem lo ding, Mawhnei mi aw;
Jesuh’n nang hong sang lai ding, Hong pai meng in.

Verse 3
Hong sap ngai in, hong pai in! Mawhnei mi aw;
Thupha sang ding hong pai in! Mawhnei mi aw.
Jesuh’n nang hong samsam lai, Mawhnei mi aw;
Thu hong ki nget sak lailai, Mawhnei mi aw.

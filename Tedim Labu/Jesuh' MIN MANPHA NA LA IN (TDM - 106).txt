Title: Jesuh' MIN MANPHA NA LA IN (TDM - 106)
Take the Name of Jesus With You [Late 72:19]

Verse 1
Jesuh min manpha nala in, Dahna gimna ta te aw;
Hehnep nuamna hong pia dingin, Napaina peuh ah keng aw.

Verse 2
Jesuh min na kem tawntung in, Gal namkim kidalna lum;
Ziaetna'n hong kimkot ciangin, Amin siangtho suangin thum.
 

Chorus
Min manpha, (Min manpha) ngaih mahmah! (ngaih mahmah!)
Leitung mite' lametna;
Min manpha, (Min manpha) ngaih mahmah, (mahmah)
Vantung ah lungdam nuamna.

Verse 3
Jesuh min a manpha mahmah, Bangzah i lung hong dam sak;
Hong san ciang' Ama ang sungah, Ama la i sak laitak.

Verse 4
Lam tawp vantung i tun ciangin, Jesuh khe ah i bok ding;
Kumpi khempeuh tung Kumpi ci'n, Ama maiah i bia ding.

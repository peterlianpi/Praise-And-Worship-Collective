Title: KEI HONG PHAWK IN (B) (TDM - 118)
(B)
Remember Me [Jer. 15:15]

Verse 1
Keihong Honpa A si luang in, Ka Kumpi hong si maw?
Tang tel bangh bek kei mah ading, A lutang hong zangh maw?

Verse 2
Keima gitlohna te hangin, Singlamteh tung' tau maw;
Lamdang hehpihna le itna, Bang tawh teh thei ding maw.
 

Chorus
Nang tung kong thumaan zawh na'ng in, Topa kei hong huh in;
Kumpi tokhom Na luah ciangin, Kei hong mangngilh kei in.

Verse 3
Ama piansak mi' mawhna hang', A piangsak Pa Khris;
Hong sihlaitak, khuamial sungah, Ni a buk mah ding hi.

Verse 4
Kei zongh singlamteh ka muh ciang', Zum in ka kiseel diam;
Lungdamna tawh lung nehno in, Ka khi ka luang sak diam.

Verse 5
Ahihhang khitui taakna in, Itna loh khin zo kei;
Topa ka pumpi kong pia hi, Hih kan ka hih thei kei.

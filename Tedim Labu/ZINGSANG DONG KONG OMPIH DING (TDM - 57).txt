Title: ZINGSANG DONG KONG OMPIH DING (TDM - 57)
I'll Stand by Until the Morning [John 6:19-21]

Verse 1
A tum dek teembaw kimkot ah, Huihpi in seem hi;
Huih in len a kisia dek hi, Tuipi thuk sungah.

Verse 2
Lamet bei teembaw nasem te, Lungsim thaneem hi;
Topa sapna aw za uh a, Lungdam khin uh hi.

Verse 3
Tuipi tung huih nun phat lai ah, Teembaw tum dek hi;
A gum ding Jesuhb vapai a, Lungman damsak hi.

Verse 4
Nang hong it manin hong kum a, Nang tang ding si hi;
Siatna paai in a thu um in, Inn na tung ding hi.
 

Chorus
Hong gum Topa ko in, Hong thu piak ngai in
Zingsang mateng kong ompih ding,
Lau ke'n hong gum ding kong pai hi;
Hi hi, zingsang mateng ompih ding,
Lau ke'n hong gum ding kong pai hi. (kong pai hi)

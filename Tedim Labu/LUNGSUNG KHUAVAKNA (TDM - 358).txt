Title: LUNGSUNG KHUAVAKNA (TDM - 358)
Sunshine in the Soul [2 San. 23:4]

Verse 1
Tuni in keima lungsim sungah, Khuavak a hongtaang hi;
Leitung ni sang avang tang zaw, Ka khuavak Jesuh hi.

Verse 2
Tuni in keima lungsim sungah, Kumpi phatna lasa;
Kei saktheih loh la ngaihte zongh, Jesuh’n ngai leh a za.
 

Chorus
Aw ka lung (sung khuavakna) sung,
lung khuavakna, (Aw ka lung sung khuavakna)
Lung sung nopna nuamna hunpha(nuamna hunpha hi)hi;
Jesuh mai nuam ka muh ciangin,
Ka lungsim sung hong tang hi.

Verse 3
Tuni in keima lungsim sungah, Topa hong tum’ manin,
Lungnopna le gamtatna pha, Hong po, hong palh pah hi.

Verse 4
Tuni in keima lungsim sungah, Nuamna, lawpna dim hi;
Hong piaksa le hong piak lai ding, Thupha, nuamna hang hi.

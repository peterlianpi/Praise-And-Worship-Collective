Title: NA KONG ZAI HON IN (TDM - 226)
Open Wide the Door! [Mang. 3:20]

Verse 1
Jesuh'n kong kiu nang hong sam, 'Lunggim mi'n hong zuan un,' ci;
Hong gum thei Amah bek hi, Na (kongza) kong za hon in.

Verse 2
Jesuh'n kong kiu hong gum ding, Nang ading A pumpi pia;
Hing kik in han pan tho khia, Na (kongza) kong za hon in.

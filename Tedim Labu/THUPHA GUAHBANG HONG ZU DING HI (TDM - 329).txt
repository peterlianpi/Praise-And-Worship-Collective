Title: THUPHA GUAHBANG HONG ZU DING HI (TDM - 329)
There shall be Showers of Blessing [Ezek. 34:26]

Verse 1
“Thupha guahbang hong zu ding hi:”
Itna tawh hong ciam bangin;
Thamalhna hun hong tung ding hi, Hongpuak suk ding Topa in.

Verse 2
“Thupha guahbang hong zu ding hi:”
Thahat kikna a manpha;
Hong ging zuazua ding hi tampi, Mualtung, guamsung, mun tengah.
 

Chorus
Thupha guah zu bangh, Thupha guah i kisam hi;
Hehpihna mal hong kiat tei hang, Hong zusuk hen a tampi.

Verse 3
“Thupha guahbang hong zu ding hi:”
Hong buak sukin Topa aw;
Thathak tu’in Nong guan ding hi, Nong ciamsa hong tungsak aw.

Verse 4
“Thupha guahbang hong zu ding hi:”
Tuni’n hong zu taleh maw;
Jesuh kiangah thu i ngen hi, Pulak in Pa kiang i mawh.

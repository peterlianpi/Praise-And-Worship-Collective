Title: HONPA LUT SAK IN! (TDM - 240)
Let the Savior In! [Mang. 3:20]

Verse 1
Zin pa kong kiangah om hi,
Lut (Honpa, Lutsak in!) sak in! (Honpa, Lutsak in)
Tamveipi hong painzo hi:
Lut (Honpa, lutsak in ) sak in (Honpa, lutsak in)
A pai ma'n lut na'ng pia in, Siangtho Topa lutsak in;
Pa Pasian a Tapa hi,
Lut (Honpa, Lutsak in) sak in. (Honpa, Lutsak in)

Verse 2
Lungsim kongkhak tu'n hon in,
Lut (Honpa, Lutsak in!) sak in! (Honpa, Lutsak in)
Ngak leh Amah paikhia ding:
Lut (Honpa, lutsak in) sak in! (Honpa, lutsak in)
Lutsak in nangma lawmpa, Nangma lungsim kem dinga;
A tawp dong hong gum ding pa,
Lut (Honpa, Lutsak in) sak in. (Honpa, Lutsak in)

Verse 3
A kam khumna ngai inla,Lut (Honpa, Lutsak in) sak in! (Honpa, Lutsak in)
Tu'n piangsak in nang teel pa:
Lut (Honpa, lutsak in) sak in! (Honpa, lutsak in)
Kongkhak kiang ding hi. Amah, Khangsak ding na lungdamna;
Ama min na phat ding a,
Lut (Hon pa, Lutsak in.) sak in.(Honpa, Lutsak in)

Verse 4
Tuin vantung khuazin pa,
Lut (Honpa, Lutsak in) sak in! (Honpa, Lutsak in)
Nang vakna pawi kham dinga:
Lut (Honpa, lutsak in) sak in! (Honpa, lutsak in)
A hong phel ding na mawhna, Lawmte tawh kikhen ciangin;
Hong kha ding vantung inn-ah,
Lut (Honpa, Lutsak in.) sak in.(Honpa, Lutsak in)

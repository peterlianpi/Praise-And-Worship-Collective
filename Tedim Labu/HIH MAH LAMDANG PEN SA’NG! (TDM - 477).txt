Title: HIH MAH LAMDANG PEN SA’NG! (TDM - 477)
The Wonder of it All [Ef. 2: 10]

Verse 1
Nitak a ni a tum lamdang sa ing,
Nisuah zongh lamdang sasem ing;
Ka lungsim hong khoihkha a lamdang pen in,
Pasian in hong itna hi, nuam sing’!
 

Chorus
O, hih mah lamdang pen sa’ng! Hih mah lamdang pen sa’ng!
Pasian in kei hong it zenzen!
O, hih mah lamdang pen sa’ng! Hih mah lamdang pen sa’ng!
Pasian in kei hong it zenzen!

Verse 2
Tukhun anlak hun te lam dang si ee,
Vantung aksi ni te zongh ee;
Ka lungsim hong khoihkha a lamdang pen in,
Hih ka phawk thak hong itna thu hi.

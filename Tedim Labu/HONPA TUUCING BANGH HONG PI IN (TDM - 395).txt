Title: HONPA TUUCING BANGH HONG PI IN (TDM - 395)
Saviour, like a Shepherd Lead us [Late 23:1]

Verse 1
Honpa, tuucing bangh hong pi in, Nong huaina ko kisam pha;
An tam gamnuam ah hong vaak in, Hong bawl kholhsak in omna.
Thupha Jesuh, Thupha Jesuh, Nong lei zo kote Nangaa;
Thupha Jesuh, Thupha Jesuh, Nong lei zo, kote Nangaa.

Verse 2
Hong pawl in, Nang neihsa kote, Ko paina lampi cingin;
Hong kem, hong hu in, Na tuu te, Lam khialh ciang’ hong zongkik in.
Thupha Jesuh, Thupha Jesuh, Ko thungetna hong za in;
Thupha Jesuh, Thupha Jesuh, Ko thungetna hong za hi.

Verse 3
Mizawng, mimawh kahi uh hang’, Nong ciam zo hong’ sang dingin;
Mawhna phel na’ng sian na'ng dal na’ng, Hehpihna Na nei cing in.
Thupha Jesuh, Thupha Jesuh, Nang lam hong kihei sak in;
Thupha Jesuh, Thupha Jesuh, Nang lam hong kihei sak in.

Verse 4
Ka neulai un, Nangma pahtak, Na awi bangh hong ta sak in;
Hong hon Topa pha aw, dim tak, Nangma itna hong guan in.
Thupha Jesuh, Thupha Jesuh, Nong it zo, hong it lai in;
Thupha Jesuh, Thupha Jesuh, Nong it zo, hong it lai in.

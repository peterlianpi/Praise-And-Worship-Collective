Title: JESUH TAWH MUN KHEMPEUH AH(A) (TDM - 331)
Anywhere with Jesus [Late 37:5]

Verse 1
Zesuh tawh mun khempeuh ka paikhawm ding hi,
Kikhen lo in hong khakna ka zui ding hi;
A khe kiang ah tu leng launa omlo a,

Verse 2
Mun khempeuh kei guak hi keng, Jesuh tawh hi’ng,
Lawmte’n nusiat hangin Jesuh keia’ hi;
Lam haksat hang, A khut tawh hong kha ding a,

*Jesuh tawh mun khempeuh ka pai khawm ding hi.
 

Chorus
Paikhawm ding, mun khempeuh launa om thei kei;
Jesuh tawh mun khempeuh ka pai khawm ding hi.

Verse 3
Jesuh tawh mun khempeuh kei ka lum thei a,
Sihna a liim in hong kiimkot hang inah;
Ka thawh ciang’ kawi vak nawnlo ding ka thei hi,

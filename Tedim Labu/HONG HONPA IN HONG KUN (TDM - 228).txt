Title: HONG HONPA IN HONG KUN (TDM - 228)
I Hear the Saviour Say [Isai. 12:2]

Verse 1
Hong honpa in hong kun, 'Na thahatna neu hi;Thanem te'n thu hong ngen un, Cingtakna ngah ding,' hong ci.

Verse 2
Topa tu'n ka thei zo, Nangma vanglian bek in;Phak le lol zong damsak zo, Suang lung nem sak tui bangin.

Verse 3
Hehpih ka ngah nading, Phatna khat zongh nei keng;Ka puan vuk bangh paak nading, Tuuno si sung hong sawp leng.

Verse 4
Sihna lupna tung pan, Ka kha a kahto ding;'Jesuh in kei mah hong tan,' ci-in lungdam kiko ding.

Verse 5
Topa tokhom maiah, Kicing in ka din ciang;Min phatna puak ning amah, Nga suk ning Jesuh' khe kiang.
 

Chorus
Jesuh'n loh khin ta, Ama tung ka ba;
Si bangh san sa ka mawhna, Vuk bangh hong sawp ngo ta.

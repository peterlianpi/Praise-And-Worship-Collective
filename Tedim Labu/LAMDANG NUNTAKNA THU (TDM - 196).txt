Title: LAMDANG NUNTAKNA THU (TDM - 196)
Wonderful Words of Life [Johan 6:63]

Verse 1
Kei hong sakkik leuleu dihin, Lamdang Nuntakna thu!
A hoihna hong lak lailai in, Lamdang Nuntakna thu!
Nuntakna thupha hi, Gamtatna'ng hong hilh hi!

Verse 2
Jesuh'n mi khempeuh hong lui hi, Lamdang Nuntakna thu!
Hong sam hi, ngai un, mawhnei mi, Lamdang Nuntakna thu!
Hong piakhong hih thugol, Vantung khua lam' hong zol!
 

Chorus
[Thu hoih mahmah! thupha mahmah! Nuntakna thu mahmah!]

Verse 3

Mawh phel, lungnopna, mi ko in, Lamdang Nuntakna thu!

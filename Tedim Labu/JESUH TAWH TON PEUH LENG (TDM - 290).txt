Title: JESUH TAWH TON PEUH LENG (TDM - 290)
If Jesus Goes With Me [II Kor. 5:8]

Verse 1
Launa namkim omna mualkuam thuk sung a hi zong,
Lung nuam a ka omna nitang khuavak sung hi zong;
Khuasia khuapha ta leh thu hoih khat ken thei ing,
Jesuh tawh ton peuh leng koimah peuh pai ning.

Verse 2
Sehnel nisa kan in mawhna, gal le sa lakah,
Nuntak thupha puak in ka pai ding phamawh hi a;
Ka mel bangbang pua in tuamun ah ka pai ding,
Jesuh tawh ton peuh leng koimah peuh pai ning.
 

Chorus
Jesuh tawh ton leng mun khempeuh, (khempeuh) Ka pai ding,
Ama omna peuh vantung hi pah ding, Kei ka om ding;
A singlamteh ke’n ka pua ding(pua ding), ka hampha ding(phading),
Jesuh tawh ton leng, mun khempeuh ka pai ding.

Verse 3
Mi dang te’n tuipi gal kan in a sep lai takin,
Kei puak ding singlamteh tual sung teng ahi zongin;
A thupiakna mang in upna kip in om ning,
Kei Hong ompihna koimah peuh ah om ning.

Verse 4
Ka Topa’ thupiakna ke’n ka nial ding hi lo hi,
Hong hilhna kammal te ke’n ka zuih ding hi zaw hi;
Inn ah om zaw ding maw khual gamla pai zaw ding,
Honpa tawh hi peuh leng koipeuh lungkim ing.

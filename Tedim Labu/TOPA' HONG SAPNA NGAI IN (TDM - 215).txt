Title: TOPA' HONG SAPNA NGAI IN (TDM - 215)
The Master's Call [Matt. 21:28]

Verse 1
Topa' hong sapna tu in ngai in, Buh at dingin hong sam;
But at ding tampi in hong ngak zo, Hihhang a at ding tawm.

Verse 2
Mawhna khuamial sung om mite kiang, Pai in va khahkhia un;
Pai in mawhna kol va sutkhia un, Mawhna hencip mi kiang.
 

Chorus
Topa min in na mai lam nawt un, Itna deihsakna tawh;
Ei hong Gupkhiatna thupha letsong, Leitung teng thangsak un.

Verse 3
Lunggim mite lungdamna sung ah, Itna tawh va zol un;
Amah nuntak tawntungna khomun, Tua lai ei hong dawn ding.

Verse 4
Kuan in, khuakhal hun bei dekta a, Ni hong tum dek ta hi;
Zan khuamial hun hong tung pak ding a, Nasep hun bei ding hi.

Title: KONGPI KIHONGSA-IN OM A (TDM - 235)
The Gate A jar for Me [Mang 21:25]

Verse 1
Kongpi ki hong sa in om a, A sung pan in, a vang pi;
Singlamteh pan hong tang khia a, Topa' itna hong lak hi.

Verse 2
Hotkhiatna zong mi te khempeuh, Tua kongpi hon, sa'n om hi;
A hau, a zawng, a lian, a neu, A kua, ci lo lut thei hi.
 

Chorus
Hi takpi zo diam tua kongpi Kei-a dingin hon sa'n om maw?
Keimah (Keimah) takpi, (takpi) Keimah hong kihon maw?

Verse 3
Hehpihna kong ki hon lai-in, Nang zong lau ke'n, delh in la;
Itna piak lukhu ngah na'ng in, Singlamteh na sang ding ta.

Verse 4
Leitung ah hong piak singlamteh, Sih gungal ah, i khia ding;
Hinna lukhu hong piak takteh, Amah i it zaw sem ding.

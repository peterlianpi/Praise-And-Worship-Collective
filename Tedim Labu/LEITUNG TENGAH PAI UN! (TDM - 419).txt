Title: LEITUNG TENGAH PAI UN! (TDM - 419)
Go Ye into All the World! [Matt. 28:18-20]

Verse 1
Gamla pi a mawhna khuamial gamsung ah,
Mi tul le then gammang in om uh hi;
Kua pai ding a Gupkhiatna thu va gen ding,
Jesuh en in, gimna ding ngaihsun ke’n.

Verse 2
Khuadak leci’n kong honsa in hong ngak hi,
Khris galkap te aw pai in lut un;
Tha neihzah teng gawm in ki khenthang loin,
Thupha hilh in mawhna kol va sut un.
 

Chorus
Vangliatna kei tung hong pia zo!
Vangliatna kei tung hong pia zo!
Vak kawikawi un la, Lungdam Thu va gen un,
Note tawh ka omkhawm tawntung ding.

Verse 3
“Banghang note si zenzen ding” hongci in,
Pasian hong sapna thu va kosawn un;
Hotkhiatna le hinna na ngah nading in,
Jesuh si in hong tan zo va ci un.

Verse 4
Pa Pasian aw, hong tung sak mengmeng ta in,
Mi namcin in Na min phat nading hun;
Hotkhiatna la lungdamna tawh kiko in,
Hallelujah, Topa Kumpi phat un.

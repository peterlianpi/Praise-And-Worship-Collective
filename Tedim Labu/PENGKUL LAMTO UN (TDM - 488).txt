Title: PENGKUL LAMTO UN (TDM - 488)
Life up the Trumpet

Verse 1
Pengkul lam to un, nakpi’n ging sak un:
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!
Khualzin te kipaak, lungdam la sa un,
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!

Verse 2
Zangkuam te kiko, mual te thawn sak un:
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!
A kigo Tuuno, vanglian in hong kum,
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!
 

Chorus
Hong pai ding hi, hong pai ding hi,
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!

Verse 3
Tuipi te’n zongh tuihual sim ging sak un:
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!
Tuipi gei sehnel tungah kitam un,
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!

Verse 4
Thangvan ngo mei in, mi za ta hilh un:
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!
Huihpi le pingpei te’n zongh zai in suut,
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!

Verse 5
Minam te’ buaina in hong theisak hi:
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!
Pilna khang semsem, sak, khang ah mi zin,
Jesuh hong pai kik ding hi!

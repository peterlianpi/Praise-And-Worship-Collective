Title: TOPA HONG PAI DING (TDM - 165)
The Lord is Coming [Matt. 25:6]

Verse 1
Topa hong pai ding, nung ciangin: Kithawi in hong pai ding!
Van innnuam panin hong pai ding: Kithawi in hong pai ding!
Amah thuman i Topa hi, A teelsa te thupha pia ding;
APa' Kumpi tokhom tu ding: Kithawi in hong pai ding!

Verse 2
Amah leitung hong pai baih ding: Kithawi in hong pai ding!
Gualzawhna ding hun hong tung' ding: Kithawi in hong pai ding!
Hallelujah van pan ging ding, Jesuh'n gupkhiatna hong puak ciang';
Meivak hoih te'n Topa mu ding: Kithawi in hong pai ding!
 

Chorus
Mopipa hong pai ciang' na kithawi diam? (kithawi diam)
Mopipa hong pai ciang' na kithawi diam? (kithawi diam)
Zingsang, sun le zan hi zong, Na meivak te tang pha in,
Mopipa hong pai ciang' na kithawi diam?

Verse 3
En in, nang le kei ading hi: Kithawi in hong pai ding!
A sawtlo in pengkul za ding: Kithawi in hong pai ding!
Ei hong sam ding thukhenni ciang', Gam khempeuh pan mun khempeuh ah;
A kumpi tokhom mai ding ding': Kithawi in hong pai ding!

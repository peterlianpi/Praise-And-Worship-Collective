Title: HONG KHIHNA IN A PHA HI (TDM - 21)
Blest be the Tie that Binds [Matt. 11:30]

Verse 1
Ei lung hong khih nading, Jesuh itna pha hi; Lung kituak a kikholhna in, Vantung itna lim hi.

Verse 2
Ei Pa tokhom mai ah, Nakpi'n thu i ngen hi; Ei lawpna, upna deihna teng, A kibang in om hi.

Verse 3
Ei te'n dahna thuak khawm, Van puak na ki pua hi; Khat veivei i lawmte a' ding, Khitui a luang thei hi.

Verse 4
Ei te ki khen ciang in, Lungsim dahna piang hi; Hih hang i lungsim tawh om khawm, Nungciang ki mu ding hi.

Verse 5
Hih bang lametna in, Lungsim hong kip sak hi; Pasian um a i muan na in, Lungnop ki cin na hi.

Title: KHAM KHUAPI (TDM - 440)
The City of Gold [Mang. 21:18]

Verse 1
Sihna kuam gal khat ah khua lianpi khat om hi,
Min thanna te ki gen khin zo lo;
Tuamun ah ni tum lo, singteh te vul ngeilo,
Tua Pasian kham khuapi hoih sungah.

Verse 2
Tua mun i it Kumpi hong tanpa Topa om,
Thuman mi te’n lungdam en uh hi;
Tuamun ah thuman mi te aksi bangh tang ding,
Tua Pasian kham khuapi hoih sungah.
 

Chorus
Tuamun ah (Tuamun ah) ni tum lo (ni tum lo),
Singteh te (singteh te) vul ngei lo;
Tuamun ah thuman mi’ maitang Topa’n en ding,
Tua Pasian kham khuapi hoih sungah.

Verse 3
Singlamteh phung i tunpih khitsa kha khempeuh,
Tuu buk sung i tutsa tuu khempeuh;
Lukhu zep nading suang manpha bangh kikem hi,
Tua Pasian kham khuapi hoih sungah.

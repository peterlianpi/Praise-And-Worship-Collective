Title: I PA HONG HEHPIHNA MEIVAK (TDM - 407)
Brightly Beams Our Father’s Mercy [Matt. 5:14]

Verse 1
I Topa hong hehpihna in, Suum’ pempam in hong tang hi;
Tua khuavak eite hong pia hi, Tuitung tengah tangsak in.

Verse 2
Mawhna in khua hong mialsak hi, Tuihualpi hong kilok hi;
Hoihtakin khuadak pha inla, Tuidung teng phatak en in.
 

Chorus
Meivak teng a vekpi’n vaak in, Tuihual teng ki muh nading;
A cimawh tuikia om kha ding, Gumkhia inla kaikhia in.

Verse 3
Na meivak phatak vaak inla, Tuihual in mi taai` kha’ ding;
Nop takin suahtak theih nading, Tawlngakna mun tun theih na’ng.

Title: I THEI NADING HI (TDM - 29)
Farther Along [Isa. 35:10]

Verse 1
Buaina haksatna, i tuah ciangin ah, Banghang hi zah ta ke'n thuak se a;
Ka kim ka paam a misia te in lah, Bangmah thuak se lo lamdang ka sa.

Verse 2
It le ngaih te tawh sihna'n hong khen ciang, I inn sung hawm in i lung zuang a;
A gilo mite a dau a pai ciang, Banghang tua ci hiam? lamdang i sa.
 

Chorus
Nung ciang a vek pi'n i thei nading hi. Nung ciang a hang i tel nading hi;
Na lung nuamsak in, ni tang sung om in, Nung ciang a vek pi'n i tel ding hi.

Verse 3
'Sih dong thu man in' Topa in hong ci, Tawlkhat sung ngak in thuak lai le teh;
Na gimna tawlna gen tham lo ding hi, Et lawm kongpi sung na lut tak teh.

Verse 4
Minthanna pi tawh vantung inn pan in, Jesuh hong kum suk i mu ding hi;
A tang innpi sung i om khop ciang in, Tel loh khempeuh i tel ta ding hi.

Title: KHATVEI IN CING KHIN HI (TDM - 263)
Once for All [Eph. 2:8]

Beating Three times to the measure

Verse 1
Thukham sungpan suahtak nuam mahmah hi,
Jesuh in a si tawh a hong sawp hi;
Thukham sung pan ka suahtak nading,
Hong itna tawh hong gum zo hi.

Verse 2
Mawhsakna pan i suakta khin zo a,
Jesuh’n gupna kicing hong pia zo ta;
Awkhum in hong sam: “Hong pai un,” ci,
Khatvei tatna tawh kicing hi.
 

Chorus
Cing ding hi, mawhnei te’n um ta un,
Cing ding hi, u te naute’n um un;
Singlamteh beel leng mawhna kia ding,
Khatvei tatna tawh cing khin hi.

Verse 3
Pasian’ Tapa cihna i ngah zo ta,
A pukte itna tawh hong phong zo ding;
Hong sap ciang sihna pan hingkik ding,
Khatvei tatna tawh kicing hi.

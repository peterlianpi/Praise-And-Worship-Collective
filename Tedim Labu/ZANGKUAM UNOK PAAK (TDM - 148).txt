Title: ZANGKUAM UNOK PAAK (TDM - 148)
The Lily of the Valley [Sol. 2:1]

Verse 1
Deihna khempeuh a kicinna, Lawmpha Jesuh nei ing,
Keia' ding tulsawm tulthen sa'ng hoih zaw hi;
Zangkuam Unok Paak zah a hoih, Amah bek ken mu ing,
Dam na'ng, sian na'ng, ka deih teng A sung om hi.
Dahhun ciang' hong hehnem in, haksat ciang' bukna hi,
Na vanggik khempeuh hong suan in hong ci;
(Hallelujah) Amah Zangkuam Unok Paak hi, ataang Zingsol zongh hi,
Keia' ding tulsawm tulthen sa'ng hoih zaw hi.

Verse 2
Kalung khamna teng lakhia in, dahna hong puak sak a,
Ziaet hun ciang' a muanhuai ka tausang hi;
Upna sia sak thei na khempeuh, Ken tu'n ka nusia ta,
Kasung pan A vangliatna tawh hong kem hi.
Leitung in kei hong nial in, Satan in hong do hang',
Jesuh hangin kama ka tun ding hi;
(Hallelujah) Amah Zangkuam Unok Paak hi, ataang Zingsol zongh hi,
Keia' ding tulsawm tulthen sa'ng hoih zaw hi.

Verse 3
Aman hong nusia ngeilo ding, zongh hong nial ngeilo ding,
Amah um in A deihna bangh ta peuh leng;
Keitung ah bangbang piang taleh, Lau nawn peuhmah kei ning,
Manna tawh ka kha hong' vaak ding nuam lua veng'.
Minthang gam zuan in leeng ning', Topa kamu ding hi,
Lungnopna guntui a luanna mun hi;
(Hallelujah) Amah Zangkuam Unok Paak hi, ataang Zingsol zongh hi,
Keia' ding tulsawm tulthen sa'ng hoih zaw hi.

Title: KEI HONG TANPA (TDM - 7)
My Redeemer [Late 19:14 ]
(Beating 3 times to the measure)

Verse 1
Hong gum Topa hong it lamdang, Lungdam sindam la ka sa;
Kiciamna hong pel sak nuam hi, Singlamteh tung si zo ta.

Verse 2
Vantung inn nuampi luah na ding, A lamdang thupha gen hi;
Hong hehpih hong it luat man in, Ka mawh hong phel sak zo hi.
 

Chorus
 (Beating 4 times to the measure)
Topa phat (na sak ding la ) na sak ding la pha, (ding la pha)
A si pha(tawh hong sawp hi,)tawh hong sawp hi; (tawh hong sawp hi)
Singlamteh(Singlamteh tung sihna thuak a,) tung sihna thuak a,
Ka mawh hong(Ka mawh hong phel sak zo hi,)phel sak zo hi. (phel sak zo hi)

Verse 3
Hong gum Topa A minthang hi, Nisim la sa in phat ning;
Sih ciang mawh tanna meikuang sung, Hong tung sak nuam lo ding hi.

Verse 4
Hong gum Pa le A hong itna, La in ka sa sa ding hi;
Sihna pan hong suak ta sak a, A mah tawh ka om ding hi.

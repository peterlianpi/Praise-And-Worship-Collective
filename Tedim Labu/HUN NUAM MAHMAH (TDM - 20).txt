Title: HUN NUAM MAHMAH (TDM - 20)
How Sweet the Hour [Efesa 3:15]

Verse 1
 Moderato
Phatna thungetna hun nuam hi, Pasian i biak khop ciang; Ama kiangah leng to ding hi, Nuamna la i sak ciang.
Kamciam manpha hong ki pia ta, I tel siang semsem ding; Lei ah lawmte tawh ki khen hang, Van ah ki mu kik ding.
* Ni tawp ni ciang ei te khempeuh Van ah i ki mu ding; Jesuh'si pha i upna hang, Van ah ki mu kik ding.

Verse 2
Ei te lungsim hong ki hual sak, Itna siangtho hi a; Pasian Topa Khris min hang in, Ei te, i ki khawm ta.
Kikhenna hun hong tun baih hang, A thu ciamna kip hi; 'Ei te' lametna vantungah, Ki mu kik ding' hong ci.
* Ni tawpni ciang ei te khempeuh Van ah i ki mu ding; Jesuh'si pha i upna hang, Van ah ki mu kik ding.

Verse 3
A gim, a tawl i khe te in, Khamgam sik baih ding hi; I it i lawm te hong ngakna, Tua kham gam nuam sung hi.
Kum ki vei lo tomkal sungin, Huihpi tuihual ven ciang; Tawntung lungdamna la sa ding, Van ah i ki muh ciang.
* Ni tawp ni ciang ei te khempeuh Van ah i ki mu ding; Jesuh'si pha i upna hang, Van ah ki mu kik ding.

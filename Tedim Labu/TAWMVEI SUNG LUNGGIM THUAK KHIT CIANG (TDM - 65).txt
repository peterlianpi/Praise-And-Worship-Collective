Title: TAWMVEI SUNG LUNGGIM THUAK KHIT CIANG (TDM - 65)
A Few More Marching Weary [Isai 27:12]

Verse 1
Tawmvei sung lunggim thuak khit ciang, Inn i tung ding;
Tawmvei sung dahna thuak khit ciang, Inn i tung ding;
Tawmvei singlamteh puak khit ciang, Jesuh tawh khamlukhu khu ding;

Verse 2
Tawmvei sung kahna khit zawh ciang, Inn i tung ding;
Tawmvei sung i ngak khit zawh ciang, Inn i tung ding;
Mawhna tungah i gualzawh ciang, Buhphal pawlkhat i kaikhawm ding;
 

Chorus
Hun te (Hun le ni te) bei hanhan a, I (Ei te i) tawlnga baih ta ding;
Lunggimna teng bei dinga, Inn i tung ding.

Verse 3
Tawmvei sung i kikhen khit ciang, Inn i tung ding;
Tawmvei sung i kiho khit ciang, Inn i tung ding;
Tawmvei sung i kikhen khit ciang, Kanaan gam lam i zuan ta ding;

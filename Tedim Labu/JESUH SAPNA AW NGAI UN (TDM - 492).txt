Title: JESUH SAPNA AW NGAI UN (TDM - 492)
Hark the Voice of Jesus Calling [Isaiah 6:8]

Verse 1
Ngai un! Jesuh’ hong sapna aw, “Tuni kua in nasem ding?
Lo te au khin anlak hun zo, Tang te kua in kaikhawm ding?”
Topa’n tu’n zong hong sam lai hi, Thaman tampi hong lui hi;
Kua in dawng ding lungdam tak in: “Topa aw, kei hong sawl in.”

Verse 2
Tuipi gal na kai zawh loh hang’, Lawki gam na pai lah zong;
Na kongbiang ah na huh theih dang, Na kiangnai zong om thei zaw;
Vanmi bangh na pau thei kei zong, Jesuh’ hong itna gen aw;
Paul bangh thu na hilh thei kei zong, Topa hong si hi, ci aw.

Verse 3
Zion kulhpi sang tung panin, Galvil na hi zo lo zong;
Na hilh thei lo zong van lampi, Nuntakna na luilah zong;
Thungetna le sumpi te tawh, Vantung’ deih na’ sem’ thei ding;
Kamsangpa’ khut te a domto, Aaron bangh nahi thei ding.

Verse 4
Mihingte asih laitak in, Topa’n nang hong sam’ lai hi;
“Bangmah ka sem thei kei,” ci in, Thadah kampau khia kei in;
Hong sawl peuh lungdam tak sem in, Thanuam tak in sem ta in;
Dawng pah in, a hong sap ciangin: “Topa aw, kei hong sawl in.”

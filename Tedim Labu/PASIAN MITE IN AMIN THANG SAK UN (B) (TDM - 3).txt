Title: PASIAN MITE IN AMIN THANG SAK UN (B) (TDM - 3)
Ye Servants of God, Your Master Proclaim [Mang. 14:3; Late 68:20]

Verse 1
Pasian' mite in, Amin thang sak un,
Gamlapi dong in, Amin tangko un;
Gualzo minthang pen Pa, Jesuh phat huai hi,
Ama gam minthang a, Mun khempeuh uk hi.

Verse 2
Van pan hong Uk pa, Hong hon zo pa hi,
Amah hong nai a, Ei hong ompih hi;
Mi siang honpi te in, Amah pahtawi un,
Hong honkhia zo Pa in, Jesuh, thei un.

Verse 3
Pasian hong Honpa, Kumpi in mang den,
Mi khempeuh awng hen, Tapa pahtawi hen;
To Jesuh min phat in, Van mi'n tangko hi,
A lu uh kun suk in, Tuu no bia uh hi.

Verse 4
Va bia ni, ei'n zong Biak mah ding hi a,
Minthanna teng zong Ama a' hi ta;
Vanmi te tawh kop in, Amah bia khawm ni,
Lungdam ko tawntung in, Amah it den ni.

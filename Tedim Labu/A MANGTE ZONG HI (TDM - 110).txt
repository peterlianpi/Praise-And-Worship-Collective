Title: A MANGTE ZONG HI (TDM - 110)
Seeking the Lost [Maku 16:15]

Verse 1
A mang' te hong' zong in hong' zol' ngeungeu,
Mualsang tung'a lam akhial mite;
'Keimah hong' zuan un,' hongci kik leuleu,
Topa'n tuni in hong' sam' eite.

Verse 2
A mang' te zong in Jesuh' lam lak in,
Thahat lo kha le a na lungsim;
Hotkhiatna lampi sungah makaih in,
Nuntak tawntungna lampi lak in.
 

Chorus
(Mualsang tung' a) Mualsang tung' a (gamla ah pai ni) gamla ah pai ni,
(A vak mite) A vak mite la kik ni (lakik lakik ni);
(Mawhnei mi ta'ng) Mawhnei mi ta'ng (ding a si Tuuno) ding a si Tuuno
(I honpa' tuu) I honpa' tuu buk ah khum in (buk ah khum in).

Verse 3
Tua bangin pai ni thupha nasem mi,
Khris zui den ni, a nisial in;
A bah mi lawpsak in, puk mi phong ni,
A mang' mi lampi Jesuh lak ni.

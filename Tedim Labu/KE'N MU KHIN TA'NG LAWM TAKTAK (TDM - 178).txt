Title: KE'N MU KHIN TA'NG LAWM TAKTAK (TDM - 178)
I Have Found a Friend Indeed [John 15:15]

Verse 1
Ke'n mu khin ta'ng lawm taktak, Ka sapna teng hong dim sak,
Ka Honpa manpha Honpa; Upna mit tawh keimah in,
Ka mu thei a tawntung in, Lam tawntung hong tonpih hi.

Verse 2
Ka mawhna hong sawpsiang sak, Ka sunglam zongh hong siangsak,
Ka Honpa manpha Honpa; Ka kha hong cidam sak a,
Ka nunna hong nuamsak a, Nisim in thupha hong pia.
 

Chorus
Hallelujah ka Honpa tawh ka paikhawm (ka paikhawm) hi,
Ka it Honpa, manpha Honpa;
Hong it hong angvanna sungah kei hong kem hi,
Kei guak hi ke'ng, kei guak pai ngei ke'ng.

Verse 3
Maipha hong pia ka dah ciang', Ziaetna lian ka thuak ciang',
Ka Honpa manpha Honpa; A angsung khawlmun nuam bangh,
Mu zo ke'ng tawldamna dang', Hong huh den pa Amah hi.

Verse 4
Inn hong bawlsak vantung ah, Limlang tuipi gal khat ah,
Ka Honpa manpha Honpa; Ka sunmang hong tangtung ding,
Tua munah kei ka teng ding, Tawntung nopsakna munah.

Title: VANGLIAN TOPA PHAT UN (TDM - 18)
Praise to the Lord, the Almighty [Late 145:14:21]

Verse 1
Topa phat un, na khempeuh a piang sak i Kumpipa,
Ke'n ka phat ding ka nuntakna hong hon, hong damsak pa;
Tua thu za te'n A biakinn lam hong zuan un,
Lungdam biakpiakna hong pawm un.

Verse 2
Topa phat un, na khempeuh lamdang a hong uk Mangpa,
A khut nuaiah keimah hong kem, hong liah a hong vaak pa;
Ka thei zo hi Topa vaihawmna bang in,
Ka deihna khempeuh hong pia khin.

Verse 3
Topa phat un, keimah hong kem, ka ma hong vang sak pa,
Ama' hoihna hehpihna tawh nisim a hong keem pa;
Hong itna tawh kei bang lel lawm in hong nei,
Kei ading hih lah nei ngei kei.

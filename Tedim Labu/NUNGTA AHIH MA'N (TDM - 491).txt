Title: NUNGTA AHIH MA'N (TDM - 491)
Because He Lives

Verse 1
Pasian inah A tapa hong sawl,
Hong it ding le hong damsak ding;
Ka mawhna te maisak ding hong pai,
Nungta ahih manin a han guak bek om hi.

Verse 2
Naungek te zongh Ama’n hong kepsak,
Bang zah tak in Lungdam huai hiam?
Tua sangin zongh lungdam huai zaw lai,
Nungta ai’h man lau nang’ om lo cih thu mahmah!
 

Chorus
Nungta ahih man bangbang piang ta se leh,
phamawh sa ke’ng zongh lau beek ke’ng;
Mailam hun te, A khutsung om cih thei ing,
Amah nungta ahih man’ ka nunna’n manh nei.

Verse 3
Nikhat ni ciang’ sihna gul gal ah,
Haksa pi in ka kah ding hi;
Tua hun ciang’ in sihna zo zo in,
Amah Kumpi a hih lam ka mu thei ding hi.

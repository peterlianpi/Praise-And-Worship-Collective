Title: KA MAWH HONG LAKKHIATSAK (TDM - 474)
He Took My Sins Away [I Pit. 2:24]

Verse 1
Dahna tawlna tawh Jesuh ka zuat leh,
Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak, Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak;
Tu’n hong itna’n hong kipak sak ta veh,
Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak.

Verse 2
Mawhna vangik ka puak zawh ding hi lo,
A vek in hong laksak, A vek in hong laksak;
Ka gimna teng Ama tung ka suan zo,
Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak.
 

Chorus
Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak,
Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak Nisim in la hong sasa sak;
Nuam lua ing, Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak,
Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak.

Verse 3
Hong ki mawhsak nang’ khat zongh om nawnlo,
Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak, Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak;
Khamuanna cing Kei tungah hong pia zo,
Ka mawh hong lakkhiat sak.

Verse 4
Tuni Jesuh Khris kiang na zuat leh,
Na mawh hong lakhia ding, Na mawhhong lakhia ding;
A itna sung nisim nuamsa niteh,
Na mawh hong la khia ding.

Title: A HOIH HUANSUNG THUNGETNA (TDM - 306)
The Beautiful Garden of Prayer [Matt. 26:36]

Verse 1
Jesuh hong ngakna mun huansung khat om,
Tua in a lamdang mun hoih khat hi;
A vangliatna khuavaakpi ataang hi,

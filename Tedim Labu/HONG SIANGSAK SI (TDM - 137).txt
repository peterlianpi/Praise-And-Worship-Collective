Title: HONG SIANGSAK SI (TDM - 137)
The Cleansing Blood [I John 1:7]

Verse 1
Enun, mawhna khempeuh a puakhia Pa,
Sihna Kalvary mual tungah;
Singlamteh tung gimna sihna thuak in,
Keia' dingin a sisan hong luang.

Verse 2
Mawh sawpsiang na'ng tui a hong luang zo hi,
Hong Gum Topa sihna mun pan';
A tawntung in tua tui ah kisil ni,
Hong Gum Topa kiang zuan in pai ni.
 

Chorus
Hong gum Topa pang pan inah, Sisan le tui hong luang khia hi;
Ka lung sungah om tawntung hi, Mawhna ki sawpsiang zo.

Verse 3
Tua si sungah a kisil khempeuh te,
A mawhna teng vuk bangh ngo ding;
'Na mawhna khempeuh uh hong maisak ding,
'Ka kiang hong pai un,' Topa'n hong ci.

Verse 4
Mawhna sungah ka pai kik nawn kei ding,
Jesuh'n kei hong suakta sak ding;
A KhaSiangtho in kei hong pi dinga,
A gam sungah hong lutpih ding hi.

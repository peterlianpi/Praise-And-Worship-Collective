Title: HONG HON TOPA HONG LIAH IN (TDM - 54)
Hide me, O my Saviour! [Late 27:5]

Verse 1
Nasian-tho-na mun ah Topa, Kei hong liah ta in;
Na vangliatna kei' tawldamna, Na maitang hong lak in.

Verse 2
Nuntak sung huihpi a nun lai, Kei hong liah ta in;
Tuipi tungah vakhu bang in, Nang kiang hong lutsak in.
 

Chorus
Hong liah ta in, Hong gum Topa hong liah in;
Lau loh na na kiang, Kei mah hong om sak in.

Verse 3
Dahna tawh lungsim ki tap lai, Lungdamna hong pia'n;
Khitui tawh nuamna ka zon lai, Kei mah hongliah ta in.

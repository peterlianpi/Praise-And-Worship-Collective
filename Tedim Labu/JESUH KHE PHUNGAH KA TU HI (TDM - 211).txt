Title: JESUH KHE PHUNGAH KA TU HI (TDM - 211)
Sitting at the Feet of Jesus [Luka 8:35]

Verse 1
Jesuh khephung ah ka tu a, Hong hopihna ka za hi;
Itna kam khum bekbek hi a, Nisim ka zaza nuam hi;
Jesuh khe phung ah ka tu a, Tanglai thu te ka phawk hi;
Hong it pha mahmah na hi a, Ka lungsim sung hong zen hi.

Verse 2
Jesuh khe phung ah ka tawlnga, A nuam zaw mun om lo pi;
Ka vangik pi teng ka suahna, Ka tawldamna mun a hi;
Jesuh khe phungah ka tawlnga, Ka deih khempeuh ka ngen hi;
Ka nget khempeuh A hong pia a, Hong piak loh om ngei lo hi.

Verse 3
Hong Hon Topa Na thupha tawh, Tu in keimah hong huh in;
Hong en in, Na itna mit tawh, Na maipha hong mu sak in;
Nangma lungsim kei hong guan aw, Na sian bangh hong siang sak in;
Midik hong tangsak Jesuh aw, Gen ning, kei Nang aa hi khin.

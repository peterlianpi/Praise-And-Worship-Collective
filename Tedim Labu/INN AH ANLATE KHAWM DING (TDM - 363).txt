Title: INN AH ANLATE KHAWM DING (TDM - 363)
Gather the Reapers Home! [Mang. 14:15]

Verse 1
Kham gamnuam panin la aw na za hiam?
Lungdamna la thak ahi;
lawptakin Buhlawm te hen khawm ve ni,
A hun sawt nawnlo ding hi.

Verse 2
Kham gamnuam panin, hong ensuk uh a,
I it i lawmngaih te hi;
Nasep khempueh zo, singlamteh puazo,
Khapsa lukhu khu uh hi.
 

Chorus
An la Topa tu’n hong kilang baih ding,
Amai mu in a aw i za ding;
An la Topa tu’n hong kilang baih ding,
Inn ah anlate khawm ding.

Verse 3
Kham gamnuam panin la ngaih hong kisa,
I lungsim hong hatsak hi;
Tua la ngaih aw in, hong thakhauh sak a,
Sun niloh na i sem hi.

Verse 4
Kham gamnuam panin la ngaih hong kisa,
Lungnopna gamnuam pan hi;
Upna tawh i thei, a sawtlo inah,
Tua la i sa khawm ding hi.

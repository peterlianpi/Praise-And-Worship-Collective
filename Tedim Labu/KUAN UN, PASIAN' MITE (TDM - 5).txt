Title: KUAN UN, PASIAN' MITE (TDM - 5)
Rise Up, O Men of God! [Late 44:26]

Verse 1
Kuan un, Pasian' mite, Thu neu te nusia un;
Kumpite' Kumpipa a'ding, Lungsim, thatang pia un.

Verse 2
Kuan un, Pasian' mite, A gam hong tung hak hi;
Unau ki itna tung sakin, Khialhna te bei sak ni.

Verse 3
Kuan un, Pasian' mite, Pawlpi in hong ngak hi;
Tha ki cing lo a hih manin, Kuankhia in va huh ni.

Verse 4
Sang sak un, singlamteh, A ma khekhap zui ni;
Jesuh sanggam i hih manin, Pasian mite kuan ni.

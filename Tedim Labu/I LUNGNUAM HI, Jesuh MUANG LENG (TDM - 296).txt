Title: I LUNGNUAM HI, Jesuh MUANG LENG (TDM - 296)
“Tis so Sweet to Trust in Jesus” [Jer. 17:7; Pau. 16:20]

Verse 1
I lungnuam hi Jesuh muang leng,
Ama hong gen bangh um leng;
Ama hong thuciamna muang leng,
Topa hong cihna thei leng.

Verse 2
I lungnuam hi Jesuh muang leng,
Hong sawpna A si muang leng;
Hong dam, hong siangsak thei,
A si Sungah upna tawh bual leng.
 

Chorus
Jesuh Jesuh ke’n ka muang hi, A muanhuaina thei zo ing;
Jesuh Jesuh manpha Jesuh, Amah muang semsem nuam ing.

Verse 3
I lungnuam hi Jesuh muang leng,
Mawhna pumpi nusia leng;
 
 3.Nuntak lungnop, tawldam, nuamna,
Jesuh khut tung’ pan’ sangleng.

Verse 4
Jesuh kong muan theih ka nuam hi,
A manpha Lawmpa nahi;
Hong honpa aw, a tawp dongin,
Nong ompih ding ka thei hi.

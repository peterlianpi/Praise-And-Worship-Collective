Title: TOPA KONG ITZAW HI (TDM - 74)
I Know I Love Thee Better Lord [I Kumpi 10:7]

Verse 1
Leitung nopsaknate sangin, Topa kong it zaw hi;
Kisia ngeilo nopsak na'ng in, Topa in hong pia hi.

Verse 2
Leitung ka zawl khempeuh sangin, Topa nai nuam zaw ing;
Leitung ah la ngaih khempeuh sang', Thu ngaihna pha zaw hi.
 

Chorus
Hong it hong ngaihna a thupha(a thupha)
Kimkhat beek gen nai kei;
Hong sawp siangthona a thupha, (a thupha)
Kimkhat beek gen nai kei. (gen nai kei)

Verse 3
Ka lung hong dam sak zo ta hi, Cikmah'n bei lo ding hi;
Hong itna thupha om kei leh, Lungsim kisia ding hi.

Verse 4
Hong omkhawm pih kithei lo pi, Hihzah lungdamna lian;
Topa mai i mu ding takpi, Bangzah a nuam ding hiam.

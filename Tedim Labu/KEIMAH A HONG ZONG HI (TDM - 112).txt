Title: KEIMAH A HONG ZONG HI (TDM - 112)
Seeking for Me [Ezek. 34:11]

Verse 1
Hong gum Topa Betlen khua hong suak a,
Bawng anneekna kuang sung kisial zota;
Aw a lamdang nuamna min pha mahmah,
Keimah a hong' zong hi.

Verse 2
Topa Jesuh in singlamteh tungah,
Kei' mawhna tang' dingin hong si zo ta;
Maw a lamdang keima lung a dam ta,
Kei tang' ding hong si hi.
 

Chorus
Keimah ahong zong hi, (zong hi, zong hi)
Keimah ahong zong hi; (zong hi, zong hi)
Maw lamdang nuamna a min pha mahmah,
keimah a hong zong hi.

Verse 3
Topa Jesuh nidang bangh mah inah,
Huan sung pan lamkhial tuu te a zong ta;
Sauveipi damtakin a hong sam hi,
Keimah ahong sam hi.

Verse 4
Jesuh vantung pan'n hong pai kik ding hi,
Kum tampi kheng zo hih thu ka um' hi;
Van pan' hong kumsuk ke'n ka mu ding hi,
Kei ading hong kum hi.

Title: JESUH KA LUNG SUNGA KONG DEIH (TDM - 154)
Jesus, Beloved of My Heart [Rom 9:25, 26]

Verse 1
Jesuh ka lungsim in kadeih, Hong hehpihna nak pi'n ka ngen;
Hong hehpihna itna hong langh, Amah ke'n ka it phasem ta.

Verse 2
Leitung itna sung a nuam pen, Ki teh thei lo Hong itna tawh;
Kong ento lametna pi tawh, Na mai muh ciang' lamdang ka sa.
 

Chorus
Lungsim phabel, (lungsim phabel) hong guan sukin, (hong guan sukin)
Tua Kham khuapi ka muh nading; (ka muh nading)
Ka lungsim sung (ka lungsim sung) hong tangsak in, (hong tangsak in)
Tua khuapi nuam keia' ding hi. (keia' ding hi.)

Verse 3
Vantung gamsung nopna dim in, Paak te' hoihna mu mahtaleng;
Kep kong le van mi Aw sang in, Nong itna hi ka lunggulh pen.

Verse 4
Nang loin nopna om thei kei, Ka lungsim hong damsak thei ding;
Nang Na om ciang' dahna nuam sak, Vantung nopna kei sung hong taang.

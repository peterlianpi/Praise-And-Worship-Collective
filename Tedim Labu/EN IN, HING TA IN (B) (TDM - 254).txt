Title: EN IN, HING TA IN (B) (TDM - 254)
Look and Thou Shall Live [Isaiah 45:22]

Verse 1
Kalvary ah Honpa en dih vaw,
Nang le kei ading thuak hi lo maw;
Itna tawh hong sap lai ngai dih vaw, En in, hing ta in.

Verse 2
Na kha vangik hong honkhia zo hi,
Nuntak tawntungna na ngah ding hi;
Mawhna tatman Jesuh’n pia zo hi, En in, hing ta in.
 

Chorus
En in, hing ta in, En in, hing ta in,
Nang a’ding sing tung sihna en in, En in, hing ta in.

Verse 3
Han pan a tho Jesuh na en ding,
Omna om lai na zuan mengmeng ding;
A mai taangin hong lungnuamsak ding, En in, hing ta in.

Verse 4
Hong thumsak ding Jesuh van ah om,
Mawhnei te’ gimna thei in hong dom;
Na paina peuh hong kha dingin om, En in, hing ta in.

Title: KA MUANNA Jesuh AHI (TDM - 320)
My Hope is Built

Verse 1
Keimah ka muanna a bulpi, Jesuh si le siangthona hi;
A dang thu teng ka um ngam kei, Jesuh a min bek ka um thei.

Verse 2
Khuamial in a mai liah ciangin, A hong hehpihna kamuang ding;
Huihpi nakpi a nun ciangah, Munliahna sungah sikkip khia.
 

Chorus
Keima dinna Jesuh Suangpi,
Omna dang teng sehnel bangh hi,
Omna dangteng sehnel bang hi.

Verse 3
A thuciamna le Ama si, Tuipi kan lai ka muang ding hi;
Ka kiimkot teng a siat ciangah, Ka muanna in Jesuh Topa.

Verse 4
Pengkul aw tawh hong pai ciangin, Ama maiah ka ding nuam hi;
A hong siangtho sak khit ciangin, Mawh neilo in ka om ding hi.

Title: TU NI HONPA'N HONG SAM (TDM - 222)
Today the Saviour Calls [Heb. 4:7]

Verse 1
Tuni Honpa'n hong sam, 'Hong pai in' ci.
'Vantung lam thei lo mi, Kha hilh ding hi'.

Verse 2
Tuni Honpa'n hong sam, Tuin ngai in,
Jesuh Topa maiah, Nangmah pai in.

Verse 3
Tuni Honpa'n hong sam, Ama kiang ah,
Nangmah na pai kei leh, Sih ding lauhuai.

Verse 4
KhaSiangtho in hong sam, Amah nial ke'n,
A hong hehpih nuam hi. Pai khia sak ke'n.

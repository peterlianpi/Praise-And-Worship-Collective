Title: HONG THUM NGET-NGUT HI (TDM - 224)
Tendely Pleading [Johan 10:9]

Verse 1
 Beating three time measure
Hingim a mang' mi aw, hong kihei in,
Tuu cing hoih pa'n hong sam, ngai in, Tuni!
Hong hon ding hong zong, sawp siang ding hong ngak,
Amah sang meng in, uaikai nawn kei in.

Verse 2
Hong ngak lai hive'n banghang mangthang ding?
Tuu buk pan gamla na vak khit hangin;
A nuntakna si tawh hong tan veve,
Hong thuakpihna lian gen zawh loh lamdang.
 

Chorus
Hong sam hiau-hiau hi, Hong thum lailai hi,
Tuucing hoihpa'n nang Hong sam za in;
Hong thum ngetngut hi, Hong sam lailai hi,
Itna tawh hong sam, 'Hong pai in,' ci.

Verse 3
A thupha ngaihsun in, Athu ngai in,
Mawh nei lo pa'n sing tung mawhna puakhia;
Mawhmaina kician nuntak tawntungna,
A sisan luanna tawh nangmah hong pia.

Verse 4
Thuman lampi zui in, tangtak pai in,
Jesuh sung lut in,nuntakna kongpi;
Jesuh in lungnem tak in hong sam hi,
Pai in lut un, a mangthang nawn lo ding.

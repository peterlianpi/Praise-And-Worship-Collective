Title: BANG ZAH’N Jesuh KA IT (TDM - 339)
Oh, How I Love Jesus [Johan 21:15, 16]

Verse 1
Ka zaknop min khat a om hi, La in sasa nuam ing;
Min teng pan’ a min ngaihbel hi, sasa ning, lolo ning.

Verse 2
Hong gumpa itna hong gen hi, Hong Tankhia ding si hi;
Ama si manpha hong hilh hi, Mawhnei mi mawhmaina.
 

Chorus
Bangzah’n Jesuh ka it, Bangzah’n Jesuh ka it;
Bangzah’n Jesuh ka it Hong it masak man hi!

Verse 3
Pa neih khempeuh kei hong hilh hi, Nisim sapna cing hi;
Khuamial sung lam ka pai khialh ciang’, Khuavak hong tangsak hi.

Verse 4
Ka lunggimna A hong theihpih, Hong it pa hong hilh hi;
Ka dahna teng A hong thuakpih, Thuakpih dang omlo hi.

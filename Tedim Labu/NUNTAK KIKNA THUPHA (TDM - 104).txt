Title: NUNTAK KIKNA THUPHA (TDM - 104)
Revive Thy Work, O Lord [Hab. 3:2]
Spirited

Verse 1
Nuntak kikna thupha, Misiang te kiang tung ta;
Mi dang te kiang minthanna tawh, Na gen ciang' ngai nuam sak.

Verse 2
Nuntak kikna thupha, Na minpha thang sak in;
Mihing khempeuh lungsim sungah, Na itna kuang sakin.
 

Chorus
Nuntak kikna thupha (thupha), Na mai khukdin ciangin (ciangin);
Topa aw, hong kumsuk inla, Thupha bel hong pia in.

Verse 3
Nuntak kikna thupha, Thuhilh apha pia a;
A siangtho Na thupha zak ciang', Upna na piangsak ta'n.

Verse 4
Nuntak kikna thupha, KhaSiangtho hong guang in;
Na minthanna kilangh sakin, Koa' dingin apha.

Title: TUUTE HONG PAIPIH'N (TDM - 203)
Bring Them In [Mt. 18:12]

Verse 1
Ngai un, gamlak khuamial sung ah,
Tuucingpa aw kiza thei hi;
Tuu khumna sung pan a pusuak,
A mang tuu te a sam kik hi.

Verse 2
A mang tuu te a zon nading,
Tuucingpa kua in huh ding hiam?
Khuadam om lo tuu khumna sung,
Kua in tuu te hong tum sak hiam.
 

Chorus
Hong paipih'n, hong paipih'n!
Khialhna mun pan-in hong paipih'n;
Hong paipih'n, hong paipih'n,
A mang' te Jesuh kiang paipih'n.

Verse 3
Gampa lak le mualtung teng ah,
Amau kahna aw hong ngai in;
Topa in nangma kiangah ci,
'Keima tuu te na zong ding hi.'

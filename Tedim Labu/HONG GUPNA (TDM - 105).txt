Title: HONG GUPNA (TDM - 105)
Salvation [Titu 2:11]

Verse 1
Topa Jesuh hong hehpihna, Lungdam in lasa un;
Leitung khempeuh a zelh na'ngin, Lungdam nading kum hi.

Verse 2
Mittaw te aw Jesuh en un, A dah te lungdam un;
Jesuh in hong suahtak zo hi, Thong sung om te'n sa un.
 

Chorus
Hong gupna, hong gupna, Thupha bel hong tung hi;
Hong gum ta, hong gum ta, Topa Jesuh Kumpi.

Verse 3
Hong it manin Jesuh si a, Phat huai lamdang bel hi;
Leitung mite' nopsak nading, Pasian hong itna hi.

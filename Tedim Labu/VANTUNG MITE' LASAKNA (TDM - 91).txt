Title: VANTUNG MITE' LASAKNA (TDM - 91)
Angels We Have Heard on High [Luke 2:8-15]

Verse 1
Vantungm ite la sakna, Zanglei tungah kiza hi;
Mualsang te'n zong sangsawn a, Lungdamna la ki thawn hi.

Verse 2
Tuucing te no lungdamna, Nuamna la te bang thu hiam?
Vantung la ngaih no sak na, Lungdamna thu bang hang hiam?
 

Chorus
Thang tahen, Pasian min thang ta hen;
Thang tahen, Van a sangbel Pasian.

Verse 3
Betlen khua ah hong pai in, Note'n zong hong en ve un;
Khris Kumpi khukdin in, Note'n zong hong phat pih un.

Verse 4
Vantung mi'n a phat laitak, Gan kuang sung kisial en un;
Phatna ko te sak laitak, Josef, Mary hong huh un.

Title: JESUH HONG PAI DING HI (TDM - 170)
Jesus is Coming [1 Thess. 4:16]

Verse 1
Lasa un! 'Jesuh in kong pai ding' ci,
A si tawh a gup mihing ta dingin;
Hong kum a, hong uk ding a minthang hi,

Verse 2
Jesuh hong pai ciang misi te tho ding,
I it i ngaih lawmte tawh kimu ding;
Vantung ah ei hong paipih nading in,

* Jesuh hong paikik ding hi!
 

Chorus
Jesuh hong pai ding, hong paikik ding hi,
Jesuh hong paikik ding hi! (Jesuh hong paikik)
Lungdam in, la pha sa un nuam ding hi!
Jesuh hong paikik ding hi!

Verse 3
Jesuh hong pai ding a misiangtho te,
Leitung lungdamna nuam pia suk ding hi;
Bei ding hi, lungkhamna sinkhamna te,

Verse 4
Jesuh hong pai ding thuciamna om hi,
A teelsa mihing te lato dingin;
Ngak in, lawp in, a kisa in om in,

Title: JESUH MUANG IN (TDM - 338)
Trusting Jesus [Job 13:15; John 6:20]

Verse 1
Nisimin Jesuh a min, Um in, Huihpi nun ciangin;
Thu-upna a tawm hangin, Jesuh bek um in muang in.

Verse 2
KhaSiangtho keima tungah, Hong taang hi, ka sin sungah;
Lam hong hilh leh puk zo lo, Jesuh bek um in muang in.
 

Chorus
Nisim in Jesuh Topa, Um in, beel in, muang inla;
LaiSiangtho hong hilh bangin, Jesuh bek um in muang in.

Verse 3
Lam a nop leh lasa ta, Khua hong mial leh thum inla;
Gimna thuak ciang’ thungen in, Jesuh bek um in muang in.

Verse 4
Ka nuntak laiteng muang ning’, Leitung bei mateng suang ning’;
Vantung khua tun mateng in, Jesuh bek um in muang in.

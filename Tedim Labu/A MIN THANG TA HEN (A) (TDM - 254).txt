Title: A MIN THANG TA HEN (A) (TDM - 254)
Glory to His Name [Eph. 1:12]

Verse 1
Jesuh sihna singlamteh phung ah,
Mawhna hong phel na’ng ka kiko a;
Tualai ah sisan in hong sawp ta, Amin thang tahen.

Verse 2
Mawhna pan hong hotna lamdang hi,
Jesuh’n nuam takin hong ompih hi;
Tua singlamteh ah kei hong sam hi, Amin thang tahen.
 

Chorus
Amin thang ta hen, Amin thang tahen;
Tualai ah sisan in hong sawp ta, Amin thang tahen.

Verse 3
Mawh sawpsiangna tuinak manpha a,
Ka tun man in kei ka nuam lua a;
Jesuh’n hong hon hong siangsak zo ta, Amin thang tahen.

Verse 4
Tua ciktui manpha ah hong pai un,
Jesuh khephung ah hong ki-ap un;
Kibual un-la, kisawpsiang sak un, Amin thang tahen.

Title: KHRIS HONG PAIKIK CIANG (TDM - 163)
Christ Returneth [Johan 14:3]

Verse 1
Zingsang, zingsol a tum madiak ahi zongin,
Khua hong vak in, ni a suah ciang ahi zongin;
Minthanna tawh dim in, Jesuh hong pai mu ding,
Amah a umte hong khop ciang'.

Verse 2
Sun hun hi taleh, zankim laitak hi taleh,
Zan khuamial sung ihsip laitak zongh hi taleh;
Pasian khuavak tawh minthang Jesuh hong pai ding,
Amah a um te hong khop ciang'.
 

Chorus
Aw, Topa cikciang hi ding hiam?
Ko kiang Nong paikik ding, Hong paikik ding,
Hallelujah, Hallelujah Amen, Hallelujah Amen.

Verse 3
Vantungmi in, Hosana ci'n a otna aw,
Mun khempeuh ah kiza ding hi, vantung tun ciang';
Vantung misiangtho te in a cing' ding uh hi,
Amah a um te hong khop ciang'.

Verse 4
Si lo in lungdam in vantung ah i pai ding,
Dahna, gentheihna, natna te om nawnlo ding;
Pasian tawh meii tung tuang in vantung i pai ding,
Amah a um te hong khop ciang'.

Title: PASIAN KIANG OM DING (TDM - 60)
To Live With God [Late 56:4; Josh. 1:9]

Verse 1
Leitung ah launa tampi a om hang,
Bangmah kihtak ka nei kei;
Kilang lo Pasian tawh ka om ciang in,
Koimah peuh pai ding ka lau lo.

Verse 2
Haksatna namkim a hong tun hangin,
Bangmah kihtak ka nei kei;
Pasian ka kiangah a hong om peuh leh,
Koimah peuh pai ding ka lau lo.
 

Chorus
Tuabang in (u le nau, Pasian lam ah) u le nau,(na lungkip hen,)
Pasian lam ah na lungkip hen; (lungkip hen)
Cikmah hun lungkia lo-in, Pasian kiang i tun' ciangin
Lungnopna bangzah tak-in a om diam.

Verse 3
Sihna guam thuk sung dong ka tung zongin,
Bangmah kihtak ka nei kei;
Gam nuamah om ding Topa' gam sungah,
Koimah peuh pai ding ka lau lo.

Verse 4
Minam kim in kei hong do hang in ah,
Bangmah kihtak ka nei kei;
Pasian vangliatna a om ka sungah,
Koimah peuh pai ding ka lau lo.

Title: JESUH KIA’N SINGLAMTEH PUA DING MAW? (TDM - 279)
Must Jesus Bear the Cross Alone? [John 19:17]

Verse 1
Jesuh kia’n singlamteh pua a, Guakpai ding maw leimi?
Hi lo, Mikim puak ding om a, Kei’ puak ding zongh om hi.

Verse 2
Singlamteh ke’n ka pua ding hi, Sihna’n hong khah mateng;
Kumpi lukhu ka ngah ding hi, Vantung innpi tung leng’.

Verse 3
A ki khenlet Jesuh khe kiang, Limlang suang’ phah tungah;
Ka kham lukhu ka ngak suk ciang’, Nuam in phat ning’ Amah.

Verse 4
Aw manpha hi, Tua singlamteh, Vanglian kumpi lukhu;
Ka ngah ding hi hi’n kik ni teh, Hong lak ding lawpna thu.

Title: NANG LE KEIMAH HONG SAM HI (TDM - 236)
Softly and Tendarly Jesus is Calling [Matt. 11:28]

Verse 1
Maitai in damtak Jesuh in hong sam hi,
Nang le keimah hong sam hi;
Topa Jesuh'n lungduai takin hong ngak hi,
Nang le keimah hong ngak hi.

Verse 2
Bang khawl i hiam Topa Jesuh'n hong thum hi,
Nang le kei kiang hong thum hi;
Hong hehpihna banghang don lo ihi hiam,
Nang le kei hong hehpih hi.
 

Chorus
Inn ah, (Innah) ciah un, (ciah un)
Gimna thuak te hong ciah un;
Lungdam takin Topa Jesuh in hong sam,
'Mawhnei te hong pai un,' ci.

Verse 3
Hun le ni te'n manlang in hong kheng thei hi,
Nang le kei hong kheng ding hi;
Khuamialna le sihna natna hong nai hi,
Nang le keimah hong nai hi.

Verse 4
Hong itna le a lamdang thuciamna pi,
Nang le kei ading koih hi;
Gamtat khialhna Topa'n hong phel sak ding hi,
Nang le kei hong phel ding hi.

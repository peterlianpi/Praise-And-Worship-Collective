Title: NI KHAT (TDM - 183)
One Day!

Verse 1
Nikhat, Ama min phatna van dim napi'n,
Leitungah mawhna zan bangh mial manin;
Nungak sung suakin, Jesuh hong pai suk in,
Mi lak hong teng hi, lampha lak ding in.

Verse 2
Ni khat, Kalvary mual kahto sak uh a,
Singtung kilhcip uh khailum ding inah;
Mawhna teng pua in, simmawh, bawlsiat, huatna,
Nakpi thuak sak uh hi, kei hong Tan Pa.
 

Chorus
Nungta in hong it, si in hong hon hi,
Kivui in ka mawhna a puakhia hi;
Thokik in hong diksak, khawnkhong tawntung hi,
Lungdam huai bel hi, hong paikik ding ni!

Verse 3
Ni khat, huan sungah Amah ki nusia a,
Nikhat, thuakna tawh peng' in tawlnga a;
Vantungmi te in, Ahan hong cing' suk a,
Lamet bei mite muan' kei hong hon Pa.

Verse 4
Ni khat, Amah han in siin zo nawnlo a,
Han kongvang khakna suangpi tukkhia a;
Sihna zo gawp in, sihna pan thokik a,
Tu'n vantung kahzo, tawntung kei Topa.

Verse 5
Ni khat, Amah pengkul in hong masuan ding,
Ama vanglian vantung pan' hong tang ding;
Ka it ka ngaihte, Amah'n hong tonpih ding,
Angtan huai hi, tua Jesuh kei' ading.

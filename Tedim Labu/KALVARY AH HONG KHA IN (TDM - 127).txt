Title: KALVARY AH HONG KHA IN (TDM - 127)
Leat Me to Calvary [Luka 22:21]

Verse 1
Ka Kumpi Na hi ta tu'n, Nang, Vangliatna teng tang' in;
Ling na khuk ka mangngilh loh na'ng, Kalvary hong kha in.

Verse 2
Na kisialna han hong lak in, Nangmah hong ki kah na;
Vantung mite'n puanpaak silh in, Nangmah a hong cin'na.
 

Chorus
Getsema ni mangngilh loh na'ng, Na thuakna ka mangngilh loh na'ng;
Nong itna ka mangngilh loh na'ng, Kalvary hong kha in.

Verse 3
Mary bangh phalvak tung inah, Paknamtui puak ding in;
Hong paipih in Kalvary ah, Na han hawm hong lak in.

Verse 4
Nangma ading ke'n pua nuam ing, Singlamteh nisim in;
Na dahna hong thuak pih nuam ing, Kei ading Nang thuak khin.

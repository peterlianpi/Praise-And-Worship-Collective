Title: EN UN, LAMDANG HONG ITNA (TDM - 68)
Behold, What Love [I Johan 3:1]

Verse 1
I lungngaih tawntung ding in ah, Topa Pasian itna;
Mawna a lian i neih napi 'Tate aw,' hong ci hi.

Verse 2
Topa tawh ki nai theih nading, Jesuh a si luangsak;
'Kata it te aw,' ci'n hong sam, A ang sung i khawl ding.
 

Chorus
 [Beating four times to the measure]
En un, (Topa Pasian in) Topa Pasian in (Topa Pasian in) 'Tate aw,' hong cihna Pasian tate i tang ding hi;
En un, (i tang ding) Topa Pasian in, (thupha phatna) Ei hong it thupha phatna.

Verse 3
Nung ciang i min bangci than ding, Tu'n ki thei nai lo hi;
Topa Jesuh hong kum kik ciang, Amah i sun ding hi.

Verse 4
Tua thu a lawpte khempeuh in, I Topa bang ding hi;
Sihna a zo minthang Topa, Mai mu dingin apha.

Title: HONG KISIN’ DIH UN (TDM - 473)
Mal. 3:10-
Verse 12

Verse 1
Pasian deihna, Lungkimna, Biakna pia un,
Abel bangin a limci pen pia un;
A gah masa, Anpal tawh zahtak lecin,
Na ansal te An tawh kidim ding hi.

Verse 2
Lungkim takin, Citpiakna, Biakna pia un,
Meigong nu sumpi piak bangin pia un;
A sia theilo Vantung ah na sum khol’ un,
Pasian hoihna Thupha na zeek ding hi.
 

Chorus
Hong (hong) kisin’ dih un, Hong (hong) ziaet dih un,
Thupha (Thupha namkim) a namkim kong pia (kongpia ding ci) ding hong ci;
Thu (thu) pha kong pia ding, Kong (kong) daupai sak ding,
Thu (thu) pha a tampi kong pia ding hong ci.

Verse 3
Ka Biakinn ah, an a om theih nading in,
Sawm ah khat a kim in hong paipih un;
Vantung kongkhak te hong in note ading,
Na hoih tampi kong piak na muding hi.

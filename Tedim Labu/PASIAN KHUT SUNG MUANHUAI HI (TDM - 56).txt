Title: PASIAN KHUT SUNG MUANHUAI HI (TDM - 56)
The Hollow of God's Hand [Isai. 49:2]

Verse 1
Lam hong lak gumpa ka kiang hong nai ciang,
Bangbang hong kimvel hang lau om nawnlo;
Huihpi, guahpi te hong daisak ding a,
A khutkuak sung muanhuai hi.

Verse 2
Itna tawh a angsung kei hong pom ciang,
Kilemna thu KhaSiangtho' hong hilh a;
Pasian khut in kei hong kiplet zawh ciang,
A khutkuak sung muanhuai hi.
 

Chorus
(A khut kuaksung) muanhuai hi, (a muanhuai hi)
(A khut kuaksung) muanhuai hi, (a muanhuai hi)
Bangbang hong kimvel hang lau om nawnlo,
A khut kuaksung muanhuai hi.

Verse 3
Ka kha kem, kei hong nusia nawnlo ding,
Hong itna khau tatsak zo om nawn lo;
Ka thei hi, Amah in hong kem den ding,
A khut kuaksung muanhuai hi.

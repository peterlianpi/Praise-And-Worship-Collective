Title: HONG LIAH IN (TDM - 75)
Hide Thou Me [Late 32:7]

Verse 1
Kitam a om a kip Suangpi, Hong liah in; Gimna le tawlna a tampi, Hong liah in.
Topa itna ka lungsim ah, Mihing khen theih lohna munah; A kip tawntung Suangpi pha aw, Hong liah in.

Verse 2
Mawhna kuam sung tu'n loh nading, Hong liah in; Topa aw ka lungmuanna ding, Hong liah in.
Leitung minthanna te inah, Ka mawh na'ng in hong zolzol a; A kip tawntung Suangpi pha aw, Hong liah in.

Verse 3
Dahna khua a hong mial laiteng, Hong liah in; Minthan ni zingkhua vak mateng, Hong liah in.
Jordan gunpi gei ka nai ciang, Na ang muanhuai sung kong tun ciang; A kip tawntung Suangpi pha aw, Hong liah in.

Title: AN LAKPHAT KHINTA HI (TDM - 405)
Harvest Fields Are Waiting [Luke 10:2]

Verse 1
An lakphat khin ta hi, Kuai khin zo ta hi,
Khris To pa’n hong sam a ni bei baih ding hi;
Hong sawl bang sem meng in, An late pawm in,
Thanuam tak sem inla an la te huh in.

Verse 2
An lakphat khin ta hi, Ki heihei kei in,
An la te sapna aw huih in hong puak khin;
Lungduai takin sem in, Khawm in anvui teng,
Kham le zaang mun khempeuh ni hong tum mateng.
 

Chorus
An (An lakphat khin ta hi) lakphat khin ta (An lakphat khin ta hi) hi,
Sep (Sep theih lai sem in) theih lai sem in (Sep theih lai sem in);
A (A hun bei hanhan hi) hun bei hanhan hi (A hun bei hanhan hi),
Tuni in sem in.

Verse 3
An lakphat khin ta hi, Tuni’n kua kuan ding?
An la te huh in buhlom te kuan pua ding;
Na ki sep theih sun hun, Hong bei baih ding hi,
Na zawh ciang’ Topa’n “Pha hi” hong ci ding hi.

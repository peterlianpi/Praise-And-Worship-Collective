Title: TUA LAI DAHNA OM NAWN LO (TDM - 437)
There Is No Sorrow There [Mang. 21:4]

Verse 1
Vantung ka ngaihsun hi, Topa Pasian omna;
A kham maitang nisim mu ding, Nop kicing man ngahna.

Verse 2
Vantung ka ngaihsun hi, Hong teel Topa omna;
Hong itna pha phat nading la, Ki sa tawntung ding hi.
 

Chorus
Dahna kipelhna mun, Dahna kipelhna mun;
Itna kicing vantung khamgam, Dahna kipelhna mun.

Verse 3
Vantung ka ngaihsun hi, Vantung mihing te in;
Puanpak silh in a om uh hi, Ei zongh i om ding hi.

Verse 4
Vantung ka ngaihsun hi, A tungsa i lawmte;
Mu a kikhen nawnlo ding a, Nuam tawntung ding hiveh.

Title: KUMPI LUKHU KHUSAK! (TDM - 14)
Crown Him King! [Mang. 19:16]

Verse 1
Kumpi lukhu, Jesuh khu sak un, Khang tawntung in Ama min phat un;
Amin thanna lian hong pahtawi un, Tu le a tawntung in.

Verse 2
Phat un, phat un, Van mihonpi aw, A tokhom zuan un, phatna la tawh;
Phat un, phat un, leimi khempeuh aw, Kumpi te' Kumpipa.
 

Chorus
Kumpi lukhu, Kumpi lukhu, Minthanna gam ah khu sak ding hi;
Lukhu, Kumpi lukhu, Khu sak in phat den ni.

Verse 3
Gualzo pa hi, Mawhna zo pa hi; Ama kha i lungsim uk sak ni;
Amin tawh ei zong i zo ding hi, Tu le a tawntung in.

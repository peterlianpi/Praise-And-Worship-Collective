Title: KA HUN NA KHUT SUNGAH (TDM - 299)
My Time Are In Thy Hands [Late 31:15]

Verse 1
Ka hun Na khut sungah, Topa kong ap khin hi;
Ka lawm te le ka pum ka kha, Topa kong ap khin hi.

Verse 2
Ka hun Na khut sungah, Nang deihna bang’ hi hen;
Khuamial khuavak nopna dahna, Nang hoihsak bangh hi hen.

Verse 3
Ka hun Na khut sungah, Patauh na’ng om ding hiam?
Ka pa’n a mawkna in A ta, Khitui a luang sak diam?

Verse 4
Ka hun Na khut sungah, Kong ap tawntung ding hi;
Ka sih khitciang na taklam ah, Ka om tawntung ding hi.

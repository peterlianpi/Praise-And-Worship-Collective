Title: TOPA AW, KA NUNTAKNA (TDM - 372)
Take My life, and let It Be [Rom 6:13, 16, 19; 12:1]

Verse 1
Topa aw, ka nuntakna, Tang in, siangtho tak zangh in;
Itna tawh Nangma sawlna, Ka khut mang sak, sem sakin,
Ka khut mang sak, sem sak in.

Verse 2
Ka pheiphung hong zangh inla, Nang ading ngalhat sakin;
Ka aw zongh Nang tang’ inla, Ngaihtak Na min phat sakin,
Ngaih tak Na min phat sak in.

Verse 3
Ka kam ka muk hong zangin, Na thupha gen theih nading;
Ka sum ka paai tang’ khin in, Neukha beek hong it kei ning’,
Neukha beek hong it kei ning.

Verse 4
Ka lungsim le ka pilna, Nangma deih bangh in zangh in;
Ka ut na, ka hanciamna, Nangmah in hong uk ta in,
Nangmah in hong uk ta in.

Verse 5
Ka itna teng hong sangin, Na khe maiah hong sung ning’;
Ka pumpi in hong tang’ in, Nangaa bek hi khin den ning’,
Nangaa bek hi khin den ning.

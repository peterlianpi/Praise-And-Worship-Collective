Title: HONG HONPA HONG PAI IN (TDM - 262)
Come,Great Deliverer, Come! [Late 40:17]

Verse 1
Kong sapna ngai in, tu’n hong hehpih in,
Hong Honpa hong pai in!
Ka lung gim zo, Nang kong ngai ta tu in,

Verse 2
Zan khuamial sung ka belh ding ka nei lo,
Hong Honpa hong pai in!
Khat vei Nong et hinna khuavak hi zo,

* Hong Honpa hong pai in!
 

Chorus
Ka va gammang mualtung khuadam lak ah,
Inn pan gamlapi ka pial khin;
Tu’n hong la in, hong ciahpih’n Na inn ah,
Hong Honpa hong pai in!

Verse 3
Lam’ gamdai a ka khe zong gim zo ta,
Hong Honpa hong pai in!
Ka dak leh Na it mai mu zenzen a,

Verse 4
Na nial ngei kei, lung tamkham te’ thumna,
Hong Honpa hong pai in!
Na ngai in lung niamkhiat-a kong thumna,

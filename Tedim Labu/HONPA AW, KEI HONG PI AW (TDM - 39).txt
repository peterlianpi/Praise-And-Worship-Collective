Title: HONPA AW, KEI HONG PI AW (TDM - 39)
Jesus, Saviour, Pilot Me [Late 78:72]

Honpa aw, kei hong pi in, Tuihual hong nawk laitak in; Nuntakna tuihual tungah, A lauhuai suangpi lak ah;
Lam hong hilh in Topa aw, Hong pi aw, kei hong pi aw.

Nu in nau a khim bang in, Tuipi lauhuai dai sak in; Tuihual in Na thu mang hi, 'Dai un' Na cih ciang dai hi;
Huihpi tuipi Ukpa aw, Hong pi aw, kei hong pi aw.

Gungal ka tun ding ciang in, Tuihual hong lauhuai ciang in; Na ang sung hong beel sak aw, Lungmuang tak hong om sak aw;
'Lau kei in! nang hong pi ning' Nong cihna aw za nuam ing.

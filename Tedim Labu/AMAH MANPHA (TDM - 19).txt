Title: AMAH MANPHA (TDM - 19)
He is Precious [I Peter 2:7]

Verse 1
Lungsim siansuah leng Topa biakinnpi hi a, Hong ompihna i thei hi;
A ma min suang in ki khawm leng nuam mahmah a, Siangtho thunget hun nuam hi.

Verse 2
A ma minthanna khuavakpi hong tang suk hi, I lametna a neu ciang;
A ta te' ngah ding itna lian tampi om hi, A mah tawh i ton khop ciang.
 

Chorus
A umte khempeuh ading A mah manpha, A sang te khempeuh ading A mah manpha;
Topa'min a ki khop ciang gen khia ni, A ma thupha, I thungetna hong dawng ding.

Verse 3
Hong paipihna peuh zui leng A mah'n hong paakta, I lungsung nopna dim hi;
I kimkot teng ah tawntung khut in hong huai a, I upna tawh i thei hi.

Verse 4
Kumpi inn sung ah lungnop tawntungna mun ah, Lungdamna khut bet ma teng;
Thu tak thei tel in khangcing ni heh pihna ah, Nopna kicing luah khawm leng.

Title: LEITUNG ADING PASIAN THUBU (TDM - 197)
An Open Bible for the World [Late 119:105]

Verse 1
Leitung ading LaiSiangtho Bu,
Hih pen zuih kim, cihkim hi hen!
Huihkhi laan ciang', tua dial kipeei,
Manpha thupha ki zeelthaang hen!

Verse 2
A tunna peuh kham khuavak hi,
Kiliah zo lo nitang bangh hi;
Meii bangh liah te thengmang ding hi,
Mawhna khempeuh a phel ding hi.
 

Chorus
Pasian kammal (Pasian kammal)
khuavak hong puak (khuavak hong puak)
Leitung tuitung mun khempeuh ah (mun khempeuh);Khuamial sung a vakthap mite, Pasian kiangah hong tunpih hi.

Verse 3
Pa' maitang ah mi teng puak a,
Mawh maisakna vang' kilang hi;
Adam khang pan a mangthangsa,
Hepihna thupha thangsak hi.

Verse 4
Lunggimte a tawldam sak hi,
Khitui luang te a hehnem hi;
Thakiam lungvai te tha guan hi,
Nuntak tawntungna silhsak hi.

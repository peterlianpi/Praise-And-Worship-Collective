Title: BANGDANG HI LO Jesuh' SI HI (TDM - 206)
Nothing But the Blood of Jesus [Heb. 9:22]

Verse 1
Bangin ka mawh sawpsiang thei? Bangdang hi lo, Jesuh' si hi!
Bangin hong damsak kik thei, Bangdang hilo, Jesuh' si hi!

Verse 2
Ka sian nading hih ka mu, Bangdang hilo, Jesuh' si hi!
Ka mawhna kiphel na'ng thu, Bangdang hilo, Jesuh' si hi!
 

Chorus
Manpha hi hong luang si, Vuk bangh hong ngosak si!
Tui dang' ka thei kei hi, Bangdang hilo, Jesuh' si hi!

Verse 3
Mawh maisak bang dang hi lo, Bangdang hilo, Jesuh' si hi!
Apha ka bawl hang hilo, Bangdang hilo, Jesuh' si hi!

Verse 4
Kei lametna, lungnopna, Bangdang hilo, Jesuh' si hi!
Keiin dikna ka ngahna, Bangdang hilo, Jesuh' si hi!

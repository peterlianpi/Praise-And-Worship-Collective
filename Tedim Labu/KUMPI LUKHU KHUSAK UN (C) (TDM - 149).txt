Title: KUMPI LUKHU KHUSAK UN (C) (TDM - 149)
(C)
Let us Crown Him [Late 8:9]

Verse 1
Dawn un, Jesuh' min vangliatna, Vantung mite bok un;
Kumpi lukhu hong la unla, Kumpi lianbel pia un;

Verse 2
Hih leitung a minam kim in, Jesuh lianbel sak un;
Liatna khempeuh Ama aa ci'n, Kumpi lianbel pia un;
 

Chorus
 Beating 3 times to the measure
Kumpi lukhu, Kumpi lukhu,
(Kumpi lukhu khu sak un, Kumpi lukhu khu sak un)
Kumpi lukhu hong tan Topa khu sak un;
Kumpi lukhu, Kumpi lukhu,
Kumpi lukhu khu sak un, Kumpi lukhu khusak un,
Kumpi lukhu khu sak un.
Kumpi lukhu hong tan Topa khu sak un.

Verse 3
Ama misiang honpi te tawh, A khe maiah bok un;
Phat khawm tawntung ni, amau tawh, Kumpi lianbel pia un;

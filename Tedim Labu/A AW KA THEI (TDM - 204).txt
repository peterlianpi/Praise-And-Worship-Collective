Title: A AW KA THEI (TDM - 204)
I Know His Voice [John 10:4]

Verse 1
Honpa ka muh theih loh hangin, Hong hehpih den hi, nisim in;
Amah ka teel tuin ka nei, Topa phat un, A aw ka thei.

Verse 2
Amel ka muh loh hang inah, Huihpi lakpan A aw ka za;
Tuihual kham in dai pah hilhial, Ni hong tang in nuam pah silsial.
 

Chorus

Hunsim in kei (Hunsim in kei) hong hopih hi,(hong hopih hi)

Verse 3
Thuman, thutak hong gen khia a, Muhbat loh tawldamna nopna;
Itna, kilemna thu gen hi, Van innpi thu zongh hong gen hi.

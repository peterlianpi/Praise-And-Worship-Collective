Title: SINGLAMTEH GALKAP (TDM - 278)
A Soldier of the Cross [I Tim 6:12]

Verse 1
Singlamteh galkap kahi hiam? Topa’ nungzui hi hiam?
Ama na’ sep nading inah, Ka maizum thei ding hiam?

Verse 2
Midang te’n nopna ngah dingin, Sih dong a do laitak;
A nuam mahmah lupna tungah, Lum hithiat ding ka hiam?
 

Chorus
Kei tangding (Kei tang ding) a si mipa (a si mipa),
A-minpha tawh do ding Singlamteh ka puak hang inah,
Zawhna lukhu ngah ding.

Verse 3
Galdo ding gal te a om hiam? Tul luan na thuak ding hiam?
Leitung gitlohna in Pasian Kiang hong paipih ding hiam?

Verse 4
Mang ding inah ka ki do na’ng, Hong hangsak in Topa;
Nangma thupha’n hong huh ta in, Gimna ka thuak ciangin.

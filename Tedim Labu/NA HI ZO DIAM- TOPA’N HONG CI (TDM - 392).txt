Title: NA HI ZO DIAM? TOPA’N HONG CI (TDM - 392)
“Are Ye Able,” Said the Master [Luke 22:42]

Verse 1
“Na hi zo diam,” Topa’n hong ci, Amah tawh kikhai khawm ding;
Sih dong Na deih bangh gamta ding, Lungsim ah khentat zo ung.

Verse 2
“Na hi zo diam,” tua thu phawk ding, Guta pa khuadakna mun;
Mawhna khempeuh ki maisak zo, Paradise mun nuam tung zo.
 

Chorus
“Hi zo mah nung,” Topa Nang aa hi ung.
Hong bawlpha in, Nang tawh kibang in;
Lam Nong hilhna vangtang ka muh uh ciang,
Na mel bangin hong it in, na thuman nung.

Verse 3
“Na hi zo diam,” haksat hun ciang, Na kiimkot khua a mial ciang;
Amah na kipiak theih nading, Na lungsim zo masa in.

Verse 4
“Na hi zo diam,” Topa’n hong sam, Nangmah hong sam lailai hi;
Galile gam aa sap bangin, Lungsim hang takin dawng in.

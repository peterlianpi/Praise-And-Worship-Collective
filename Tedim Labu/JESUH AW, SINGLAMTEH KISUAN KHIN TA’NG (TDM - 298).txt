Title: JESUH AW, SINGLAMTEH KISUAN KHIN TA’NG (TDM - 298)
Jesus, I My Cross Have Taken [Matt. 16:24]

Verse 1
Jesuh aw, Nang hong zui dingin, Singlamteh kisuan khin ta’ng;
Nangma aa bek hiden dingin, Simmawh muhdah thuak khin ta’ng.
Ka tup, ka deih ka zonsa teng, A vekpi’n bei khin ta hi;
Ahih hang’ ngaihsun pha kik leng’, Pasian nangawn kei aa hi.

Verse 2
Mi’n hong simmawh, hong nusia in, Honpa nangawn taisan hi;
Mi khempeuh ahoih bangkhem in, Nang tuabang na hi kei hi.
Itna Pasian Nangmah kong zong, Nong maitaina, kei nuamna;
Tual le gal in hong taisan zong, Nong et bek zong’ kei nuamna.

Verse 3
Midang te’ hong bawlsiatna in, Na ang hong tun’ zawsop hi;
Leitung nuntak haksatna in, Vantung nopna hong tun’ hi.
Kei Na hong it lai teng inah, Dahna te siat na’ng hi lo;
Nang kihel loh lungdamna ah, Lungnop kicing om thei lo.

Verse 4
Upna lum tawi, thungen inah, Hehpihna tawh minthang ding;
Ka mai-a vantung nopna ah, Pasian khut in hong kha ding.
Leitung nasep bei baih ding a, Ka khualzinna bei ding hi;
Ka lametna taksuak ding a, Upna ki mu thei ding hi.

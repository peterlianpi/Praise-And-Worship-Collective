Title: EN IN, KONG BIANG-AH KONG DINGLAI (TDM - 231)
Behold Me Standing at the Door [Mang 3:20]

Verse 1
 With felling
En in, kongbiang ah kong ding lai,
Ngai in! nang ka hong zolzol lai;
Mawhnei mi aw, kong lut thei diam?
Na lung sungah kong lut thei diam?

Verse 2
Nang tang ling lukhu ka khu hi,
Lungduai takin kong ngakngak hi;
Mawhnei mi aw, kong lut thei diam?
Lunggim mi aw kong lut thei diam?
 

Chorus
Enin, kongbiang ah kong ding lai,

Verse 3
Nang hong mawk zol kahi kei hi,
Ka thuakna te ngaihsut huai hi;
Nang tang hong si, hi lo ka hiam?
Honghon ding in konglut thei diam?

Verse 4
Van pan nuamna nang kong puak hi,
Itna, nopna mawhphelna hi;
Mawhnei mi aw, kong lut thei diam?
Lunggim mi aw, kong lut thei diam?

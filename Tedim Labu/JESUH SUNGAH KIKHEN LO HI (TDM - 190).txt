Title: JESUH SUNGAH KIKHEN LO HI (TDM - 190)
In Christ There Is No East or West [Johan 14:23]

Verse 1
Jesuh sungah kikhen lo hi, Nisuah, nitum, sak, khang;
Leitung khempeuh kua mi, kua mi, Lawm khat pawlkhat hi hang.

Verse 2
Jesuh sungah lungsim mante'n, Pawl ngah kawikawi ding hi;
Ama nasepna, miteng hen khawm, kham khau zangh bangh hi.

Verse 3
Khut kilen ni up kikhat teng, U le nau bangh hihang;
Bang nam hi leng, Pa' nasem teng, Beh khat phung khat hi hang.

Verse 4
Jesuh sungah, i kimat hi, Sak mi, khang mi, pam mi;
Jesuh lungsim bangh anei, mi khempeuh pumkhat suak hi.

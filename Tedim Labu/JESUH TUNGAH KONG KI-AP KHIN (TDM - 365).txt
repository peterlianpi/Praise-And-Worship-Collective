Title: JESUH TUNGAH KONG KI-AP KHIN (TDM - 365)
All to Jesus I Surrender [Mark. 10:28]

Verse 1
Hong gum Topa keimah inah, Na khempeuh paai in zui ning;
Ka Topa aw, Nang mah inah, Lam hong hilh Pa Na hi ding.

Verse 2
Hong gum Topa keimah inah, Nangma kiang ka hong nai hi;
Kisik a thupha kong ngetna, Ngai in hong gum in, kong ci.
 

Chorus
Na khempeuh paai a, Na khempeuh paai a,
Na khempeuh Topa khut sungah, Kei mah’n hong pia ning.

Verse 3
Hong gum Topa keimah inah, Leitung nopna ka nusia;
KhaSiangtho le Topa Pasian, Hong om in ka lung sungah.

Verse 4
Hong gum Topa keimah inah, Nong gupna la pha sa ning;
Vantung gun gei kong tun’ mateng, Nang hangin ka lungdam ding.

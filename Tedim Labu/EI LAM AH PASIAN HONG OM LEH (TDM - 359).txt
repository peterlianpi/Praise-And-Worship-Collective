Title: EI LAM AH PASIAN HONG OM LEH (TDM - 359)
If God be for Us [Rom. 8:31]

Verse 1
Topa sung nuam un, A hehpihna phat un,
Hong hencip khempeuh phelkhia hi;
Si tawh hong tanzo, launawn peuhmah kei un,
Jesuh ah deih nateng cing hi.

Verse 2
Topa sung hat un, a thahatna suang un,
Nisim thumanin citak ni;
Siatna’n hong nawk ciang, thuman ah dingkip un,
Amah i tha i bukna hi.
 

Chorus
Ei lamah Pasian, ei lamah Pasian,
Ei lam hong om leh kua i lang’ pang zo ding?
Kua? (Kua?) kua? (kua?) kua? (i lang’ pang zo ding?)
Kua i lang’ pang zo, kua pang zo ding?

Verse 3
Topa thu muang un, A ciampel ngeilo hi,
Khris sungah “Amen” hi khinta;
Lei abei zongin, a thu beilo ding hi,
LaiSiangtho in gen khinzo ta.

Verse 4
Topa sung kip un, A ang muanhuai sungah,
Tawntung nuntak kipatna hi;
A thanem pen zongh, a khut sung pan inah,
Kuamah in sut zolo ding hi.

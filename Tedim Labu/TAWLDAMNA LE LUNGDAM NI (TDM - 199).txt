Title: TAWLDAMNA LE LUNGDAM NI (TDM - 199)
O Day of Rest and Gladness
(Tune: Mendness)

Verse 1
Tawldamna le lungdam ni, Khuavakna, nuamna ni;
Lungkhamna dahna damsak, Avaak a hoihpen ni;
Tanglai a kipan tuni, Alian aneu kituak;
'Siangtho, siangtho, siangtho' ci'n, Vanglian Pasian phatni.

Verse 2
Na khempeuh a piancil ni, Khuavak hen acih ni;
Eite hong honkhia Jesuh, Sihna pan' thawhkik ni;
Sihna galzo i Topa'n, KhaSiangtho, hong puak ni;
Tua hi a, hih Nipi ni, Thupha nam thum ngah ni.

Verse 3
Tuni in minam kim tung', Vantung khomun hong tung';
Misiangtho kikhopna ah, Lungdamna thu ki hilh;
A gim a tawlte kisil, Tawldamna lui sungah;
Lungdamna thupha a vaang, Misiangte maiah taang.

Verse 4
Vantung gam misiang te bangh, Hehpihna a banban;
Tawldam ni, Nipi ni pan', A thuapthuap i ngah hi;
Tuni'n phatna kiza hi, Pawlpi mite kam pan';
Phat un thum gawm a siangtho Pa, Ta le KhaSiangtho.

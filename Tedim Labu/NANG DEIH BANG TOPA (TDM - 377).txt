Title: NANG DEIH BANG TOPA (TDM - 377)
Have Thine Own Way, Lord [Luke 22:42]

Verse 1
Nang deih bangh Topa, Nang deih bangbang!
Nangmah beelseek pa, Kei tungman bangh.
Nangma deih bangin, Kei hong bawl in;
Tang lo, mang dingin, Nang kong ngak hi.

Verse 2
Nang deih bangh Topa, Nang deih bangbang!
Tuni in Topa, Hong sit in Nang!
Vuk sang’ ngozaw in, Tu’n hong sawp in;
Lungsim neem takin, Nang mai kong kun.

Verse 3
Nang deih bangh Topa, Nang deih bangbang!
Ka liam ka gimna, Hong huh in, Nang!
Vangliatna khempeuh, Nangmah’n Na nei;
Hong damsak in kei, Hong Honpa aw!

Verse 4
Nang deih bangh Topa, Nang deih bangbang!
Keima nuntakna, Hong uk in, Nang!
Khris keima sung, Mi’n muhna ding;
Hong guan in kei sung, Na KhaSiangtho!

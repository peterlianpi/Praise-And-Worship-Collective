Title: NOEL MASA, VANTUNGMI SAK (TDM - 82)
The First Noel, the Angels Did Say [Matt. 2:9-11]

Verse 1
Noel masa vantungmi te sak, Tuucing te pawlkhat tuu cin laitak hi;
Gampalak ah tuu vil laitak, Phalbi khuadam lai zan khuamial laitak.

Verse 2
A khuadak ciang nisuahna lamah, Gamlapi pan, aksi taang khat kilak;
Leitung teng ah nakpi taang hi, Sun le zan ci lo in taang tawntung hi.
 

Chorus
Noel, Noel, Noel, Noel, Itazila Kumpipa suak.

Verse 3
Tua aksipi taang zui kawm inah, Gamlapi pan mipil thum te inah;
Kumpi zong ding hong pai uh hi, Aksi paina khempeuh hong zui uh hi.

Verse 4
Tua aksi'n nitumna lam naih hi, Bethlehem khuapi tung lam hong naih hi;
Tualai ah aksipi hong khawl, Jesuh a kisialna tung ah hong khawl.

Verse 5
Mipil thumte zong buk sung lut a, Zahtakna lianpi tawh khukdin uh hi;
Kham, paaknamtui, paaknamtui kha, Ama kiangah pia in a bia uh hi.

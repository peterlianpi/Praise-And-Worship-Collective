Title: KALSUAN SIMIN (TDM - 487)
Each Step I Take [Ps. 37: 23]

Verse 1
Kalsuan simin Honpa ka maiah pai hi,
A itna khut tawh hong’ makaih a;
Husan simin Topa Nangmah kong’ bia hi,
Nangmah tawh tonkhawm leng nuam ka sa.

Verse 2
Ka upna tha Khat veivei a hong kiam ciang’,
Ka etna khempeuh lungkiatna hi;
Kihei kik in kei hong’ Gumpa ka et ciang’,
Hong ompih manin ka thahat hi.
 

Chorus
Kalsuan simin A hong pi ding ka thei a,
A sang zaw ah hong paipih tawntung hi;
Khatvei ni ciang lam tawpna ka tung ding a,
Kalsuan simin Inn ka nai semsem hi.

Verse 3
Bangbang hong piang taleh Pasian ka muang hi,
Nuntakna Ama’ sungah om hi;
Amah lampi a hong’ honsak thei Pa hi,
Khapsa gamnuam ah hong tun ding hi.

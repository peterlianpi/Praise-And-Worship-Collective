Title: GUPKHIAT (TDM - 113)
Redeemed [Late 81]

Verse 1
Gupkhiatna thu thangsak nuam sing! Tuuno si tawh gupkhiat ka hih;
A itna tawh gupkhiat ka hi, A tawntung Ama ta ka hi.

Verse 2
Gupkhiat ka hi, Jesuh'n hong gum, Kalawpna gen zo kam om kei;
Ama ompihna khuavak pha, Keisung hong tang den ding ka thei.

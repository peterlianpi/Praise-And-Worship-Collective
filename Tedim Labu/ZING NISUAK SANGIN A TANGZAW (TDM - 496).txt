Title: ZING NISUAK SANGIN A TANGZAW (TDM - 496)
Fairer than the morning [Solomon 5:16]

Verse 1
Zingni suak sangin a tang zaw, Ka Honpa le Kumpi hi;
Na hoihna le na thupina, La in ka sasa nuam hi.

Verse 2
Vangliatna puantawh kizem in, Minthanna lukhu khu hi;
Thupha vangliatna le hatna, A tawntung in na nei hi.
 

Chorus
Zing nisuak sangin a tang zaw,
Sun nitang sang’ tang zaw Nang’ nahi;
Vangliatna (Vangliatna) tawh dim na maiah,
Khuamial le meiilum theng hi.

Verse 3
Na migitna lian mahmah a, Na hehpihna kicing hi;
Na ukna it na tawh dim a, Kei ading hong bawlsak hi.

Verse 4
Ngun meivak khaute kitat in, Ka nuntakna bei ciangin;
Kumtul kum then Topa maiah, Phatna la ka sa ding hi.

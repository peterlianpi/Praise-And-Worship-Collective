Title: JESUH IN HONG SAM (TDM - 237)
Jesus is Calling [Matt. 11:28]

Verse 1
Jesuh in itna tawh inn ah hong sam:
Tuni'n hong sam, tuni'n hong sam;
Ni bangh tang hong itna pan a pua lam,
Bang ding ki daithah na hiam?

Verse 2
Jesuh'n hong pia nuam a gim te tawldam:
Tuni'n hong sam, tuni'n hong sam;
Na van va puak in-la, ngah in lungdam,
Nang a hong nial kei ding hi.
 

Chorus
Tuni'n hong sam, (Tuni'n hong sam)
Tuni'n hong sam; (Tuni'n hong sam)
Jesuh in hong sam, (Jesuh'n hong it manin tuni'n hong sam)
Hong it manin tuni'n hong sam.

Verse 3
Jesuh'n hong ngak hi, pai in tu laitak:
Tuni'n hong ngak, tuni'n hong ngak;
Na mawhna mah tawh va kun in niamtak,
Pai in, ki ziakai sak ke'n.

Verse 4
Jesuh'n in hong zol hi, A aw ngai in:
Tuni'n za in, tuni'n za in.
Nuamna ngah ding hi, Amah um te in,
Tho in pai pah vingveng in.

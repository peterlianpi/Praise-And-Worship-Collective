Title: DANIEL PAWLTE (TDM - 280)
Dare to Be Daniel [Dan. 1:8]

Verse 1
Hanciamna pha a nei mi, Topa’ thu mang te;
Pahtawi un, a muanhuai mi, Phat un, Daniel pawlte.

Verse 2
Daniel pawl a pawmsa te, Pasian’ mi tampi;
Hang tak a ding ngam lo te, Mangthang khin zo uh hi.
 

Chorus
Daniel bangh hi ngam in, Hang takin ding ni;
Hanciamna kip nei ngam in, Hang tak in gen ni.

Verse 3
Mi thahat kihtak huai te, Tampi a om hang’;
Daniel pawl a langpang te, A vekpi un mangthang.

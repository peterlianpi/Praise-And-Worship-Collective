Title: UM PAH LEL’ VAW! (TDM - 481)
Only Believe [Lk. 12:32]

Verse 1
Lau ken, tuuhon te, sihna pan hinna ah,
Nangma ading in, Topa pai zo tah;
Vangliatna khempeuh, leitung le vantung ah,
Amah kipia zo nangma ading inah.

Verse 2
Lau ken, tuuhon te, Topa mapai zo ta,
Nang hong cing’ Topa’n, na lam nawk zo ta;
Tuikha om khempeuh, Aman khum sak ding hi,
Tuikha khempeuh te Aman dawn khin zo hi.
 

Chorus
Um pah lel vaw! Um pah lel vaw!
Na khempeuh piang thei hi, Um pah lel vaw!
Na khempeuh piang thei hi, Um pah lel vaw!

Verse 3
Lau ken, tuuhon te, Bangbang piang taseleh,
Kong kikhak khempeuh, Topa’n hong zo veh!
Hong don khial ngeilo, hong ki nusia ngeilo,
Khuamial, khuavak ah hong ompih khial ngeilo.

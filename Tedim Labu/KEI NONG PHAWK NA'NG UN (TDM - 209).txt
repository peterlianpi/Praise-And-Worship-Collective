Title: KEI NONG PHAWK NA'NG UN (TDM - 209)
In Re membrance of Me [I Korin 11:24]

Verse 1
Nong hehpihna hangin kong phat uh hi, Topa,
Tua in ko te tudong hong keem hi;
Na hoihna, nong itna ka zahtak uh a,
Nangma tungah hong kiap in kong kuun uh hi.

Verse 2
Nuntak khomun ne ding ka kilawm kei uh hi,
A them tawm' ding zongh kilawm kei ung;
A hi zongin hong hehpih in nong pia a,
Na hong itna hang bek in a ne thei hi ung.
 

Chorus
Topa aw, tuni'n nong vaikhak sa mah bangin,
Na sabuai ah an kong um uh hi;
'Kei nong phawk na'ng un ne un' nong cih bangh in,
Nang, phawkna in ka nei uh hi.

Verse 3
Upna tawh ko te in khomun ka ne uh hi,
Lungsim, pumpi hong thahat sak hen;
Nangma sihna thu hong gengen ahi hi,
Gen zawh loh na minthanna sung hong tun tahen.

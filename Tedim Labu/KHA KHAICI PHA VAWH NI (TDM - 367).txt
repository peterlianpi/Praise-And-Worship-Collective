Title: KHA KHAICI PHA VAWH NI (TDM - 367)
Sowing to the Spirit [2 Kor. 9:7]

Verse 1
Kha khaici pha vawh ni, Nisim in vawh ni,
Lam dung tengah vawh ni, Tua itna khaici;
Kha khaici pha vawh ni, Muang den ni Pasian,
Nasepna nuamsa in, Sang ding thaman lian.

Verse 2
Kha khaici pha vawh ni, Thu ngen in kuan ni,
I vawh khaici pha te, Hong pokhia ding hi;
Upna kip tawh vawh ni, Van guah hong zu ding,
I lo zau khempeuh teng, Kham khai tawh dim ding.
 

Chorus
Kha khaici pha vawh ni, Ni aniam hang zongh;
Lungkia ke’n cimtak ke’n, Nasep zawh siang dong.

Verse 3
Kha khaici pha vawh ni, Upna aneu hang,
Thanem taleng vawh ni, Khitui a luan hang;
Nasep i zawh siang dong, Hun ngah bang sem ding,
Anlak hun hong tun dong, Lungdam tawntung ding.

Title: ZING NI SUAK SENSAN TA (TDM - 22)
When Morning Gilds the Sky [Matt. 21:9]

Verse 1
Zingni suak sensan ta, Ka lungsim khanglo ta;
Jesuh Khris phat huai! Nasem ding, thungen ding,
Jesuh kiang va zuan ning; Jesuh Khris phat huai!

Verse 2
Lungsung dahna om hiam, Hih ah ka dahna kiam;
Jesuh Khris phat huai! Leitung nuamna bei leh,
Hehnepna om veve; Jesuh Khris phat huai!

Verse 3
Vantung lungdamna ah, La a ngaih pen hihmah;
Jesuh Khris phat huai! Khuamial vaang te'n kihta,
A zak uh ciang hih la; Jesuh Khris phat huai!

Verse 4
Leitung ka hin laiteng, Sa ning ka sih mateng;
Jesuh Khris phat huai! Sih ciang vantung tung leng,
Ka la hihmah hi ding; Jesuh Khris phat huai!

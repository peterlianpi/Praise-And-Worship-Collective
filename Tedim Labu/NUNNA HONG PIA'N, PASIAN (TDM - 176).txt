Title: NUNNA HONG PIA'N, PASIAN (TDM - 176)
Breathe On Me, Breath of God [Job. 33:4]

Verse 1
Nunna hong pia'n Pasian, Nang bawl bangh bawl dingin;
Nadeih bangh ka deih theih nading, Hinna thak hong guan in.

Verse 2
Nunna hong pia'n Pasian, Ka lungsim sian' dong in;
Nang tawh sin khat lung khat suah dong, Bawlkhawm, thuak khawm dingin.

Verse 3
Nunna hong pia'n Pasian, Nang mi kahih khit dong;
Leitungna khat ahi kei sung, Nathu mei bangh kuan dong'.

Verse 4
Nunna hong pia'n Pasian, Si ngei nawnlo dingin;
Hin tawntungna gamnuam sungah, Nang tawh omkhawm dingin.

Title: JESUH IN HONG KEM HIAM? (TDM - 349)
Does Jesus Care? [1Peter 5:7]

Verse 1
Ka lampi sau in a gimhuai a,
Vangik pua in ki kemkhial in;
Ka lampi sau in, lungsim a nat ciang,
Jesuh in kei a hong kem hiam?

Verse 2
Sun khuavak in a hong mial ciangin,
Kei kiangah a hong nai om hiam?
Ka lampi mial in, lauhuai a tam ciang,
Jesuh in kei a hong kem hiam?
 

Chorus
A hong kem hi, Ke’n ka thei hi,
Ka dahna hong thuakpih hi,
Sun in om nuamlo in, zan lauhuai laitak,
Hong gumpa’n hong kem. (hong kem)

Verse 3
Haksa ziaet ka thuak laitakin,
Zan tawntung khitui luanna tawh;
Hong hon ding omlo ka dah laitak in,
Jesuh in kei a hong kem hiam?

Verse 4
I it te tawh mangpha kipia in,
Lungkham lutangnat a bei ciang;
Topa in ei bangci in hong mu diam,
Jesuh in kei a hong kem hiam?

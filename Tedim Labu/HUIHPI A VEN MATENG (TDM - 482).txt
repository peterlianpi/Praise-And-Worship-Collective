Title: HUIHPI A VEN MATENG (TDM - 482)
Till the Storm Passes By [Isa. 25:4]

Verse 1
Zankim khuamial laitak in lau in ka bu zel a,
Ka tungah huihpi nung’ in, ka belh ding lah thei keng’;
Hih vanginna phual panin, hong ngai in, kong’ thumna,
“Hong kem’ in huihpi a ven mateng.”

Verse 2
Hahkat hang a mawknapi, dahna bei theilo pi,
Lametna zongh om beek kei ci’n Satan’ hong khem hi;
A’ih hang Nong ompih thei ing zingciang tho to inah,
“Ka pai ding huihpi om lohna ah.”
 

Chorus
Huihpi te veng mang’ khin a, vanging te a bei siang dong’,
van a meii te bei mang’ a khua phat dong’;
Topa aw, hong’ len’ in na khutkuak sung hong’ koih in,
Hong’ kem’ in huihpi a ven mateng.

Verse 3
Zan saupi bei khin ta a huihpi a ven ciangin,
Na maiah ka ding ding hi, Tua a tang gun gei ah;
Tua gam ah huihpi omlo hong teeng ning Topa aw,
“Huihpi a ven khit tak ciangin maw.”

Title: HONG HEHPIHNA THUPHA (TDM - 117)
The Gospel of Thy Grace [Johan 3:16]

Verse 1
Hong hehpihna thupha'n, Lungsim khauh zo thei hi;
Leitung a it man' a, A Tapa hong pia hi.

Verse 2
Gamlak ah gul kikhai, A noptuam mahbangin;
Topa Jesuh kikhai, Nuntak tawntung na'ng hi.
 

Chorus
Kuamah peuhmah in thuum leh Hin tawntungna pha ngah ding hi;
Hin tawntungna pha ngah ding hi.

Verse 3
Mawhnei te sih kik ding, Thukhenna i thei hi;
Athu bangh zui kei leng, Kisia takpi ding hi.

Verse 4
Leitung mawhsak dingin, Jesuh hong pai lo hi;
A minthan mahbangin, Leitung hong gum ding hi.

Verse 5
A um lo te hehpih, Up na'ng lungsim guan hi;
Thuphabel hong tun' ve'n, Hong um pha sak tahen.

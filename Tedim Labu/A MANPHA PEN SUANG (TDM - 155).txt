Title: A MANPHA PEN SUANG (TDM - 155)
The Pearl of Greatest Price [Matt. 13:45, 46]

Verse 1
A manpha pen suang' ke'n mu ing, Lungdam in la ka sa;
Jesuh kei aa ci'n lasa ning, Khris la in sa ning.

Verse 2
Jesuh Siampi ka Kumpi hi, Khuavak Kamsangpa hi;
Tokhom maiah Kamsanglian pa, Ka van Kumpipa hi.
 

Chorus
A manpha pen suang ke'n mu ing, Lungdam in la ka sa;
Jesuh keia' ci'n la sa ning! Khris la in sa ning'.

Verse 3
Amah topa te' Topa hi, kumpi te' Kumpi hi;
Amah in thumanna Ni hi, A kha nuai cidamna.

Verse 4
Kilem nading kei tang' hong si, Kei tang' A si hong' bua;
A lamdang kei hong gum Pa hi, Amah mahin hong gum.

Verse 5
Khris ah deihna teng cing a, Ka lungmuanna ahi;
Leitung hinna Amah hi a, Vantung hinna zongh hi.

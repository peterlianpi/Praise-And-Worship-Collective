Title: LA THAK (TDM - 450)
The New Song [Mang. 14:3]

Verse 1
Jesuh maiah mi tampi kikhawm uh a,
Amin phatna la sa in, gawsem tum uh hi:

Verse 2
Mawhna nei ngei te tua mihon maitang ah,
Puanpak silh in, sa uh hi, Amin phatna la:
 

Chorus
Ei hong it a, i mawhna hong phelsak Topa,
Ama Min thang tawntung ta hen! Amen, Amen.

Verse 3
A gal te, siampi le kumpi suaksak hi,
A si pha tawh lei in, la thak a sa sak hi:

Verse 4
Hong tatkhiatna om kha zenzen lo hi leh,
Mawhnei mi te bangzah in, i cimawh ding hiam:

Verse 5
Amah phatna aw nakpi in ging sak ni,
Mi dang te’n zongh um a la thak a sak na’ng hi:

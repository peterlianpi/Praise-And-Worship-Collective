Title: KA HONPA' HONG ITNA (TDM - 17)
My Saviour's Love [Luka 22:24]

Verse 1
Nazareth mi Jesuh mai ah, Lamdang sa in ka ding hi; Mawhnei nin bang ki nawlkhin sa, Kei nangawn a hong it hi.

Verse 2
Huan sungah thu hong nget sak in, 'Nang deih bang hi zaw hen ci''; A ma ading in, luang lo in, Kei hang khuaul, si luang hi.
 

Chorus
Lam- (Aw lam-) dang lua e! Pian- (Aw pian-) dang lua e! Ka sak ding la; Lam- (Aw lam-) dang lua e! Pian- (Aw pian-) dang lua e! Ka Honpa'n kei hong itna.

Verse 3
Van mi te'n hehpihhuai sa in, Khuavak gam pan en uh hi; Dahna hong thuaksak tua zan in, Hehnem ding hong pai suk hi.

Verse 4
Ka mawh ka dahna teng la in, A ma tung ah ki suan hi; Kalvary ah tua van pua in, A mah bek in sih lawh hi.

Verse 5
Tatsa te tawh minthang ding a, A ma maitang ka mu ding; Hong itna la ka sa ding a, Khang tawntung ka lungdam ding.

Title: SI A KHUT GUAK MASUAN DING MAW? (TDM - 271)
Must I Go-and Empty-Handed? [Dan. 12:3]

Verse 1
Si a khut guak masuan ding maw, Topa mu ding hih bangin;
A na ni khat beek zong sem lo, Puak ding bangmah keng lo in.

Verse 2
Sih ding kihta ka hi kei hi, Topa in hong hon zo tah;
Ahih hang in guak pai ding pen, Ngaihsut ciang sinlung kha lah.
 

Chorus
Si ding a khut guak masuan in, Hong Honpa’ mai mu ding maw?
Amah dawn nading mi khat zongh, Tonpih lo guak pai ding maw?

Verse 3
Mawhna sung a mawkbei kum teng, Khatvei sam kik thei leng maw;
A vekpi in Topa pia in, A deihbang mang nuam sing maw.

Verse 4
Hahkat un, ding un, misiang te, Sunlai na citak in sem;
Sihna zan in hong phak ma in, Mi ngah na’ng sep theih lai sem.

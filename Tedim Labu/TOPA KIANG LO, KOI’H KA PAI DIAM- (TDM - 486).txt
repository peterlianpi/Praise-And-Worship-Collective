Title: TOPA KIANG LO, KOI’H KA PAI DIAM? (TDM - 486)
Where Could I Go? [Jn. 6:68]

Verse 1
Mawhna dim’ hih lei ah ka nuntakna,
Ka Kha buk na’ng mun zong dingin;
Ziaetna lianpi ke’n ka sunsiah hi,
Topa kiang lo, koi’h ka pai diam?

Verse 2
I it lawmte khempeuhte ke’n it ing,
I lungsung ah kingilh thei lo;
Ka kha adingin Kha an kisam ing,
Topa kiang lo, koi’h ka pai diam?
 

Chorus
Koi’h ka pai diam? Oh! Koiah ka pai diam?
Ka kha buk na’ng mun zong dingin;
Ni tawpni ciang’ hong’ gum’ ding’ kisam ing,
Topa kiang lo, koi’h ka pai diam?

Verse 3
Nun in nuam e hiah i it lawmte tawh,
Pasian hong’ piak hehnepna hi;
Sihna daitui ka tal ah hong kai ciang’, 
Topa kiang lo, koi’h ka pai diam?
